HOSTILITY

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOSTILITY, the action of an enemy, or a state of warfare. The word is Latin, hostilitas, formed of the primitive hostis, which signifies "enemy;" and which anciently signified "stranger," hostes.
