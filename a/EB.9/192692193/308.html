HOMAGE

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOMAGE, in Law, is the ſubmiſſion, loyalty, and ſervice, which a tenant promiſed to his lord when he was firſt admitted to the land which he held of the lord in fee: alſo that owing to a king, or to any ſuperior.
