HESPERIA

HESPERIA, an ancient name of Italy; so called by the Greeks from its western situation. Hesperia was also an appellation of Spain; but with the epithet ultima (Horace), to distinguish it from Italy, which is called Hesperia magna (Virgil), from its extent of empire.
