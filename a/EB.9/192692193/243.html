HOARSENESS

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOARSENESS, in Medicine, a diminution of the voice, commonly attended with a preternatural asperity and roughness thereof. The parts affected are the aspera arteria and larynx. For its causes and cure, see MEDICINE Index.
