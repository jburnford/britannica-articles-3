HOBGOBLIN

article · 320 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOBGOBLIN, is a name vulgarly applied to fairies or apparitions. Skinner calls the word robogoblin, and derives it from Robin Goodfellow, Hob being the nick-name of Robin: but Wallis and Junius, with greater probability, derive it from hoppoblin, empusae, because they are supposed to hop without moving both their feet.
