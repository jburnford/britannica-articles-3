HETERODOX

article · 313 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HETERODOX, in Polemical Theology, something that is contrary to the faith or doctrine established in the true church. The word is formed of the Greek ἱερόδοξος; a compound of ἱερός, "alter," and δοξός, "opinion."

Thus, we say a heterodox opinion, a heterodox divine, &c. The word stands in opposition to orthodox.
