HOSPODAR

article · 347 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HOSPODAR, a title borne by the princes of Walachia and Moldavia, who receive the investiture of their principalities from the grand signior. He gives them a vest and standard; they are under his protection, and obliged to serve him, and he even sometimes deposes them; but in other respects they are absolute sovereigns within their own dominions.
