HUMANITIES

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HUMANITIES, in the plural, signify grammar, rhetoric, and poetry, known by the name of literæ humaniores; for teaching of which, there are professors in the universities of Scotland, called humanists.
