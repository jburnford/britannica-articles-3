HONOURABLE

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HONOURABLE, a title conferred on the younger sons of earls, the sons of viscounts and barons; as also on such persons as have the king's commission, and upon those who enjoy places of trust and honour.
