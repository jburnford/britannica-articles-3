HORNSEY, a town in Yorkshire, 188 miles from London. It is almost surrounded by a small arm of the sea; and the church having a high steeple, is a noted sea-mark. Not many years ago there was a street here called Hornsey-beck, which was washed away by the sea, except a house or two. E. Long. o. 6. N. Lat. 54. 0.
