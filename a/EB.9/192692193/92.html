HESPERI CORNU

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HESPERI CORNU, called the Great Bay by the author of Hanno's Periplus; but most interpreters, following Mela, understand a promontory; some Cape Verd, others Palmas Cape: Vossius takes it to be the former, since Hanno did not proceed so far as the latter cape.
