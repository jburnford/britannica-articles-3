HERISSON

HERISSON, in Fortification, a beam armed with a great number of iron spikes with their points outwards, and supported by a pivot on which it turns. These serve as a barrier to block up any passage, and are frequently placed before the gates, and more especially the wicket-doors, of a town or fortress, to secure those

Heritable those passages which must of necessity be often opened and shut.
