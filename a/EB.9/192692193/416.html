HORROX of a Vacuum

sub_entry · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HORROX of a Vacuum, was an imaginary principle among the ancient philosophers, to which they ascribed the ascent of water in pumps, and other similar phenomena, which are now known to be occasioned by the weight of the air.
