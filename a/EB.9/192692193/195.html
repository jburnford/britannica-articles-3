HINNOH, or the Valley of HINNOH, in Ancient Geography, a place that lay to the south of Jerusalem.

It was also called the valley of Tophet, and was remarkable for the cruel and barbarous worship of the god Moloch, where parents made their children pass through the fire in honour of that idol.
