HUSBAND-Land

sub_entry · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HUSBAND-Land, a term used in Scotland for a portion of land containing six acres of fock and scythe land; that is, of land that may be tilled with a plough, and mowen with a scythe.
