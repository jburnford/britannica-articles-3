HERSILLON

Cross-references: HERSE

HERSILLON, in the military art, a sort of plank or beam, ten or twelve feet long, whose two sides are driven full of spikes or nails, to incommodate the march of the infantry or cavalry. The word is a diminutive of herse; the hersillon doing the office of a little herse. See HERSE.
