HODEGOS

article · 432 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HODEGOS, a term purely Greek, ὁδῆς, signifying guide. The word is chiefly used as the title of a book composed by Anatolius the Senate, towards the close of the fifth century; being a method of disputing against the heretics, particularly the Acepalli.
