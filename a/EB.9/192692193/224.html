HIRCH-HORN

HIRCH-HORN, a town of Germany, in the circle of the lower Rhine, with a strong castle. It is seated on the side of a hill on the river Neckar, and belongs to the elector Palatine. E. Long. 9. o. N. Lat. 49. 28.
