HUSUM

HUSUM, a town of Denmark, in the duchy of Sleswick, and capital of a bailiwick of the same name, with a strong citadel, and a very handsome church. It is seated near the river Ow, on the German sea, and is subject to the dukes of Holstein-Gottorp. E. Long. 9. 4. N. Lat. 54. 5.
