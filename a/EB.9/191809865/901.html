BARBARA

article · 370 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARBARA, among Logicians, the first mode of the first figure of syllogisms. A syllogism in barbara is one whereof all the propositions are universal and affirmative; the middle term being the subject of the first proposition, and attribute in the second.

Examp. BAR. Every wicked man is miserable;

BA. All tyrants are wicked men;

RA. Therefore all tyrants are miserable.
