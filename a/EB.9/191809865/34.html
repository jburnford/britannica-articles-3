ASTERIUS, or ASTURIUS, a Roman consul, in
449. We have under his name, "A Conference on the
Old and New Testament," in Latin verse: in
which each strophe contains, in the first verse, an hi-
storical fact in the Old Testament; and in the second,
an application of that fact to some point in the New.
