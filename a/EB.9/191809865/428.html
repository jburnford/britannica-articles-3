AUTOMATE

AUTOMATE, called also Hiera, one of the Cyclades, an island to the north of Crete (Pisay), said to have emerged out of the sea, between the islands Thera and Therafia, in the fifth year of the emperor Claudius; in extent 30 stadia, (Orofius).
