BABYLONIAN, BABYLONIUS

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BABYLONIAN, BABYLONIUS, is used in some ancient writers for an astrologer, or any thing related to astrology. Hence Babylonia cura, the art of calling nativities; and numeri Babylonii, the computation of astrologers.
