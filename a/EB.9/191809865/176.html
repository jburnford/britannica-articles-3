ATTIC, any thing relating to Attica; or to the city of Athens: thus Attic salt, in philology, is a delicate poignant sort of wit and humour peculiar to the Athenian writers; Attic witness, a witness incapable of corruption, &c.

Attic Order. See ARCHITECTURE.

Attic Bases, a peculiar kind of base used by the ancient architects in the Ionic order; and by Palladio, and some others, in the Doric.

Attic Story, in Architecture; a story in the upper part of a house, where the windows are usually square.
