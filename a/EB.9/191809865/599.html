BADIANE

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BADIANE, or BANDIAN, the feed of a tree which grows in China, and smells like anife-feed. The Chinese, and the Dutch in imitation of them, fometimef ufe the badiane to give their tea an aromatic tafte.
