AUXERRE

AUXERRE, an ancient town of France in the department of Yonne, and capital of the Auxerrois, and lately a bishop's see. The episcopal palace was one of the finest in France, and the churches were also very beautiful. This town is advantageously situated for trade with Paris, on the river Yonne. E. Long. 3. 35. N. Lat. 47. 54.
