BAGLANA

article · 539 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAGLANA, or BUOLANA, a province of the kingdom of Dekkan in the Mogul's empire. It is bounded on the north and east by Guzzarat and Ballagat; and on the south and west by that part of Visapour called Konban, belonging to the Mahrattas. It ends in a point at the sea coast between Daman and Balora, and is the least province in the kingdom. The Portuguese territories begin in this province at the port Daman, 21 leagues south of Surat; and run along the coast by Bassain, Bombay, and Chawl, to Dabul, almost 50 leagues to the north of Goa.
