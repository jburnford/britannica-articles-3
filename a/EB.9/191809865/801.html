BANC, or BENCA

sub_entry · 166 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BANC, or BENCA, in Law, denotes a tribunal, or

judgment-seat; hence king's bane is the same with the court of king's bench, and common bane with that of common pleas.
