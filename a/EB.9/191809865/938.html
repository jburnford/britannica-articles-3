BARCELOS, a town of Portugal, with the title of a duchy. It is seated on the river Cavado, over which there is a handsome bridge. W. Long. 7. 0. N. Lat. 41. 20.

BARCINO in Ancient Geography, a town of the Terraconensis in Spain, and capital of the Laetani. Now BARCELONA. See that article.
