AVAIL OF MARRIAGE

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AVAIL OF MARRIAGE, in Scots Law, that casualty in wardholding, by which the superior was entitled to a certain sum from his vassal, upon his attaining the age of puberty, as the value or avail of his tocher.
