AURACH

AURACH, a town of Germany with a good castle, in the south part of Suabia, in the duchy of Wirtemberg. It is the usual residence of the youngest sons of the house of Wirtemberg, and is seated at the foot of a mountain on the rivulet Ernst. E. Long. 9. 20. N. Lat. 48. 25.
