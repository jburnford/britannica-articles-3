ASTA Regia

ASTA Regia, a town of Beticia, (Pliny); situated at the mouth of the Betis which was choked up with mud, to the north of Cadiz: 16 miles distant from the port of Cadiz, (Antonine). Its ruins show its former greatness. Its name is Phœnician, denoting a frith or arm of the sea, on which it stood. It is said to be the same with XERA; which see.
