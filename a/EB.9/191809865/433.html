AUTRICUM, the capital of the Carnutes, a people of Gallia Celtica; afterwards called Carnotena, Carnotenus, and Crodat Carnotenum: Now Charres, in the Orleanois on the Eure. E. Long. 1. 32. N. Lat. 48. 47.
