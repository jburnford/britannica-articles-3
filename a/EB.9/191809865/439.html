AUVERNAS

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUVERNAS, a very deep-coloured heady wine, made of black raisins so called, which comes from Orleans. It is not fit to drink before it is above a year old; but if kept two or three years, it becomes excellent.
