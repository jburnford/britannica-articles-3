ASTAROTH, in Ancient Geography, the royal residence of Og king of Bashan; whether the same with Astaroth Carnaim, is matter of doubt: if one and the same, it follows from Eusebius's account, that it lay in Bashan, and to the east of Jordan, because in the confines of Arabia.
