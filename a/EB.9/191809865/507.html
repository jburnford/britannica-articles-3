AZYMOUS

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AZYMOUS, something unfermented, or made without leaven; as unleavened bread. Sea biscuit is of this kind; and therefore, according to Galen, less wholesome than bread that has been fermented.
