BALNEARIUS SERVI

article · 330 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALNEARIUS SERVI, in antiquity, servants or attendants belonging to the baths. Some were appointed to heat them, called fornicatores; others were denominated capararii, who kept the clothes of those that went into them; others allipiae, whose care it was to pull off the hair; others unctuarii, who anointed and perfumed the body.
