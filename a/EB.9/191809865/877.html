BAR

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BAR, in Hydrography, denotes a bank of sand, or other matter, whereby the mouth of a river is in a manner choked up.

The term bar is also used for a strong beam wherever the entrance of a harbour is secured: this is more commonly called boom.
