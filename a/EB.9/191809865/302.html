AUGUSTA Ausciorum

sub_entry · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUGUSTA Ausciorum, a town of Aquitania, so named out of compliment to Augustus, being originally called Climberum, which name it afterwards resumed. In the middle age, it took the name of the people, Ausci; and is now called Auch, the capital of Gascogne.
