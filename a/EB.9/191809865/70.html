ATALANTA

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATALANTA, an island in the Euripus of Euboea, near the Locri Opuntii, said to have been originally a city of the Locri, but torn from the continent in the

time of the earthquake, and during an eruption of Mount Ætna. This happened in the fourth year of the 93d Olympiad, in the reign of Artaxerxes Mnemon.
