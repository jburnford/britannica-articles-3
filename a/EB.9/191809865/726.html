BALIVO AMOVENDO

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALIVO AMOVENDO, in Law, was a writ for removing a bailiff from his office, for want of having sufficient land in this bailiwick to answer the king and his people, according to the statute of Westminster, 2 reg. Orig. 78.
