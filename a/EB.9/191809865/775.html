BALTIMORE

BALTIMORE, a town of Ireland in the county of Corke, and province of Munster, with the title of a barony. It is seated on a headland which runs into the sea, five miles north-east of Cape Clear. W. Long. 9. 10. N. Lat. 51. 15.
