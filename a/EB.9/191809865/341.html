AUKLAND

AUKLAND, BISHOP'S, a town in the bishopric of Durham in England, situated on the river Were. It is a sanctuary for debtors; and here the bishop has a princely palace and a noble park. W. Long. 0. 35. N. Lat. 54. 44.
