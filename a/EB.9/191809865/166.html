ATTAINT

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATTAINT, among Farriers, a knock or hurt in a horse's leg, proceeding either from a blow with another horse's foot, or from an over-reach in frosty weather, when a horse, being rough shod, or having shoes with long caulkers, strikes his hinder feet against his fore leg.
