AUBUSSON, a small town of France, in the province of La Marche, and the government of the Lyonnois, now the department of Creuse. Its situation is very irregular, on the river Creuse, in a bottom surrounded with rocks and mountains. A manufacture of tapestry is carried on here, by which the town is rendered very populous. E. Long. 2. 15. N. Lat. 45. 58.
