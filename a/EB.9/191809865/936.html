BARCELONETTA

BARCELONETTA, a town of France, in the department of the Lower Alps, formerly in the government of Dauphiny, and capital of the valley of its own name. It belonged to the duke of Savoy, and was ceded to France by the treaty of Utrecht in 1712. E. Long. 6. 40. N. Lat. 44. 26.
