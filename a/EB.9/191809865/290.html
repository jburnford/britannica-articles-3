AUGMENTATION

article · 272 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUGMENTATION, in Heraldry, are additional charges to a coat-armour, frequently given as particular marks of honour, and generally borne either in the escutcheon or a conton; as have all the baronets of England, who have borne the arms of the province of Ulster in Ireland.
