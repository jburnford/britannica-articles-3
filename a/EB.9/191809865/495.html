AZEKA

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AZEKA, in Ancient Geography, a city of the Ammorites, in the lot of Judah; situated between Eleutheropolis and Elia (Jerome); where the five kings of the Ammorites and their army were destroyed by hailstones from heaven, (Joshua).
