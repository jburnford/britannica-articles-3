ATTOURNMENT

article · 159 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATTOURNMENT, or ATTORNMENT, in Law, a transfer from one lord to another of the homage and service a tenant makes; or that acknowledgment of duty to a new lord.
