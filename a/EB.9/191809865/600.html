BADIGEON

article · 365 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BADIGEON, a mixture of plafter and free-ftone, well ground together, and sifted; ufed by flatuaries to fill up the little holes, and repair the defects in ftones, whereof they make their ftatues and other work.

The fame term is alfo ufed by joiners for faw-duft mixed with ftrong glue, wherewith they fill up the chaps and other defects in wood after it is wrought.
