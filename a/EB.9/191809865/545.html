BACCHIC song

sub_entry · 163 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BACCHIC song, is sometimes used for a chançon à boire,
or composition to inspire jollity. But in a more proper
sense it is restrained to a dithyrambic ode or hymn.
