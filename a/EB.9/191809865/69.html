ATABYRIS

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATABYRIS, a very high mountain in the island of Rhodes, on which, according to Strabo and Diodorus Siculus, there stood a temple of Jupiter Atabyrius, whose worship a colony of Rhodians carried into Sicily, where a temple was built to the same deity at Agrigentum.
