BALDACHIN

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALDACHIN, or BALDAQIN, in Architecture, a building in form of a canopy, supported by pillars, and frequently used as a covering to insulated altars. Some also use the term baldachin for the shell over a door.
