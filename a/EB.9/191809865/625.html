BAGNERES, a town of France in Gascony, and in

Bagnalack in the county of Bigorre, now the department of the Upper Pyrenees, so called from its mineral waters, which are much resorted to. It is seated on the river Adour, in E. Long. 0. 12. N. Lat. 43. 3.
