AUSTRAL

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUSTRAL, AUSTRALIS, the same with southern. The word is derived from aufter, "south wind." Thus austral signs are the six last signs of the zodiac; so called because they are on the south side of the equinoctial.
