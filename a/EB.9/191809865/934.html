BARCALON

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARCALON, an appellation given to the prime minister of the king of Siam. The barcalon has in his department every thing relating to commerce, both at home and abroad. He is likewise superintendent of the king's magazines.
