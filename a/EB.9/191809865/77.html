ATCHIEVEMENT

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATCHIEVEMENT, in Heraldry, denotes the arms of a person or family, together with all the exterior ornaments of the shield; as helmet, mantle, crest, scrolls, and motto, together with such quarterings as may have been acquired by alliances, all marshalled in order.
