AULON, anciently a town and dock or station for ships in Illyricum, on the Adriatic; now Valona, or Volana, a port-town in the duchy of Ferrara on one of the mouths of the Po, on the gulf of Venice. E. Long. 13. N. Lat. 44. 50.
