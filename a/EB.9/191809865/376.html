AURENGABAD

article · 484 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AURENGABAD, a city in the East Indies, capital of the province of Balagate, in the dominions of the Great Mogul. It is furnished with handsome mosques and caravanseras. The buildings are chiefly of freestone, and pretty high, and the streets planted on each side with trees. They have large gardens well stocked with fruit trees and vines. The soil about it is also very fertile, and the sheep fed in its neighbourhood are remarkably large and strong. E. Long. 75. 30. N. Lat. 19. 10.
