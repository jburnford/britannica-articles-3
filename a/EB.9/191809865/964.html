BARI

BARI, a very handsome and rich town of Italy, in the kingdom of Naples; the capital of Terra di Bari, and an archbishop's see. It is well fortified, is seated on the gulf of Venice, and had formerly a good harbour, but it was destroyed by the Venetians. E. Long. 17. 40. N. Lat. 41. 35.
