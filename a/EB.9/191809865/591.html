BADENOCH

BADENOCH, the moft eaftly part of Invernefsfhire, in Scotland, extending about 33 miles in length from eaft to weft, and 27 from north-eaft to fouth-weft where broadeft. It has no confiderable town, and is very barren and hilly, but abounds with deer, and other kinds of game.
