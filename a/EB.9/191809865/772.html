BALTAGI

article · 342 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BALTAGI, among the Turks, porters, and hewers of wood, in the court of the grand signior; who also mount on horseback when the emperor rides out. Part of them also, who, for that purpose, must be castrated, keep watch at the gates of the first and second courts of the seraglio. The first are called capigi, and their commander capigi pascha.
