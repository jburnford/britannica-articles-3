ASTIGI, in Ancient Geography, a colony, and con-
ventus juridicus, of Bretica, furnished Augusta Firma,
situated on the Singulus, which falls into the Batis;
called also Colonia Astigitana (Pliny): Now Eyca,
midway between Seville and Cordova. W. Long. 5°. N.
Lat. 37. 20.
