ATHY

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ATHY, a town of Ireland, in the county of Kildare, not far from the borders of Queen's county. W. Long. 7. 0. N. Lat. 53. 0. It is situated on the river Barrow; is governed by a sovereign, two bailiffs, and a recorder; and is, alternately with Naas, the affixes town.
