AVOIRDUPOIS

article · 370 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AVOIRDUPOIS. This is the weight for the larger and coarser commodities, such as groceries, cheese, wool, lead, &c. Bakers, who live not in corporation towns, are to make their bread by avoirdupois weight, those in corporations by troy weight. Apothecaries buy by avoirdupois weight, but sell by troy. The proportion of a pound avoirdupois to a pound troy is as 17 to 14.
