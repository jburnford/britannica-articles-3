AUTUMNAL POINT

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AUTUMNAL POINT, is that part of the equinox from which the sun begins to descend towards the south pole.

Autumnal Signs, in Astronomy, are the signs Li-Autumnal
Autumnal Equinox, that time when the sun enters the autumnal point. Auxiliary.
