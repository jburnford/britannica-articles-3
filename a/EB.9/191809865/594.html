BADGE

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BADGE, in naval architecture, fignifies a fort of ornament placed on the outfide of fmall fhips, very near the ftern, containing either a window for the convenience of the cabin, or a reprefentation of it. It is commonly decorated with marine figures, martial inftruments, or fuch like emblems.
