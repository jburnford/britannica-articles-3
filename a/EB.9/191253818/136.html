ABEL-MEBOLAB

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABEL-MEBOLAB, the country of the prophet Elijah, situated on this side Jordan, between the valley of Jerreel and the village Bethmael, in the plains of Jordan, where the Midianites were defeated by Gideon. Judges vii. 22.
