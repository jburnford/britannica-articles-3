ACCELERATION

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACCELERATION, in Mechanics, the increase of velocity in a moving body. Accelerated motion is that which continually receives fresh accessions of velocity. Acceleration stands directly opposed to retardation, which denotes a diminution of velocity.
