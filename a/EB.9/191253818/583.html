ACOMINATUS

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACOMINATUS, NICETAS, was secretary to Alexius Comnenus and to Isaacus Angelus successively: he wrote a history from the death of Alexius Comnenus in 1118, where Zonaras ended his, to the year 1203, which has gone through many editions, and has been much applauded by the best critics.
