ACQUARIA

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACQUARIA, a small town of Italy, in Frigiana, a district of Modena, which is remarkable for its medicinal waters. It is 12 miles south of the city of Modena. E. Long. 11. 17. N. Lat. 42. 24.
