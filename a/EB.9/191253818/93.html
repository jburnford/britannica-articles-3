ABBOTS-LANGLEY, a village in Herts, four miles from St. Alban's, famous as the birthplace of Pope Adrian IV.

ABBREVIATE of ADJUDICATIONS, in Scots Law, an abridgment or abridgement of a decree of adjudication, which is recorded in a register kept for that purpose.
