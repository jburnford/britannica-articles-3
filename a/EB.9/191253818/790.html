ADEPTS

article · 352 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADEPTS, a term among Alchemists for those who pretended to have found the panacea and philosophers stone. "Such is the nature," says Paracelsus, of this higher philosophy, that one mortal can no more communicate it to another, than the paper on which letters are traced can of itself declare their meaning. It originates not from man, but from heaven."
