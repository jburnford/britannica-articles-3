ADMIRATION

article · 180 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADMIRATION, in Ethics, is that passion of the mind which is excited by the contemplation of superior and rare excellence, as superior or uncommon wisdom, ingenuity, or benevolence.
