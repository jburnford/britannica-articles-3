ABELLA

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABELLA, anciently a town of Campania, near the river Clanius. The inhabitants were called Abellani, and said to have been a colony of Chalcidians. The nux Avellana, called also Prænestina, or the basel nut, takes its name from this town, according to Macrobius. Now Avella.
