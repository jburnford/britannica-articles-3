AGISTOR

article · 275 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGISTOR, or AGISTATOR, an officer belonging to forests, who has the care of cattle taken in to be grazed, and levies the monies due on that account. They are generally called quest-takers or gift-takers, and are created by letters-parent. Each royal forest has four agistors.
