ACHARACA

article · 177 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACHARACA, anciently a town of Lydia, situated between Tralles and Nysa; in which were the temple of Pluto, and the cave Charonum, where patients slept in order to obtain a cure.
