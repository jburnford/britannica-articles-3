AGNO

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AGNO, a river of Naples, which, taking its rise in the mountainous parts of Terra di Lavoro, waithes the town of Acerra; and, passing between Capua and Aversa, falls into the Mediterranean, about seven miles north of Puzzuoli.
