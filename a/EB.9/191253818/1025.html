ENONA, in Ancient Geography, a city of Liburnia, called by Pliny Civitas Primi, the reason of which is unknown; also Enona, and is now called

Enona; on the Adriatic, by which it is for the greater part surrounded; over against the island Gissa, from which it is distant four miles to the west. E. Long. 16°. N. Lat. 28°.
