ACO

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACO, in Geography, a town of Peru in South America. It is also the name of a river in Africa, which rises in the Abyssinian mountains, runs in a south-east course, and discharges itself into the Indian ocean.
