ABSTRUSE

article · 313 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABSTRUSE, something deep, hidden, concealed, or far removed from common apprehensions, and therefore not easily understood; in opposition to what is obvious and palpable. Thus metaphysics is an abstruse science; and the doctrine of fluxions, and the method de maximis et minimis, are abstruse points of knowledge.
