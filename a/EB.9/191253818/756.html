ADDICTION

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADDICTION, among the Romans, was the marking over goods to another, either by sale, or by legal sentence; the goods so delivered were called bona addicta. Debtors were sometimes delivered over in the same manner; and thence called servi addicti.
