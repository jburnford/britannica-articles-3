ADRIANI, JOANNI BATTISTA, was born of a patrician family at Florence in 1511. He wrote a History of his own Times in Italian; which is a continuation of Guicciardini, beginning at the year 1536; to which Thuanus acknowledges himself greatly indebted: besides which, he composed six funeral orations,

* Barrenies
Anat. tom.
xii. an. 1154

Adrianists, on the emperor Charles V. and other noble personages; and is thought to have been the author of a long letter on ancient painters and sculptors, prefixed to the third volume of Vasari. He died at Florence in 1579.
