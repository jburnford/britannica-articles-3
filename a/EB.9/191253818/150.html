ABERAVON

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABERAVON, a borough town of Glamorganshire in Wales, governed by a portreeve. It had a market, which is now discontinued. The vicarage is discharged, and is worth 451. clear yearly value. It is seated at the mouth of the river Avon, 194 miles west of London. W. Long. 3. 21. N. Lat. 51. 40.
