ACCOLTI, Francis

sub_entry · 446 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACCOLTI, Francis, brother of the former, was born about the year 1418. He was professor of jurispru-

dence in several universities, and was styled the prince of lawyers. His understanding was vigorous, his knowledge was extensive, and his eloquence powerful; but he was so fondly parsimonious that he amassed immense treasures. He died about the year 1470; and left behind him several works on law, and some translations of the works of Chrysostom.
