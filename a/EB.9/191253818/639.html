ACROCERAUNIA, or MONTES CERAUNII, in
Ancient Geography, mountains running out into the sea
(so called from their being often thunderstruck), sep-
arating the Ionian sea from the Adriatic; where Illyria
ends and Epirus begins: now called Monti della Chi-
mera.
