AFWESTAD

article · 308 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AFWESTAD, a large copper-work belonging to the crown of Sweden, which lies on the Dala, in the province of Dalecarlia, in Sweden. It looks like a town, and has its own church. Here they make copper plates; and have a mint for small silver coin, as well as a royal post-house. W. Long. 14. 10. N. Lat. 58. 10.
