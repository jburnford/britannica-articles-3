ABSURD

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABSURD, an epithet applied to any thing that is contrary to human apprehension, and contradicts a manifest truth. Thus, it would be absurd to say that 6 and 6 make only 10, or to deny that twice 6 make 12. When the term absurd is applied to actions, it has the same import as ridiculous.
