ADOLLAM

article · 171 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADOLLAM, or ODOLLAM, in Ancient Geography, a town in the tribe of Judah, to the east of Eleutheropolis. David is said to have hid himself in a cave near this town (Bible).
