ACILISENE

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACILISENE, in Ancient Geography, a district of the lesser Armenia towards the head of the Euphrates, having that river on the west, and on the south a river to which Xenophon and Pliny seem to have given the same name.
