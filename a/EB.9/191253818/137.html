ABEL-MINRIM

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABEL-MINRIM, (called also the Threshing-floor of Atad), signifying the lamentation of the Egyptians; in allusion to the mourning for Jacob, Gen. i. 3, 10, 11. Supposed to be near Hebron.
