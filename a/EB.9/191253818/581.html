ACOMA

ACOMA, a town of New Mexico, seated on a hill, with a strong castle. To reach the town, you walk up 50 steps cut out of the rock. It is the capital of that province, and was taken by the Spaniards in 1599. W. Long. 104. 15. Lat. 35. 0.
