ACCOMPANIMENT

article · 170 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACCOMPANIMENT, in Painting, denotes such objects as are added, either by way of ornament or fitness to the principal figures; as dogs, guns, game, &c. in a hunting piece.
