ACRASIA

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACRASIA, among Physicians, implies the predominance of one quality above another, either with regard to artificial mixtures, or the humours of the human body. The word is Greek, and compounded of a privative, and sigmos, to mix; q. d. not mixed in a just proportion.
