ACEPHALI

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACEPHALI, the levellers in the reign of King Henry I. who acknowledged no head or superior. They were reckoned so poor, that they had not a tenement by which they might acknowledge a superior lord.
