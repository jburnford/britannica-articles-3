ADAGIO

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADAGIO, in Music. Adverbially, it signifies softly, leisurely; and is used to denote the slowest of all times. Used substantively, it signifies a slow movement. Sometimes this word is repeated, as adagio, adagio, to denote a still greater retardation in the time of the music.
