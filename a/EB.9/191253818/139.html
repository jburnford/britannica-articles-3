ABEL-SATTIM

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABEL-SATTIM, or Sattim, a town in the plains of Moab, to the north-east of the Dead Sea, not far from Jordan, where the Israelites committed fornication with the daughters of Moab: So called, probably, from the great number of sittim trees there.
