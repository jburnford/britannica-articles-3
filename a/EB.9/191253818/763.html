ADDITION

ADDITION, in Music, a dot marked on the right side of a note, signifying that it is to be sounded or lengthened.

Addition ened half as much more as it would have been without such mark.

Adel. ADDITION, in Law, is that name or title which is given to a man over and above his proper name and surname, to show of what estate, degree, or mystery he is; and of what town, village, or country.
