ABALAK

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABALAK, a small town of Siberia, two miles from Tobolisk, in E. Long. 64. 10. N. Lat. 57. 1. Abalak is famous as the resort of many pilgrims who visit an image of the virgin Mary, which is annually carried in procession to Tobolisk.
