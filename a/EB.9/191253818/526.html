ACHMET

article · 265 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACHMET, son of Seerim, an Arabian author, has left a book concerning the interpretation of dreams according to the doctrine of the Indians, Persians, and Egyptians, which was translated into Greek and Latin. The original is now lost. He lived about the 4th century.
