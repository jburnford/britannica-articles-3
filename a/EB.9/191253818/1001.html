ÆINAUTÆ

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ÆINAUTÆ, in Antiquity, aiναυται, always mariners, a denomination given to the senators of Miletus, because they held their deliberations on board a ship, and never returned to land till matters had been agreed on.
