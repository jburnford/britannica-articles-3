ADJAZZO, ADRAZZO, or AJACCIO, in Geography, a handsome town and castle of Corsica in the Mediterranean, with a bishop's see, and a good harbour. It is populous, and fertile in wine. It is 27 miles south-west of Corte. E. Long. 41. 54. N. Lat. 38. 5.
