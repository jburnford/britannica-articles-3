ABBOTSBURY

ABBOTSBURY, a small town in Dorsetshire, in W. Long. 1. 17. N. Lat. 50. 40. The abbey near

this town was founded by a Norman lady, about the year 1026. Edward the Confessor and William the Conqueror were considerable benefactors to it.
