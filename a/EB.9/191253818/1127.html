AFSLAGERS

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AFSLAGERS, persons appointed by the burgh-masters of Amsterdam to preside over the public sales made in that city. They must always have a clerk of the secretary's office with them, to take an account of the sale. They correspond to our brokers, or auctioneers.
