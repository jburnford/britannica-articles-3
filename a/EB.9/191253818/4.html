AAM

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AAM, or HAAM, a liquid measure in common use among the Dutch, containing 128 measures called mingles, each weighing nearly 36 ounces avoirdupois; whence the Aam contains 288 English, and 148½ pints Paris measure.
