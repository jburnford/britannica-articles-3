ABTHANES

article · 254 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABTHANES, in History, a title of honour used by the ancient inhabitants of Scotland, who called their nobles thanet, which in the old Saxon signifies king's ministers; and of these the higher rank were styled abthanet, and those of the lower underthanet.
