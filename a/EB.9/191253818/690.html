ACTORUM TABULÆ

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACTORUM TABULÆ, in Antiquity, were tablets instituted by Servius Tullius, in which the births of children were registered. They were kept in the treasury of Saturn.
