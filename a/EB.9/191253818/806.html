ADIAPHORISTS

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADIAPHORISTS, in Church History, a name importing lukewarmness, given, in the 16th century, to the moderate Lutherans, who embraced the opinions of Melanchthon, whose disposition was much more pacific than that of Luther.
