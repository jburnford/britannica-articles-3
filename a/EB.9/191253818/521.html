ACHILLES TATIUS. See TATIUS.

Tendo Achilles, in Anatomy, is a strong tendinous
cord formed by the tendons of several muscles, and in-
serted into the os calcis. It has its name from the fa-
tal wound Achilles is said to have received in that part
from Paris the son of Priam.
