ADDA

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADDA, in Geography, a river of Switzerland and Italy, which rises in Mount Branlio, in the country of the Grisons, and, passing through the Valaisine, traverses the lake Como and the Milanese, and falls into the Po, near Cremona.
