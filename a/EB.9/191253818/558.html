ACINASIS

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACINASIS, in Ancient Geography, a river of Asia, at the southern extremity of Colchis, which discharges itself into the Euxine sea, between the Bathys and the Ibis. It is mentioned by Arrian in his Periplus.
