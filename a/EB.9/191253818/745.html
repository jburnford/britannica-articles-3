ADATAIS

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADATAIS, ADATSI, or ADATYS, in Commerce, a muslin or cotton cloth, very fine and clear, of which the piece is ten French ells long, and three quarters broad. It comes from the East Indies; and the finest is made in Bengal.
