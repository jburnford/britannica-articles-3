ADYUTANTS-general

sub_entry · 379 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADYUTANTS-general, among the Jesuits, a select number of fathers, who resided with the general of the order, each of whom had a province or country assigned him, as England, Holland, &c. and their business was to inform the father-general of state occurrences in such countries. To this end they had their correspondents delegated, emissaries, visitors, regents, provincials, &c.
