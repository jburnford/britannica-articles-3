ABAS

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABAS, in Heathen Mythology, was the son of Hypothon and Meganira, who entertained Ceres, and offered a sacrifice to that goddess; but Abas ridiculing the ceremony, and giving her opprobrious language, she sprinkled him with a certain mixture she held in her cup, on which he became a newt or water lizard.
