ABLAY

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABLAY, in Geography, a country of Great Tartary, governed by a Calmuck chief, but subject to Russia, to obtain its protection. It lies east of the river Irtsch, and extends 500 leagues along the southern frontiers of Siberia, from E. Long. 72° to 83°. N. Lat. from 51° to 54°.
