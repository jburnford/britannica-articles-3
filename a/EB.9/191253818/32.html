ABAISSED

article · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABAISSED, abaissé, in Heraldry, an epithet applied to the wings of eagles, &c. when the tip looks downwards.

Abaka downwards to the point of the shield, or when the wings are shut; the natural way of bearing them being extended.
