ACCEDAS AD CURIAM

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACCEDAS AD CURIAM, in English Law, a writ lying where a man has received, or fears, false judg-

ment in an inferior court. It lies also for justice delayed, and is a species of the writ RECORDARE.
