AERSCHOT

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AERSCHOT, a town of the Austrian Netherlands, in the duchy of Brabant, and capital of the duchy of Aerschot. It is seated on the river Demur, ten miles east of Malines or Mechlin, and eight north of Louvain. E. Long. 5. 44. N. Lat. 51.
