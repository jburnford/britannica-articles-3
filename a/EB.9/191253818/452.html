ACCUBITOR

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACCUBITOR, an ancient officer of the emperors of Constantinople, whose business was to lie near the emperor. He was the head of the youth of the bed-chamber, and had the cubicularius and procubitor under him.
