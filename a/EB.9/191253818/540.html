ACHROMATIC Telescopes, are telescopes contrived to remedy the aberrations in colours. They were invented

Achtzling ed by Mr John Dollond, optician, and have been since improved by his son and others. See ABERRATION.
Acidalus.—A more particular account of the invention and construction of these instruments will be found under OPTICS.
