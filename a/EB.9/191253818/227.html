ABORTIVE CORN

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABORTIVE CORN, a dissemper of corn mentioned by M. Tillet, and suspected to be occasioned by insects. It appears long before harvest, and may be known by a deformity of the stalk, the leaves, the ear, and even the grain.
