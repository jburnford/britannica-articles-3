AD PONDUS OMNIUM

article · 372 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AD PONDUS OMNIUM, among Physicians, an abbreviation in their prescriptions, signifying that the last mentioned ingredient is to weigh as much as all the rest together.

Ad Quod Damnum, in the English Law, a writ directed to the sheriff, commanding him to inquire into the damage which may arise from granting certain privileges to a place, as a fair, a market, or the like.
