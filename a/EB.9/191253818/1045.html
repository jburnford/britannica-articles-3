ARARIUM VICESMARUM

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARARIUM VICESMARUM, the place where the money arising from the taxes levied from foreign countries was laid up, so called because it most commonly consisted of a twentieth part of the produce.
