ACROCHERISMUS

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACROCHERISMUS, among the Greeks, a sort
of gymnastic exercise, in which the two combatants
contended with their hands and fingers only, without
closing or engaging the other parts of the body.
