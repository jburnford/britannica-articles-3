AGENOIS

AGENOIS, in Geography, a country of France, in the department of the Garonne, formerly the province of Guienne. It contains about one hundred and twenty square leagues; is fertile and healthy; and, according to Cæsar, was inhabited by the Nitiobriges. It constituted part of the kingdom of Aquitania; was held by the counts of Toulouse, and successively by the English and French.
