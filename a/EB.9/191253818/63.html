ABATE

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABATE, in the manège, implies the performing any downward motion properly. Thus a horse is said to abate or take down his curvets, when he puts both his hind legs to the ground at once, and observes the same exactness in all the times.
