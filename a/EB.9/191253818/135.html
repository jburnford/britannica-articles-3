ABEL-KERAMIM

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABEL-KERAMIM, or Vinearum, beyond Jordan, in the country of the Ammonites, where Jephthah defeated them, seven miles distant from Philadelphia, abounding in vines, and hence the name. It was also called Abela.
