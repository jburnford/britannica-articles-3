ABSYRTUS

article · 305 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABSYRTUS, in Heathen Mythology, the son of Ates and Hypse, and the brother of Medea. The latter running away with Jason, after her having assisted him in carrying off the golden fleece, was pursued by her father; when, to stop his progress, she tore Absyrtus in pieces, and scattered his limbs in his way.
