ABACTORS

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABACTORS, or ABACTORES, a name given to those who drive away, or rather steal, cattle by herds, or great numbers at once; and are therefore very properly distinguished from furors or thieves.
