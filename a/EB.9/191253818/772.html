ADEB

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADEB, in Commerce, the name of a large Egyptian weight, used principally for rice, and consisting of 210 okes, each of three rotolos, a weight of about two drams less than an English pound. But this is no certain weight; for at Rosetto the adeb is only 150 okes.
