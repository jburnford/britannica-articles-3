ACHROMATIC

article · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ACHROMATIC, an epithet expressing want of colour. The word is Greek, being compounded of a privative, and χρῶν, colour. This term was first introduced into astronomy by De la Lande.
