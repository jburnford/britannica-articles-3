ABUSE

article · 310 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABUSE, an irregular use of a thing, or the introducing something contrary to the true intention thereof. In grammar, to apply a word abusively, or in an abusive sense, is to misapply or pervert its meaning.—A permutation of benefits, without the consent of the bishop, is termed abusive, and consequently null.
