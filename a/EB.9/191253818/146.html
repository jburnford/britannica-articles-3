ABEN MELEK

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ABEN MELEK, a learned rabbin, who wrote a commentary on the Old Testament in Hebrew, entitled, "The Perfection of Beauty." This rabbin generally follows the grammatical sense and the opinions of Kimchi. The best edition is that of Holland.
