ADDRESS

article · 274 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ADDRESS, in a general sense, is used for skill and good management, and of late has been adopted from the French. It is used also in commerce, as synonymous with direction to a person or place. The word is formed of the French verb, adresser, To direct anything to a person.
