GIUSTANDEL

article · 177 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GIUSTANDEL, a large and strong town of Turkey in Europe, and in Macedonia, with a Greek archbishop's see. It is seated near the lake Ochrida, in E. Long. 20. 50. N. Lat. 41. 10.
