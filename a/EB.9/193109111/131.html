GENERATED

article · 319 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GENERATED, or GENITED, is used, by some mathematical writers, for whatever is produced, either in arithmetic, by the multiplication, division, or extraction of roots; or in geometry, by the invention of the contents, areas, and fides; or of extreme and mean proportionals, without arithmetical addition and subtraction.
