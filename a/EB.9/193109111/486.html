GOLD-WIRE FLATTED

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: STUFFS

GOLD-WIRE FLATTED, is the former wire flattened between two rollers of polished steel, to fit it to be spun on a stick, or to be used flat, as it is, without spinning, in certain stuffs, laces, embroideries, &c. See STUFFS, &c.
