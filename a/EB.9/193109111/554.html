GORITIA

GORITIA, or GORITZ, a strong town of Germany, in the circle of Austria, and duchy of Carniola, with a castle; seated on the river Lizonzo, 20 miles north east of Aquileia, and 70 north-east of Venice. E. Long. 13. 43. N. Lat. 46. 12.
