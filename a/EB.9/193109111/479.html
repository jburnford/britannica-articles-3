GOGMAGOG HILLS

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOGMAGOG HILLS, are hills so called, three miles from Cambridge, remarkable for the intrenchments and other works cast up here: whence some suppose it was a Roman camp; and others, that it was the work of the Danes.
