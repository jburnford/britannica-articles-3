GORGET

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GORGET, a kind of breast-plate like a half-moon, with the arms of the prince thereon; worn by the officers of foot. They are to be either gilt or silver, according to the colour of the buttons on the uniforms.
