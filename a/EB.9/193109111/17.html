GASCONADE

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GASCONADE, a boast or vaunt of something very improbable. The term has its rise from the Gascos, or people of Gascony in France, who it seems have been distinguished for bragging and rhodomontade.
