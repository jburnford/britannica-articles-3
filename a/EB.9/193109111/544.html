GOREY

GOREY, a borough, fair, and post-town in the county of Wexford, province of Leinster, otherwise called Newborough. It stands about 18 miles north of Wexford town, and 45 from Dublin. N. Lat. 52. 40. W. Long. 6. 30. It sends two members to parliament; patronage in the family of Ram.
