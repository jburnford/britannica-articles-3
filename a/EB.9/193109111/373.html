GLANDORE Harbour

sub_entry · 545 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GLANDORE Harbour, situated two leagues west of the Galley-head in the county of Cork, province of Munster, N. Lat. 51. 22. W. Long. 8. 56. Between this harbour and Ross the coast continues high and bold, with only two small coves; that to the east called Millicove, and that to the west Cowecove. This harbour lies three miles west of Ross; and though small, is an exceeding good one; near it is a castle of the same name, and on the upper end is a deep and dangerous gulf, called the Leap. Glandore gives title of earl to the family of Crosbie.
