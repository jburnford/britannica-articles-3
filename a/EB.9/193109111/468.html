GOBLET

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GOBLET, or GOBELET, a kind of drinking cup, or bowl, ordinarily of a round figure, and without either foot or handle. The word is French, gobelet; which Salmasius, and others, derive from the barbarous Latin cupa. Budeus deduces it from the Greek κωπιδας, a sort of cup.
