GLOSS

article · 324 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GLOSS, a comment on the text of any author, to explain his sense more fully and at large, whether in the same language or any other. See the article COMMENTARY.—The word, according to some, comes from the Greek γλωσσα, "tongue;" the office of a gloss being to explain the text, as that of the tongue is to discover the mind.
