GLOTTIS

Cross-references: ANATOMY

GLOTTIS, in Anatomy, the narrow slit at the upper part of the aspera arteria, which is covered by the epiglottis when we hold our breath and when we swallow. The glottis, by its dilatation and contraction, modulates the voice. See ANATOMY, No 183.
