GLYNN

GLYNN, a county in the lower district of Georgia, in America, bounded on the east by the ocean, on the north by the river Alatahaha, by which it is separated from Liberty county, and on the south by Camden. It contains 413 people, of which 215 are slaves. The chief town is Brunswick.
