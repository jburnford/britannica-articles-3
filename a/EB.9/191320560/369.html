EQUES Auratus

sub_entry · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EQUES Auratus, is used to signify a knight-bachelor, called auratus, q. d. gilt, because anciently none but knights might gild or beautify their armour or other habiliments of war with gold. In law this term is not used, but instead of it miles, and sometimes chevalier.
