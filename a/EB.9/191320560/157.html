EMUNCTORY

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EMUNCTORY, in Anatomy, a general term for all those parts which serve to carry off the excrementitious parts of the blood and other humours of the body. Such more especially are the kidneys, bladder, and most of the glands.
