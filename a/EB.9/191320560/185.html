ENERGUMENS

article · 451 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: EXORCISM

ENERGUMENS, in church-history, persons supposed to be possessed by the devil, concerning whom there were many regulations among the primitive Christians. They were denied baptism and the eucharist; at least, this was the practice of some churches; and though they were under the care of exorcists, yet it was thought a becoming act of charity to let them have the public prayers of the church, at which they were permitted to be present.
