ERASISTRATUS

article · 317 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ERASISTRATUS, a celebrated physician, grandson to the philosopher Aristotle. He discovered by the motion of the pulse the love which Antiochus had conceived for his mother-in-law Stratonicus, and was rewarded with 100 talents for the cure by the father of Antiochus. He was great enemy to bleeding and violent physic.
