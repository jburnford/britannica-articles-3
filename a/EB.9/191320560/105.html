EMBRICATION

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EMBRICATION, in Surgery and Pharmacy, an external kind of remedy, which consists in an irrigation of the part affected, with some proper liquor, as oils, spirits, &c. by means of a woolen or linen cloth, or a sponge, dipped in the same.
