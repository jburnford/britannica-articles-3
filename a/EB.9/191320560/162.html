ENCENIA

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENCENIA, the name of three several festivals celebrated by the Jews in memory of the dedication, or rather purification, of the temple, by Judas Maccabaeus, Solomon, and Zorobabel. This term is likewise used in church history for the dedication of Christian churches.
