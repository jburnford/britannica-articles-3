ESCUAGE

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ESCUAGE, in our old customs, a kind of knight-service, called service of the shield, by which the tenant was bound to follow his lord to the wars at his own charge. See the articles CHIVALRY, FEODAL SYSTEM, and KNIGHT-SERVICE.
