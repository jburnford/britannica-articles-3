EXODIUM

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXODIUM, in the ancient Greek drama, one of the four parts or divisions of tragedy, being so much of the piece as included the catastrophe and unraveling of the plot, and answering nearly to our fourth and fifth acts.
