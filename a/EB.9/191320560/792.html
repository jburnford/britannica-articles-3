EXPRESSION

article · 302 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXPRESSION, in Medicine, Chemistry, &c. the act of expression or extracting the juices or oils of plants, fruits, or other matters, by squeezing, wringing, or pressing them in a press. After having let the herbs infuse a due time, their juice must be drawn by expression in a linen cloth or by a press.
