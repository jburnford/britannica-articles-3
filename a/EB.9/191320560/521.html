ETERNITY

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ETERNITY, in Mythology, a divinity among the Romans, who had neither temples nor altars. They represented it under the figure of a woman, who held the sun in one hand and the moon in the other: her symbols were a phoenix, globe, and elephant.
