ENTE

article · 316 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENTE, in Heraldry, a method of marshalling, more frequent abroad than with us, and signifying grafted or ingrafted.

We have indeed, one instance of enté in the fourth grand quarter of his majesty's royal ensign, whose blazon is Brunswick and Lunenburg impaled with ancient Saxony, enté en pointe, "grafted in point."
