ENTABLER

Cross-references: ACULER

ENTABLER, in the manège, the fault of a horse whose croup goes before his shoulders in working upon volts; which may be prevented by taking hold of the right rein, keeping your right leg near, and removing your left leg as far from the horse's shoulder as possible.

This is always accompanied with another fault called aculer. See ACULER.
