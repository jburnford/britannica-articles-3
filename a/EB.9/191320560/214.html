ENMANCHE

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENMANCHE, in Heraldry, is when lines are drawn from the centre of the upper edge of the chief to the sides, to about half the breadth of the chief; signifying sleeved, or resembling a sleeve, from the French manche.
