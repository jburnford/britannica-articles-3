EPHORUS

EPHORUS, an orator and historian of Cumæ in Æolia, about 352 years before Christ. He was disciple to Isocrates, by whose advice he wrote a history which gave an account of all the actions and battles that had happened between the Greeks and barbarians for 70 years. It was greatly esteemed by the ancients; but is now lost.
