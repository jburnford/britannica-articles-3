ELIJAH

article · 1,058 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ELIJAH, who is sometimes denominated Elias, was one of the most distinguished of the Jewish prophets, and furnished the Tifbbite, probably from the district in which he was born. He began his prophetic office about 920 years before Christ, in the reign of wicked Ahab, by whom the Sidonian idolatry was introduced among the Israelites. The prophet was commissioned to appear before this impious prince, and threaten the country with a long drought as a punishment for his crimes.
