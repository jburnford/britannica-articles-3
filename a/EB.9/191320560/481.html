ESCULENT

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ESCULENT, an appellation given to such plants, or the roots of them, as may be eaten: such are beets, carrots, artichokes, leeks, onions, parsnips, potatoes, radishes, scorzoneria, &c.
