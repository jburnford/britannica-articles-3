EXALTATION

article · 603 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXALTATION, in Astrology, is a dignity which a planet acquires in certain signs or parts of the zodiac; which dignity is supposed to give it an extraordinary virtue, efficacy, and influence. The opposite sign, or part of the zodiac, is called the dejection of the planet. Thus the 15th degree of Cancer is the exaltation of Jupiter, according to Albamazor, because it was the ascendant of that planet at the time of the creation; that of the sun is in the 19th degree of Aries, and its dejection in Libra; that of the moon is in Taurus, &c. Ptolemy gives the reason of this in his first book De Quadrup.
