EPISODE

Cross-references: POETRY

EPISODE, in Poetry, a separate incident, story, or action, which a poet invents, and connects with his principal action, that his work may abound with a greater diversity of events; though, in a more limited sense, all the particular incidents whereof the action or narration is compounded are called episodes. See POETRY.
