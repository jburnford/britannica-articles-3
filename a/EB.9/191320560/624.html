EUSTACHIUS, BARTHOLOMEW

article · 165 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EUSTACHIUS, BARTHOLOMEW, physician and anatomist at Rome, flourished about the year 1550. His anatomical plates were discovered there in 1712, and published in 1714.
