EPEUS

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EPEUS, of the line of Endymion, the inventor of the battering ram, an engine of great service in sieges to make a breach. He is thought to have built the Trojan horse, and to have founded the city Metapontum.
