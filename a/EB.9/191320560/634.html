EUTYCHIUS

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EUTYCHIUS, patriarch of Alexandria, lived about the ninth age; and wrote annals in the Arabic language, printed at Oxford in 1658, with a Latin version by Mr Pococke. Selden had printed something of his before.
