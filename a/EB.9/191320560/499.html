ESSAYING

article · 410 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: CHEMISTRY

ESSAYING, or ASSAYING, in Chemistry and Metallurgy, signifies the examination of a small quantity of any ore or mineral by fire, in order to discover the quantity of metal it contains. This is very necessary for those who intend to deal largely in metallurgical operations, in order to avoid unnecessary expence, by becoming previously acquainted with the nature of the ore.
