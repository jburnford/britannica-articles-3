EUNUCHS

article · 380 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EUNUCHS, in church history, a sect of heretics in the third century, who were mad enough to castrate, not only those of their own persuasion, but even all others they could lay hold of. They took their rise from the example of Origen, who, misunderstanding the following words of our Saviour, "and eunuchs who made themselves eunuchs for the kingdom of heaven," castrated himself.
