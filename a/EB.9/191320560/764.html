EXPEDITION

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXPEDITION, the march of an army to some distant place, with a view of hostilities. Such were the expeditions of Cyrus against Artaxerxes, and of Bacchus and Alexander into the Indies.

Expeditions for the recovery of the Holy Land were called crusades.
