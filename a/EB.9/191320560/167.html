ENCEINTE

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ENCEINTE, in Fortification, is the wall or rampart which surrounds a place, sometimes composed of bastions or curtains, either faced or lined with brick or stone, or only made of earth. The encinte is sometimes only flanked by round or square towers, which is called a Roman wall.
