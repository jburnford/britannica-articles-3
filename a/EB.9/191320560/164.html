ENCANTHIS

Cross-references: SURGERY

ENCANTHIS, in Surgery, a tubercle arising either from the caruncula lachrymalis, or from the adjacent red skin; sometimes so large, as to obstruct not only the puncta lachrymalia, but also part of the sight or pupil itself. See SURGERY.
