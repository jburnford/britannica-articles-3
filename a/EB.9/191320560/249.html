EOSTRE

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EOSTRE, in Mythology, a Saxon goddess to whom they sacrificed in the month of April, called the month of Eofra; and thence the name Easter, which the Saxons retained after their conversion to Christianity, applying it to the festival celebrated in commemoration of our Saviour's resurrection.
