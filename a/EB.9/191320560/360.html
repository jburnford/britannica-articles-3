EQUANIMITY

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EQUANIMITY, in Ethics, denotes that even and calm frame of mind and temper, under good or bad fortune, whereby a man appears to be neither puffed up nor overjoyed with prosperity, nor dispirited, soured, or rendered uneasy by adversity.
