ESCHARA

ESCHARA, in Natural History, the name of a species of coralline, &c. the characters of which are these: they are of a stony or coral-like hardness, and resemble a woven cloth in their texture; and the microscope informs us, that they consist of arrangements of very small cells, whose surfaces appear much in that form. Linnæus makes it a species of millepora, in the class of lithophytes.
