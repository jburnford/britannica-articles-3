ESCOUADE

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: BRIGADE

ESCOUADE, or SQUAD, is usually the third or fourth part of a company of foot: to divided for mounting guards, and for the more convenient relieving of one another. It is equivalent to brigade of a troop of horse. See BRIGADE.
