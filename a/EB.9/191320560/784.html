EXPOSITOR

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXPOSITOR, or EXPOSITORY, a title which some writers have given to a lesser kind of dictionaries or vocabularies, serving to expound or explain the meaning of the obscure or difficult words of a language. It is also used in the same sense with commentary and paraphrase.
