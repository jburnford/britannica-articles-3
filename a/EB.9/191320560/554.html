EUCRASY

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EUCRASY, (of ev, well, and agous, temperature), in Medicine, an agreeable well-proportioned mixture of qualities, whereby a body is said to be in good order, and disposed for a good state of health.
