EXPOSURE, in Gardening, the situation of a garden wall, or the like, with respect to the points of the compass, as south or east. See GARDENING.

Ex-post-facto, in Law, denotes something done after another thing that was committed before. An estate granted may be made good by matter ex-post-facto, that was not so at first by election, &c.
