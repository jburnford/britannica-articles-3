ERYTHEA

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ERYTHEA, or ERYTHIA, an island adjoining, according to the ancients, either to or a part of Gades; nowhere now to be found by the description given of it by ancient authors. The poets feign this to be the habitation of the fabulous Geryon, disarmed by Hercules, who drove away his cattle.
