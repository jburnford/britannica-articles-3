EYE, in Agriculture and Gardening, signifies a little bud or shoot, inserted into a tree by way of graft. See ENGRAFTING.

Eye of a Tree, a small pointed knot to which the leaves stick, and from which the shoots or sprigs proceed. See GEMMA.
