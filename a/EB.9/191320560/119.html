EMIMS

article · 368 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EMIMS, ancient inhabitants of the land of Canaan beyond Jordan, who were defeated by Chedorlaomer and his allies, Gen. xiv. 5. Moses tells us, that they were beaten in Shaveh Kirjathaim, which was in the country of Sihon conquered from the Moabites, Josh. xiii. 19.—21. The Emims were a warlike people, of a gigantic stature, great and many, and tall as the Ammonites.
