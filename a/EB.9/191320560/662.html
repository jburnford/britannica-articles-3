EXCELLENCY

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXCELLENCY, a title anciently given to kings and emperors, but now to ambassadors, generals, and other persons who are not qualified for that of highness, and yet are to be elevated above the other inferior dignities.
