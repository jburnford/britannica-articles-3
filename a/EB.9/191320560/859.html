FABIUS

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FABIUS, styled Pictor, a Roman general and historian. He first introduced painting at Rome; and having caused the walls of the temple of Health to be painted, some authors have erroneously reckoned him a painter. He died about 216 B. C.
