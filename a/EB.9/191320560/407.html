ERATO

article · 366 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ERATO (from ἔρως, I love), in Mythology, the name of one of the nine muses, who presided over love-poetry. To this muse some have ascribed the invention of the lyre and lute; and she is represented with a garland of myrtles and roses, holding a lyre in one hand and a bow in the other, and at her side a Cupid with his torch. There is also a Nereid of the same name.
