EXODIUM

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EXODIUM, in the Septuaginta, signifies the end or conclusion of a feast. Particularly it is used for the eighth day of the feast of tabernacles, which, it is said, had a special view to the commemoration of the exodus or departure out of Egypt.
