COMO

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMO, the lake so called, is the largest in Italy. It is situated in the duchy of Milan in the Comasco, on the confines of Switzerland and the Grisons. It is 88 miles in circumference, yet is not above 6 miles over in any part.
