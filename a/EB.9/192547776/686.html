COHORN (N.) the greatest engineer Holland has produced. Among his other works, which are esteemed masterpieces of skill, he fortified Bergen-op-zoom; which, to the surprise of all Europe, was taken by the French in 1747; but that, it is believed, was the effect of treachery. He wrote a treatise on fortification, and died in 1704.
