Sepulchral COLUMN

sub_entry · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Sepulchral COLUMN, anciently was a column erected on a tomb or sepulchre, with an inscription on its base. Those over the tombs of persons of distinction were very large; those for the common people small: these last are called stelae and epipi.
