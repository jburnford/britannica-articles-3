COL, one of the western islands of Scotland, which is annexed to the county of Argyle. It is 13 miles long, and 9 broad. It abounds in corn, pasture, salmon, eels, and cod. The inhabitants are chiefly employed in the fisheries. W. Long. 7. 15. N. Lat. 57.
