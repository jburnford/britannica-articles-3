CHISON, KISON, or KISSON, (Judges iv. and v.) a river of Galilee; said to rise in Mount Tabor, to run by the town of Naim, and to fall into the Mediterranean between Mount Carmel and Ptolemais, (1 Kings xviii. 40.)
