White CINNAMON

sub_entry · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: CANELLA

White CINNAMON; called also Winter's bark, is the bark of a tree frequent in the isles of St. Domingo, Guadaloupe, &c. of a sharp biting taste like pepper. Some use it instead of nutmeg; and in medicine it is esteemed a stomachic and antiscorbutic. See CANELLA.
