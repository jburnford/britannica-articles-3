Indicative COLUMN

sub_entry · 348 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: NILOMETER

Indicative COLUMN, that which serves to show the tides, &c. along the sea-coasts. Of this kind there is one at Grand Cairo of marble, on which the overflowing of the Nile are expressed; by this they form a judgment of the succeeding seasons; when the water, for instance, ascends to 23 feet, it is a sign of great fertility in Egypt. See NILOMETER.
