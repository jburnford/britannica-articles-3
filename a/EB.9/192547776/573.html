CLYTIA

article · 442 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLYTIA, or CLYTIE, daughter of Oceanus and Tethys, beloved by Apollo. She was deserted by her lover, who paid his addresses to Leucothoe; and this so irritated her, that she discovered the whole intrigue to her rival's father. Apollo despised her the more for this; and she pined away, and was changed into a flower, commonly called a sun-flower, which still turns its head towards the sun in his course in token of her love.
