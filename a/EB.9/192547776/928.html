COMMEMORATION

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMMEMORATION, in a general sense, the remembrance of any person or thing, or the doing any thing to the honour of a person's memory, or in remembrance of any past event. Thus, the eucharist is a commemoration of the sufferings of Jesus Christ.
