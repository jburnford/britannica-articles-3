CIOBAT

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CIOBAT, a sea-port town of Provence in France; famous for Muscadine wine. It is seated on the bay of Laquea, between Marseilles and Toulon; and the harbour is defended by a strong fort. E. Long. 5. 30. N. Lat. 43. 10.
