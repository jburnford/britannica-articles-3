CLEOMBROTUS II. son of Pausanias king of Sparta, after his brother Ageispolis I. He made war against the Boeotians, and left he should be suspected of treacherous communications with Epaminondas, he gave that general battle at Leuctra, in a very disadvantageous place. He was killed in the engagement, and his army destroyed, in the year of Rome 382.
