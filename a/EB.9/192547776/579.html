COACH

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COACH, or COUCH, is also a sort of chamber or apartment in a large ship of war near the stern. The floor of it is formed by the aftmost part of the quarter-deck, and the roof of it by the poop: it is generally the habitation of the captain.
