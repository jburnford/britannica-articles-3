COLONOS

article · 407 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLONOS, in Ancient Geography, an eminence near Athens, whither Ædipus, after his banishment from Thebes, is said to have retired; and hence it is that Sophocles calls the tragedy on the subject Oedipus Coloneus. A place sacred to Neptune, and where stood an equestrian statue of him. Here also stood Timon's tower; who, for his love of solitude, and hatred to mankind, was called Misanthropos, (Paufanias).
