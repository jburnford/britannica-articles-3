CLEMENT V

article · 298 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLEMENT V. POPE, the first who made a public sale of indulgences. He transplanted the holy see to Avignon in France; greatly contributed to the suppression of the knights templars; and was author of a compilation of the decrees of the general councils of Vienne styled Clementines. He died in 1314.
