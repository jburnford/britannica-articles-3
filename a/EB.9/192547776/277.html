CIRCULAR Numbers

sub_entry · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CIRCULAR Numbers, called also spherical ones, according to some, are such whole powers terminate in the roots themselves. Thus, for instance, 5 and 6, all whole powers do end in 5 and 6, as the square of 5 is 25; the square of 6 is 36, &c.
