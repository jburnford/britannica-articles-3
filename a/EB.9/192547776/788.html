COLLIQUATION

article · 360 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLLIQUATION, in Physic, a term applied to the blood, when it loses its crasis or balsamic texture; and to the solid parts, when they waste away, by means of the animal fluids flowing off through the several glands, and particularly those of the skin, faster than they ought; which occasions fluxes of many kinds, but mostly profuse, greasy, and clammy sweats.
