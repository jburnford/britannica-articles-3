COMMUNIS

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMMUNIS, in Botany, the name of a class in Linnaeus's Methodus Galcinea, consisting of two plants which, like teazel and dandelion, have a calyx or flower-cup common to many flowers or florets. These are the aggregate or compound flowers of other systems.
