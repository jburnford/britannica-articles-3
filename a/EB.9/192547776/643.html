CODEX, in antiquity, denotes a book or tablet on which the ancients wrote. See COD.

Codex also denoted a kind of punishment by means of a clog or block of wood, to which slaves who had offended

Codia || Coccum. fended were tied fast, and obliged to drag it along with them; and sometimes they sat on it closely bound.
