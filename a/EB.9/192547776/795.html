COLLYRÆ

article · 179 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLLYRÆ, or COLLYRIDES, in antiquity, a certain ornament of hair, worn by the women on their necks. It was made up in the form of the small roundish cakes called καλλυρα, collyre.
