COLONUS

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLONUS, a husbandman, or villager, who was bound to pay yearly a certain tribute, or at certain times of the year to plough some part of the lord's land; and from hence comes the word clown, who is called by the Dutch, boer.
