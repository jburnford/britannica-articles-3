CHITON, in Zoology, a genus of the order of vermes testaceæ. The name chiton is from χίτων, lorica,

a coat of mail. The shell is plated, and consists of many parts lying upon each other transversely: the inhabitant is a species of the Doris. See CONCHOLGY Index.
