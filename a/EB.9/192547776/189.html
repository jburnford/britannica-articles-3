CIBUS FERALIS

article · 158 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CIBUS FERALIS, in antiquity, an entertainment peculiar to a funeral; for which purpose, beans, parsley, lettuce, bread, eggs, lentils, and salts, were in use.
