CILICIUM

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CILICIUM, in Hebrew antiquity, a sort of habit made of coarse stuff, formerly in use among the Jews in times of mourning and distress. It is the same with what the Septuagint and Hebrew versions called sackcloth.
