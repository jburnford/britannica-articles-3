COLNbrook

sub_entry · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLNbrook, a town of Buckinghamshire in England, seated on the river Coln, which separates this county from Middlesex. It is a great thoroughfare on the western road, and has several good inns. W. Long. 0. 25. N. Lat. 51. 30.
