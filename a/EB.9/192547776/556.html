CLOTH-LINES

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLOTH-LINES are for the same purpose as clue-garnets; only that the latter are confined to the courses, whereas the former are common to all the square sails. See these ropes as represented in the article SAIL.
