Polar CIRCLES

sub_entry · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Polar CIRCLES, are immovable circles, parallel to the equator, and at a distance from the poles equal to the greatest declination of the ecliptic. That next the northern pole is called the ARCTIC; and that next to the southern one the ANTARCTIC.
