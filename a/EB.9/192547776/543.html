CLOTHO

article · 321 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLOTHO, the youngest of the three Parcae, daughters of Jupiter and Themis. She was supposed to preside over the moment that we are born. She held the distaff in her hand and spun the thread of life, whence her name κλωθιν, to spin. She was represented wearing a crown with seven stars, and covered with a variegated robe.
