CIRCUMLOCUTION

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CIRCUMLOCUTION, an ambages, or tour of words, used either when a proper term is not at hand, to express a thing naturally and immediately by; or when one chooses not to do it, out of respect, or for some other reason. The word comes from circumloquor, "I speak about."
