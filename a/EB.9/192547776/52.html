CHION VINUM, Chian Wine, or wine of the growth of the island of Chios, now Scio, is commended by Dioscorides as affording good nourishment, fit to drink, less disposed to intoxicate, endued with the virtue of restraining delusions, and a proper ingredient in ophthalmic
