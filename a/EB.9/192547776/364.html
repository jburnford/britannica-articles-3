CLANDESTINE

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLANDESTINE, any thing done without the knowledge of the parties concerned, or without the proper solemnities. Thus a marriage is said to be clandestine, when performed without the publication of banns, the consent of parents, &c.
