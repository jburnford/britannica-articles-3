COLONIA METALLINA

COLONIA METALLINA, or Metellinenfis, a town of Lusitania, situated on the right or west side of the Anas, or Guadiana; but now on the left or east side, from the river's shifting its bed or channel, and called Medellin, a town in Estremadura. W. Long. 6. 12. Lat. 38. 45.
