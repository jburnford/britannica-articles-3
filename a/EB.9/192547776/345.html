CIVITA-DI-PENNA

CIVITA-DI-PENNA, an ancient town of Italy, in the kingdom of Naples, and in the Farther Abruzzo, with a bishop's see. It is situated near the river Salino, 25 miles north-east of Aquila. E. Long. 13. 3. N. Lat. 42. 25.
