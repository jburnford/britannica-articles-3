CLAVARIUM

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLAVARIUM, in antiquity, an allowance the Roman soldiers had for furnishing nails to secure their shoes with. They raised frequent mutinies, demanding largesses of the emperors under this pretence.
