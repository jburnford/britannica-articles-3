COLLECT, COLLECTION, a voluntary gathering of money, for some pious or charitable purpose. Some say, the name collect, or collection, was used, by reason those gatherings were anciently made on the days of collects, and in collects, i. e. in assemblies of Christians; but, more probably, quia colligebatur pecunia.
