CONCERT

CONCERT, or CONCERTO, in Music, a number or company of musicians, playing or singing the same piece of music or song at the same time.

CONCERTATO intimates the piece of music to be composed in such a manner, as that all the parts may have their recitatives, be it for two, three, four, or more voices or instruments.
