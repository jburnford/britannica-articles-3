CLAUDIA Copia (Inscriptions), a name of Lugdunum, or Lyons in France, the birth-place of the emperor Claudius: A Roman colony called Claudiana, from its benefactor the emperor; and Copia, from its plenty of all necessaries, especially corn. See LUGDUNUM.
