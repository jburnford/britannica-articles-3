CHORDEE

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHORDEE, in Medicine and Surgery, a symptom attending a gonorrhœa, consisting in a violent pain under the frenum, and along the duct of the urethra, during the erection of the penis, which is incurved downwards. These erections are frequent and involuntary.
