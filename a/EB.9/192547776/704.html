COLAPIS

COLAPIS, COLORS, in Ancient Geography, a river of Liburnia, which after a winding north-east course, falls into the Savus, at the Insula Segetica. Now the Culpe, the boundary of the Alps, running through Croatia into the Save. Colapiani, the people living on it (Pliny).
