COLONIA

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COLONIA, in Ancient Geography, a town of the Trinobantes, a little above Camelodunum. Now Colchester in Essex, according to Camden, who supposes it to take its name from the river Colne, and not that it was a colony; though others think Antonine's distances agree with Sudbury.
