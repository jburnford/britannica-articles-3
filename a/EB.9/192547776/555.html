CLUE-GARNETS

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLUE-GARNETS, are a sort of tackles fastened to the clues, or lower corners of the mainsail or foresail, to truss them up to the yard as occasion requires, which is usually termed clueing up the sails.
