CLEROMANCY

article · 521 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLEROMANCY, a kind of divination performed by the throwing of dice, or little bones; and observing the points, or marks, turned up. The word comes from κλερος, "lot," and μαντικός, "divination." At Bura, a city of Achaia, was a temple and celebrated oracle of Hercules, where such as consulted the oracle, after praying to the idol, threw four dice, the points whereof being well scanned by the priest, he was supposed to draw an answer from them.

Something of this kind seems to have been practiced with regard to Jonah.
