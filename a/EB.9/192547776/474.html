CLERK of the Escheats

sub_entry · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLERK of the Escheats, an officer in the exchequer, who every term receives the escheats out of the lord-treasurer's remembrancer's office, and writes them out to be levied for the crown.
