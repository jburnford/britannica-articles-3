CLERK of the Pipe

sub_entry · 420 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CLERK of the Pipe, an officer of the exchequer, who having the accounts of all debts due to the king, delivered out of the remembrancer's office, charges them in a great roll folded up like a pipe. He writes out warrants to sheriffs, to levy the said debts on the goods and chattels of the debtors; and if they have no goods, then he draws them down to the treasurer's remembrancer to write escheats against their lands.
