CHIOURLIC

article · 170 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHIOURLIC, an ancient town of Turkey in Europe, and in Romania, with a fee of a Greek bishop. It is seated on a river of the same name, in E. Long. 7. 47. N. Lat. 41. 18.
