CHIPPENHAM

CHIPPENHAM, a town of Wiltshire, seated on the river Avon. It is a good thoroughfare town; has a handsome stone bridge over the river, consisting of 16 arches; and sends two members to parliament. There is here a manufacture of the best superfine woollen cloth in England. W. Long. 2. 12. N. Lat. 51. 25.
