COLONIA TRAJANA, (Antonine, Peutinger); a town

of Belgica, furnished also Ulpia, (Antonine); and Trifina, from being the station of the thirtieth legion, (Ammian). Now Kellen, a village of the duchy of Cleves, a mile from the Rhine.
