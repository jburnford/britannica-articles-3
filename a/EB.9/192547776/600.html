COBITIS, the LOACHE, in Ichthyology, a genus of fishes belonging to the order of abominales. See ICHTHYOLOGY Index. It is frequent in the stream near Amesbury in Wiltshire, where the sportsmen, through frolic, swallow it down alive in a glass of white-wine.
