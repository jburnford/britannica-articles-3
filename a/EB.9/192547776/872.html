Chronological COLUMN

sub_entry · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Chronological COLUMN, that which bears some historical inscription digested according to the order of time; as by lustris, olympiads, fasti, epochs, annals, &c. At Athens, there were columns of this kind, whereon was inscribed the whole history of Greece digested into olympiads.
