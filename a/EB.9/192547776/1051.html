COMPOUND Numbers

sub_entry · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COMPOUND Numbers, those which may be divided by some other number besides unity, without leaving any remainder; such are 18, 20, &c. the first being measured by the numbers 2, 6, 9; and the second by the numbers 2, 4, 5, 10.
