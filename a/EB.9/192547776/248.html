CINUS

article · 278 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CINUS, or CYNUS, a famous civilian of Pitosa in the 14th century. His commentary on the Code was finished in 1313; he also wrote on some parts of the digest. He was no less famous for his Italian poems, and is ranked among those who first gave graces to the Tuscan lyric poetry.
