CLERK of the Essoin

Cross-references: ESSOIN

CLERK of the Essoin, in the court of common pleas, keeps the essoin roll, or enters essoins: he also provides parchment, cuts it into rolls, marks the numbers on them, delivers out all the rolls to every officer, and receives them again when written. See ESSOIN.
