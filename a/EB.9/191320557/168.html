CHIAUSI

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHIAUSI, among the Turks, officers employed in executing the vizirs, bakhaws, and other great men: the orders for doing this, the grand signior sends them wrapped up in a black cloth; on the reception of which they immediately perform their office.
