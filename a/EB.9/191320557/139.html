CHESNE, ANDREW DU

CHESNE, ANDREW DU, styled the father of French history,

history, was born in 1584. He wrote, 1. A history of the popes. 2. A history of England. 3. An inquiry into the antiquities of the towns of France. 4. A history of the cardinals. 5. A bibliotheca of the authors who have written the history and topography of France, &c. He was crushed to death by a cart, in going from Paris to his country house at Verriere, in 1640.
