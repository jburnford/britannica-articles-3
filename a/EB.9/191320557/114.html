CHEPELIO

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHEPELIO, an island in the bay of Panama and province of Darien, in South America, situated about three leagues from the city of Panama, which it supplies with provisions. W. Long. 81. N. Lat. 9.
