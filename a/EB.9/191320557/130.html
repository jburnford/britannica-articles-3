CHERSO

CHERSO, an island in the gulf of Venice, with a town, of the same name near Croatia, belonging to the Venetians. The air is good, but the soil stony; however, it abounds in wine, cattle, oil, and excellent honey. E. Long. 15. 5. N. Lat. 45. 8.
