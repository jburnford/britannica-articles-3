CHATEAU-NEUF

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHATEAU-NEUF, the name of several towns of France, viz. one in Perche; another in Angoumois, on the river Charente, near Angoulême; a third in Berry, seated on the river Cher; and several other small places.
