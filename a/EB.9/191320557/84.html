CHEBRECHIN

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHEBRECHIN, a town of Poland, in the province of Russia and palatinate of Belskow. It is seated on the declivity of a hill, and the river Wierpi waters its walls, and afterwards falls into the river Bog. The Jews there are very rich. E. Long. 23. 51. N. Lat. 50. 35.
