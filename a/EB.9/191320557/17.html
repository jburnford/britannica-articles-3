CHARNEL

article · 237 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHARNEL, or CHARNEL HOUSE, a kind of portico, or gallery, usually in or near a churchyard, over which were anciently laid the bones of the dead, after the flesh was wholly consumed. Charnel-houses are now usually adjoining to the church.
