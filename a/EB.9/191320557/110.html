CHEMNITZ, MARTIN

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHEMNITZ, MARTIN, a famous Lutheran divine, the disciple of Melanchthon, was born at Britzen in Brandenburg, in 1522. He was employed in several important negotiations by the princes of the same communion; and died in 1589. His principal work is the examen of the Council of Trent, in Latin.
