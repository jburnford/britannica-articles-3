CHEMOSIS

article · 350 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHEMOSIS, a disease of the eyes, proceeding from an inflammation; wherein the white of the eye swells above the black, and overtops it to such a degree, that there appears a sort of gap between them. Others define it to be an elevation of the membrane which surrounds the eye, and is called the white; being an affection of the eye, like white flesh.
