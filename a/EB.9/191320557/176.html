CHICUITOS

article · 375 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHICUITOS, a province of South America, in the government of Santa-Cruz de la Sierra. The chief riches consist of honey and wax; and the original inhabitants are very voluptuous, yet very warlike. They maintained bloody wars with the Spaniards till 1690;

since which, some of them have become Christians. It is bounded by la Plata on the north east, and by Chili on the west.
