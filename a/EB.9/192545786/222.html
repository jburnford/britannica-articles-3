MARBLE

Cross-references: MINERALOGY

MARBLE, a calcareous stone, of which there are many beautiful varieties. The word comes from the French marbre, and from the Latin marmor, of the Greek μαρμαρ to "shine or glitter." See MINERALOGY Index.
