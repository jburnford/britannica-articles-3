MATAMAN

MATAMAN, a country of Africa, bounded by Benguela on the north, by Monomotapa on the east, by Caffraria on the south, and by the Atlantic ocean on the west. There is no town in it, and the inhabitants live in miserable huts, it being a desert country, and but little visited by the Europeans.
