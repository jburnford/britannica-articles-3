MASSACHUSETTS COLONY

article · 237 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MASSACHUSETTS COLONY, the principal subdivision of New England, having Hampshire on the north, the Atlantic ocean on the east and south, and Connecticut and New York on the west. It is about 100 miles long, and 40 broad. See New ENGLAND.
