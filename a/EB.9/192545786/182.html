MANTLE

article · 179 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MANTLE, or MANTLE-TREE, in Architecture, the lower part of the chimney, or that piece of timber which is laid across the jambs, and sustains the compartments of the chimney-piece.
