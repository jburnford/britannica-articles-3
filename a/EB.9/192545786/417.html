MASQUE

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MASQUE, in Architecture, is applied to certain pieces of sculpture, representing some hideous forms, grotesque, or satyrs faces, &c. used to fill up and adorn vacant places, as in friezes, the panells of doors, keys of arches, &c. but particularly in grottos.
