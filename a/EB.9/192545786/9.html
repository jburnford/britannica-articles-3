MAIA

article · 214 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAIA, in fabulous history, the daughter of Atlas and Pleione. She was the mother of Mercury by Jupiter. She was one of the Pleiades, the most luminous of the seven sisters; (see PLEIADS). Also, a surname of Cybele.
