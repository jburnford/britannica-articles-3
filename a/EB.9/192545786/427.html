MASSALIANS

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MASSALIANS, a set of enthusiasts who sprang up about the year 361, in the reign of the emperor Constantius, who maintained that men have two souls, a celestial and a diabolical, and that the latter is driven out by prayer.
