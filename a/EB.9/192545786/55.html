MALE, among zoologists, that sex of animals which has the parts of generation situated externally. See SEX and GENERATION.

The term male has also, from some similitude to that sex in animals, been applied to several inanimate things; thus we say, a male flower, a male screw, &c. See MAS PLANTA, MASCULUS FLOS, and SCREW; also FE-MALE and FLOS.
