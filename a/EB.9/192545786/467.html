MATACA

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MATACA, or MANTACA, a commodious bay in America, on the north coast of the island of Cuba. Here the galleons usually come to take in fresh water in their return to Spain. It is 35 miles from the Havannah. W. Long. 85. 6. N. Lat. 25. 0.
