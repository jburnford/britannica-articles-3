MAGNA ASSISA ELIGENDA

article · 275 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAGNA ASSISA ELIGENDA, is a writ anciently directed to the sheriff for summoning four lawful knights before the justices of assize, in order to choose 12 knights of the neighbourhood, &c. to pass upon the great assize between such a person plaintiff and such a one defendant.
