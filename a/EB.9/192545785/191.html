LIXA

LIXA, or LIXUS, in Ancient Geography, a town on the Atlantic near the river Lixus; made a Roman colony by Claudius Cæsar; famous in mythology for the palace of Antæus and his encounter with Hercules, (Pliny). Now Larache, 65 leagues to the south of the straits of Gibraltar.
