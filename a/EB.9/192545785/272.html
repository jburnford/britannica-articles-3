LONGTOWN

LONGTOWN, a town of Cumberland, on the Scots borders, near the conflux of the Esk and Kirkcudry, 10 miles from Carlisle, and 313 miles from London; it has a market on Thursday, and a charity school for 60 children, with two fairs in the year.
