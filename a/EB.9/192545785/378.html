LUCRINUS LACUS

LUCRINUS LACUS, in Ancient Geography, a lake of Campania, between Baiae and Puteoli, famous for its oysters (Horace, Martial, Juvenal); Lucrinenses (Cicero), the people dwelling on it. Now a perfect bay since the earthquake in 1538.
