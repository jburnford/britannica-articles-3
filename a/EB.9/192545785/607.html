MÆMACTERION

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MÆMACTERION, was the fourth month of the Athenian year, containing twenty-nine days, and answering to the latter part of our September, and the beginning of October. It received its name from the festival Mæmacteria, which was observed about this time. This month was called by the Boeotians Alcomenius.
