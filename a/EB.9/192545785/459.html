LUSITANIA

article · 373 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LUSITANIA, in Ancient Geography, one of the divisions of Spain, extending to the north of the Tagus, quite to the sea of Cantabria, at least to the Promontorium Celticum. But Augustus, by a new regulation, made the Anas its boundary to the south, the Durius to the north; and thus constituting only a part of the modern Portugal. Lusitani the people, (Diodorus, Stephanus).
