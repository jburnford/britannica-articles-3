LUNARIUM, in Ancient Geography, a promontory of the Hither Spain, between Blanda and Baetulo. Commonly called el Cabo de Palafugel, in Catalonia, on the Mediterranean; or Cabo de Tosa, on the same coast, and in Catalonia, 15 miles from the former, to the west.
