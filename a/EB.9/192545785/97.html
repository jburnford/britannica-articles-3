LINEAR NUMBERS

article · 365 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LINEAR NUMBERS, in Mathematics, such as have relation to length only; such is a number which represents one side of a plain figure. If the plain figure be a square, the linear figure is called a root.

LINEAR Problems; that which may be solved geometrically by the intersection of two right lines. This is called a simple problem, and is capable but of one solution.
