LYSIARCH

article · 301 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LYSIARCH, an ancient magistrate, who superintended the sacred games, and presided in matters of religion in the province of Lycia. He was created in a council consisting of deputies from all the provincial cities, in number 23. The lysarchs were both heads of the council and pontiffs of the province.
