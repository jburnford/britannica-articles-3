MADRE DE POPA, a town and convent of South America, in Terra Firma, seated on the river Grande. It is almost as much resorted to by pilgrims of America as Loretto is in Europe; and the image of the Virgin Mary is said to have done many miracles in favour of the seafaring people. W. Long. 76. o. N. Lat. 11. o.
