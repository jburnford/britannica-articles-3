LYCÆUS

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LYCÆUS, in Ancient Geography, a mountain of Arcadia, sacred to Jupiter; whence Jupiter Lyceus (Pilsay). Sacred also to Pan (Virgil); and hence Lyceus, the rites performed to Pan on this mountain; which Evander carrying with him to Latium, were called Lupercalia (Virgil).
