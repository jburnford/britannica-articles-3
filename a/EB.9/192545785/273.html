LONGUEVILLE

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LONGUEVILLE, a town of France, in the department of Lower Seine, and in the territory of Caux, seated on the small river Lee, 17 miles north of Rouen.

It has the title of a duchy. E. Long. 1. 10. N. Lat. 49. 46.
