LOCAL

article · 340 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LOCAL, in Law, something fixed to the freehold, or tied to a certain place; thus, real actions are local,

since they must be brought in the country where they lie; and local customs are those peculiar to certain countries and places.

Local Medicines, those destined to act upon particular parts; as fomentations, epithems, vesicatories, &c.
