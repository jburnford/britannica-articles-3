LIMOGES

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIMOGES, an ancient town of France, in the late province of Guienne, and capital of the department of Upper Vienne, with a bishop's see. It is a trading place, and its horses are in great esteem. It is seated on the river Vienne, in E. Long. 1. 20. N. Lat. 45. 50.
