LYGII, LIGII, Lugii, or Logiones, in Ancient Geo-
graphy, a people of Germany, to the west of the Vistula,
where it forms a bend like a crescent; Ligii, (Dio);
Lugii, (Strabo); Logiones, (Zofimus). Their name
Lugii is conjectured to be derived from their mutually
close confederacy or league. The Vistula was their
boundary to the north, east, and south, with Mount
Aschburgius to the west. Now the whole of that coun-
try lies in Poland, on this side the Vistula.
