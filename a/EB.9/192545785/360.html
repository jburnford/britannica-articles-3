LUCERIA, in Ancient Geography, a town of Apulia in Italy; which in Strabo's time still exhibited marks of Diomed's sovereignty in those parts. Ptolemy has Nuceria; whether from mistake, or the custom of his time, uncertain. Now Nocera de Pagani, in the kingdom of Naples. E. Long. 15. 0. N. Lat. 40. 40.
