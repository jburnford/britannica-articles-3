MAGONTIACUM, MOGONTIACUM, or Mogontiacus, truncated afterwards by the poets to Mogontia, Maguntia, and Moguntia: a town of Gallia Belgica. Now Mentz, capital of the electorate of that name; situated at the confluence of the Rhine and Maine. E. Long. 8° N. Lat. 50°.
