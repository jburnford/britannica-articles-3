LYONOIS

article · 454 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LYONOIS, a large province of France; bounded on the north by Burgundy; on the east, by Dauphiny, Bresse, and the principality of Dombes; on the south, by Vivarais and Velay; and on the west by Auvergne and a small part of Bourbonnois. It comprehends Lower Lyonnois, Beaujolais, and Forez; and it produces corn, wine, fruits, and more especially excellent chestnuts. The principal rivers are the Saone, the Rhone, and the Loire. Lyons is the capital town.
