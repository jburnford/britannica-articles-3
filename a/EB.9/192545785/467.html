LUTE

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LUTE, or LUTING, among chemists, a mixed, tenacious, ductile substance, which grows solid by drying, and, being applied to the juncture of vessels, stops them up so as to prevent the air from getting either in or out.
