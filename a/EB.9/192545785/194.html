LIXNAW

article · 388 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LIXNAW, a barony in the county of Kerry and province of Munster in Ireland, which gives title of baron to the earls of Kerry; the village here of this name being their ancient seat, where the castle was erected. This seat stands agreeably on the river Brick, which is here cut into several pleasant navigable canals, that adorn its plantations and gardens. W. Long. 9. 15. N. Lat. 52. 15.
