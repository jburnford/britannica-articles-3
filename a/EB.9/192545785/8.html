Lords-LIEUTENANTS of Counties

sub_entry · 444 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Lords-LIEUTENANTS of Counties, are officers, who upon any invasion or rebellion, have power to raise the militia, and to give commissions to colonels and other officers, to arm and form them into regiments, troops, and companies. Under the lords-lieutenants,

are deputy-lieutenants, who have the same power; these are chosen by the lords-lieutenants, out of the principal gentlemen of each county, and presented to the king for his approbation.
