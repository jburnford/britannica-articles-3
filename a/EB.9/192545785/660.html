MAGNUS PORTUS

MAGNUS PORTUS, in Ancient Geography, a port of the Belge, in Britain, on the Channel. Now thought to be Portsmouth in Hampshire.—Another Portus Magnus of Beticia in Spain; a port to the east of Abdera.
