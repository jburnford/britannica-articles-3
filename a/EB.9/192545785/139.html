LISIEUX

article · 295 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LISIEUX, a considerable town of France, in Upper Normandy, with a bishop's see. The churches and religious houses, and the bishop's palace, are all very handsome structures. It is a trading place; and is seated at the confluence of the rivers Arbeck and Gassi, in E. Long. 0. 20. N. Lat. 49. 11.
