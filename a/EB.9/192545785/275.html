LONGUS

article · 475 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LONGUS, a Greek sophist, author of a book entitled Παιδεία, or Pastorals, or a romance containing the loves of Daphnis and Chloe. Huettius, bishop of Avranches, speaks very advantageously of this work; but he censures the obscene touches with which it is interspersed. None of the ancient authors mention him, so that the time when he lived cannot be certainly fixed. There is an English translation of this author, which is ascribed to Mr J. Craggs, once secretary of state.
