LITANA SILVA, in Ancient Geography, a wood of

Blackh.
Comment.

* See Re-
venue.

Litany. of the Boii, in Gallia Togata, or Cispadana, where the Romans, under I. Posthumius Albinus (whose head the Boii cut off, and carried in triumph into their most sacred temple), had a great defeat; of twenty-five thousand scarcely ten escaping (Livy). Holstenius conjectures, that this happened above the forings of the Scultenna, in a part of the Appennine, between Cersinianum and Mutina. Now Selva di Lugo.
