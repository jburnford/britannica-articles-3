SICILIAN

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SICILIAN, in Muse, denotes a kind of gay sprightly air, or dance, probably invented in Sicily, somewhat of the nature of an English jig; usually marked with the characters \frac{6}{8}, or \frac{12}{8}. It consists of two strains; the first of four, and the second of eight, bars or measures.
