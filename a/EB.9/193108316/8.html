SCRUPLE

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCRUPLE, in Chaldean Chronology, is \frac{1}{24} part of an hour, called by the Hebrews helakin. These scruples are much used by the Jews, Arabs, and other eastern people, in computations of time.
