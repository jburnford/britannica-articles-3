SHAMBLES

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHAMBLES, among miners, a sort of niches or
landing places, left at such distances in the adits of the
mines, that the shovel-men may conveniently throw up
the ore from shamble to shamble, till it comes to the top
of the mine.
