SHALLOP

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHALLOP, SHALLOOP, or SLOOP, is a small light vessel, with only a small main-mast and fore-mast, and lug-fails, to haul up, and let down, on occasion.—Shallops are commonly good sailors, and are therefore often used as tenders upon men of war.
