SHEKEL

SHEKEL, the name of a weight and coin current among the ancient Jews. Dr Arbuthnot makes the weight of the shekel equal to 9 pennyweights 2½ grains Troy weight; and the value equal to 2s. 3½d. Sterling. The golden shekel was worth 11. 16s. 6d.
