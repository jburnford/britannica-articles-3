SHEPPEY

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHEPPEY, an island at the mouth of the river Medway, about 20 miles in circumference. It is separated from the main land by a narrow channel; and has a fertile soil, which feeds great flocks of sheep. The borough-town of Queenborough is seated thereon; besides which it has several villages.
