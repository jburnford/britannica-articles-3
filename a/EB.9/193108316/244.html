SEPTUAGESIMA

article · 182 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEPTUAGESIMA, in the calendar, denotes the third Sunday before Lent, or before Quadragesima Sunday: supposed by some to take its name from its being about seventy days before Easter.
