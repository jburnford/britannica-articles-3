SHOP-LIFTERS

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHOP-LIFTERS, are those that steal goods privately out of shops; which, being to the value of 5s. though no person be in the shop, is felony without the benefit of clergy by the 10 and 11 W. III. c. 23.
