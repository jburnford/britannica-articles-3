SHELVES

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHELVES, in sea-language, a general name given to any dangerous shallows, sand-banks, or rocks, lying immediately under the surface of the water, so as to intercept any ship in her passage, and endanger her destruction.
