SEQUANI

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEQUANI, a people anciently forming a part of Gallia Celtica, but annexed to Belgica by Augustus, separated from the Helvetii by Mount Jura, with the Rhine on the east (Strabo), bordering on the Edui and Segustiano to the south, and Lingones to the west (Tacitus). Now Franche Comte.
