SEIZE

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SEIZE, in the sea-language, is to make fast or bind, particularly to fasten two ropes together with rope-yarn. The seizing of a boat is a rope tied to a ring or little chain in the foreship of the boat, by which means it is fastened to the side of the ship.
