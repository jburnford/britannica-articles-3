SEXTANT, in Mathematics, denotes the sixth part of a circle, or an arch comprehending 60 degrees.

The word sextant is more particularly used for an astronomical instrument made like a quadrant, excepting that its limb only comprehends 60 degrees. The use and application of the sextant is the same with that of the quadrant. See QUADRANT; and NAVIGATION, p. 609, &c.
