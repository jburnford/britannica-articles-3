SHELF

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SHELF, among miners, the same with what they otherwise call fast ground, or fast country; being that part of the internal structure of the earth which they find lying even and in an orderly manner, and evidently retaining its primitive form and situation.
