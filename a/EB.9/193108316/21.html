SCYLAX

article · 366 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCYLAX, a celebrated mathematician and geographer of Caria, flourished under the reign of Darius Hystaspes, about 558 B. C. Some have attributed to him the invention of geographical tables. We have under his name a geographical work published by Hoefchelius; but it is written by a much later author, and is perhaps only an abridgement of Scylax's Ancient Geography.
