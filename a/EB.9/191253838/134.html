MIRANDOLA

MIRANDOLA, a town of Italy, and capital of a duchy of the same name, situated between the duchies of Mantua and Modena; is well fortified, and has also a strong citadel and fort. It has been several times taken and retaken. E. Long. 11. 5. N. Lat. 44. 52.
