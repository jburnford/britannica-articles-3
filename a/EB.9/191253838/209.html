MODIFICATION

article · 317 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MODIFICATION, in Philosophy, that which modifies a thing, or gives it this or that manner of being. Quantity and quality are accidents which modify all bodies.

Decree of MODIFICATION, in Scott Law, a decree ascertaining the extent of a minister's stipend, without proportioning it among the persons liable in payment.
