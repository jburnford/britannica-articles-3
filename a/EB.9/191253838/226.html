MOHILA

MOHILA, or MOELIA, one of the Comora islands in the Indian sea, between the north end of Madagascar and the continent of Africa. The inland parts are mountainous and woody; but the lands adjoining to the sea are watered by several fine streams. The island abounds with provisions of all kinds; and the East India ships of different nations sometimes touch here for refreshment.
