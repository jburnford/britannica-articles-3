MINTURNÆ

article · 330 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MINTURNÆ, a town of Campania, between Sinnucia and Formiae. It was in the marshes in this neighbourhood that Marius concealed himself in the mud to avoid the partizans of Sylla. The people condemned him to death; but when his voice alone had terrified the executioner, they showed themselves compassionate and favoured his escape.
