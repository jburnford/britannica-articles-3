MIDHURST

article · 339 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MIDHURST, a town of Sussex, 52 miles from London, has been represented in parliament every since the 4th of Edward II. It is a neat small town, on a hill surrounded with others, having the river Arun at the bottom; and is a borough by prescription, governed by a bailiff, chosen annually by a jury at a court-leet of the lord of the manor.
