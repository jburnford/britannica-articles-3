MONDOVI

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONDOVI, a considerable town of Italy, in Piedmont; with a citadel, university, and bishop's see. It is the largest and most populous town of Piedmont, and is seated in E. Long. 8. 6. N. Lat. 44. 33.
