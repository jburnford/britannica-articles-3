MITCHELSTOWN

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MITCHELSTOWN, a post town of Ireland, in the county of Cork and province of Munster in Ireland, where there is a college founded by the Earl of Kingston for the support of 12 decayed gentlemen and 12 decayed gentlewomen, who have 401. yearly, and handsome apartments.
