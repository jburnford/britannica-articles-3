MIEZA

article · 295 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MIEZA, in Ancient Geography, a town of Macedonia, which was anciently called Strymonium, situated near Stagira. Here, Plutarch informs us, the stone seats and shady walks of Aristotle were shown. Of this place was Paucellus, one of Alexander's generals, and therefore furnished Miezus, (Arrian.)
