MONTFORT-DE-LEMOS

article · 265 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONTFORT-DE-LEMOS, an ancient town of Spain, in the kingdom of Galicia, with a magnificent castle, where the Comarca of Lemos resides. It is seated in a fertile country, 25 miles north-east of Orense, and 55 south-east of Compostella. W. Long. 7. 9. N. Lat. 42. 28.
