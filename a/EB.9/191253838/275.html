MONANDRIA

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONANDRIA, (from monos alone, and andria a man or husband), the name of the first class in Linnaeus's sexual system; consisting of plants with hermaphrodite flowers, which have only one stamen.
