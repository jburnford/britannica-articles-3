MONTÉ-FALCONE, a town of Italy, in Friuli, with a castle. It belongs to the Venetians, and is near the river Ponzano, 10 miles north-west of Aquileia, and 12 north-west of Trieste. E. Long. 13. 0. N. Lat. 46. 4.
