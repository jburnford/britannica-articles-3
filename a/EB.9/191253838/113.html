MINORS

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MINORS, or FRIERS MINOR, an appellation which the Franciscans assume, out of show of humility; calling themselves fratres minores, i. e. lesser brothers, and sometimes minorites. There is also an order of regular minors at Naples, which was established in the year 1588, and confirmed by Sixtus V.
