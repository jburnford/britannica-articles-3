MONTÉ-MOR-O-VELHO

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONTÉ-MOR-O-VELHO, or Monte-major-el-velho, a town of Portugal in the province of Beira, with a very large castle, seated in a fertile country, 10 miles south-west of Coimbra, and 83 north of Lisbon. W. Long. 8. 9. N. Lat. 40. 5.
