MIZEN

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MIZEN, in the sea language, is a particular mast
or sail. The mizen mast stands in the sternmost part
of the ship. In some great ships there are two of
these; when that next the main-mast is called the
main-mizen, and that next the poop the bonaventure
mizen.
