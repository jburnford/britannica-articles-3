MIMOSA, the SENSITIVE PLANT, a genus of plants belonging to the monocotia class; and in the natural method ranking under the 33d order, Lomentaceæ. See BOTANY and MATERIA MEDICA Index.

The name mimosa, signifying "mimic," is given to this genus on account of the sensibility of the leaves, which, by their motion, mimic or imitate, as it were, the motion of animals.
