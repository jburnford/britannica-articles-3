MOLA

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MOLA, an ancient town of Italy, in the kingdom of Naples, and in the Terra di Lavoro, where they pretend to show the ruins of Cicero's house. It is seated on the gulf of Venice, in E. Long. 17. 50. N. Lat. 41. 5.
