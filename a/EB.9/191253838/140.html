MISANTHROPY

article · 177 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MISANTHROPY (from mis, hated, and anthropos, a man); a general dislike or aversion to man, and mankind. In which sense it stands opposed to philanthropy, or the love of mankind.
