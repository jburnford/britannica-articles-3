MONTÉ-PULSANO

MONTÉ-PULSANO, a town of Italy, in Tuscany, with a bishop's see; seated on a high mountain, near the river Chiana, in a country noted for excellent wine, 25 miles south-east of Siena, and 50 south by east of Florence. E. Long. 11. 49. N. Lat. 43. 10.
