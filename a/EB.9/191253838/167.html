MISUSER

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MISUSER, in Law, is an abuse of any liberty or benefit; as "He shall make fine for his MISUSER." Old. Nat. Br. 149. By misuser a charter of a corporation may be forfeited; so also an office, &c.
