MISE

article · 385 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MISE, in law books, is used in various senses: thus it sometimes signifies costs or expences; in which sense it is commonly used in entering of judgements in actions personal. It is also used for the issue to be tried on the grand assize; in which case, joining of the mise upon the mere right, is putting in issue between the tenant and demandant, who has the best or clear-est right.
