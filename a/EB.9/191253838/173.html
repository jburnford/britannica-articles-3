MITHRAS, or MITHRA, a god of Persia and Chaldea, supposed to be the sun. His worship was introduced at Rome. He is generally represented as a young man, whose head is covered with a turban after the manner of the Persians. He supports his knee upon a bull that lies on the ground, and one of whose horns he holds in one hand, while with the other he plunges a dagger in his neck.
