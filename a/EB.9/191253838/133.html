MIRANDO-DE-DOURO

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MIRANDO-DE-DOURO, or Duero, a strong town of Portugal, and capital of the province of Tránsito-Montes, with a bishop's see. It is well fortified, and seated on a rock near the confluence of the rivers Douro and Eresma. W. Long. 5. 40. N. Lat. 41. 30.
