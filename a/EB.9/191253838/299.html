MONOGLOSSUM

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONOGLOSSUM, in Ancient Geography, a mart town of the Hither India, situated on the Sinus Canthi, into which the Indus empties itself. Said to be Mangalor on the coast of Malabar. E. Long. 74°, N. Lat. 13°.
