MONTÉ-FIASCONE

article · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONTÉ-FIASCONE, a small but populous town of Italy, in the territory of the Church, with a bishop's see; seated on a mountain, near the lake Bolsena, in a country abounding with excellent wine, 12 miles south-west of Orvieto, and 45 north-west of Rome. E. Long. 12. 4. N. Lat. 42. 26.
