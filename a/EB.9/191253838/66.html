MILLO

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MILLO, a part of Mount Zion at its extremity; and therefore called Millo of the city of David (2 Chron. xxxii.), taken in with the wall that encompassed Mount Zion. Uncertain whether Beth Milo, (Judges ix. 20.) denotes a place; if it did, it lay near Sechem.
