MONSEIGNEUR

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONSEIGNEUR, absolutely used, was a title restrained to the dauphin of France. This custom was unknown till the time of Louis XIV. before which the dauphin was styled Monseigneur le Dauphin.
