MONTÉGO BAY

article · 341 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MONTÉGO BAY, a town of Jamaica, and, next to Kingston, the most flourishing in the island. It has a very considerable commerce. The harbour is spacious; but rather exposed to the north winds, which at certain times in the year blow with great violence. In June 1795, a fire consumed an immense quantity of stores, and great part of the town.
