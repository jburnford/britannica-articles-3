BLATTARIÆ

Cross-references: VERESCUM

BLATTARIÆ, (from Blatta, a moth or little worm), the title of Scopoli's 12th natural class, in his Flora Carnolica. It is taken from the Blattaria, which was Tournefort's generic name for the verbaecum of Linnaeus. See VERESCUM.
