BARRICADE

article · 352 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARRICADE, or BARRICADO, a military term for a fence formed in haste with vessels, baskets of earth, trees, palliades, or the like, to preserve an army from the shot or assault of the enemy.—The most usual materials for barricades consist of pales or stakes, crossed with batoons, and shod with iron at the feet, usually set up in passages or breaches.
