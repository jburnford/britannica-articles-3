BISTONIS

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BISTONIS, in Ancient Geography, a lake of Thrace near Abdera, on which dwelt the Bistones: hence Bistonius Tyrannus is by Lucan used to denote Diomedes king of Thrace, who fed his horses with human flesh; and Bistonius turbo, a wind blowing from Thrace.
