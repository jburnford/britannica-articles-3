BASTARNÆ

article · 275 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BASTARNÆ, or BASTERNE, a people of German original, manners, and language; who extended themselves a great way to the east of the Vistula, the east boundary of Germany, among the Sarmatæ, as far as the mouth of the Ister and the Euxine; and were divided into several nations.
