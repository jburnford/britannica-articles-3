BOIANO

article · 196 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOIANO, a town of Italy, in the kingdom of Naples, and county of Molese, with a bishop's see. It is seated at the foot of the Apennines, near the river Tiberio, in E. Long. 14. 38. N. Lat. 41. 30.
