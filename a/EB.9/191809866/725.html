lon, in the time of Ptolemy Philadelphus, wrote the History of Chaldæa, which is often cited by the ancients, and of which Josephus gives some curious fragments. The Athenians, according to Pliny, caused his statue, with a golden tongue, to be placed in their Gymnasium.
