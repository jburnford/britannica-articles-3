BELIDOR, BERNARD FOKEST DE, a Catalonian engineer in the service of France, and member of the academies of sciences at Paris and Berlin, and of the

Belief royal society at London; a celebrated mathematician,
Belisarius and author of a number of military tracts in which the
science of mathematics is applied to military uses. Died
in 1765, aged 70.
