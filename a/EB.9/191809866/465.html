BEFORT, a small town of France in the department of Upper Rhine. It was ceded to France by the treaty of Westphalia in 1648. There are not above 100 houses in this town, but it is important on account of the great road by this place from Franche Comte. The fortifications were greatly augmented by Louis

XIV. It is seated at the foot of a mountain. E. Long. 6. 2. N. Lat. 47. 38.
