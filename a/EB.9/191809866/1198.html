BLASTED

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BLASTED, something struck with a blast. Among the Romans, places blasted with lightning were to be consecrated to Jupiter, under the name of bidentalia and putealia. It was also a ceremonial of religion to burn blasted bodies in the fire.
