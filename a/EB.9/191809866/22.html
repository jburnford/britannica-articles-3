ST BARNABAS'S GOSPEL

sub_entry · 489 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ST BARNABAS'S GOSPEL, another apocryphal work, ascribed to St Barnabas the apostle, wherein the history of Jesus Christ is related in a manner very different from the account given us by the four Evangelists. The Mahometans have this gospel in Arabic, and it corresponds very well with those traditions which Mahomet followed in his Koran. It was, probably, a forgery of some nominal Christians; and afterwards

altered and interpolated by the Mahometans, the better to serve their purpose.
