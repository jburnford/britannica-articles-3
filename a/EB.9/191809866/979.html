BIOUAC

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BIOUAC, BIOUAC, or BIOVAC, in the military art, a nightly guard performed by the whole army, when there is an apprehension of danger from the enemy. The word is formed by corruption from the German weytwacht, a double watch or guard.
