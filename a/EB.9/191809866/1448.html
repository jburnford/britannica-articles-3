BOMBUS

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOMBUS, in Medicine, denotes a murmuring noise, as of wind breaking out of a narrow into a larger cavity, frequently heard in the thick intestines. The bombus heard in the ears, in acute diseases, is laid down by Hippocrates as a sign of death.
