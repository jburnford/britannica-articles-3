BAUCONICA

BAUCONICA, in Ancient Geography, a town of the Vangiones in Gallia Belgica; nine miles from Mogontiacum, and eleven from Borbitomagum; and therefore supposed to be Oppenheim, a town in the palatinate of the Rhine, and situated on that river.
