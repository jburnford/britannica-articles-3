BIR, or BERG, a town of the province of Diarbeck in Turkey in Asia, with a castle where the governor resides, seated on the eastern bank of the river Euphrates, near a high mountain in a very pleasant and fertile country. E. Long. 38. 6. N. Lat. 36. 10.
