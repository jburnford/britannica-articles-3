BETROTHMENT

article · 324 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BETROTHMENT, a mutual promise or compact between two parties for a future marriage. The word imports as much as giving one's troth; that is, true faith, or promise. Betrothment amounts to the same with what is called by civilians and canonists sponsalia, or "espousals; sometimes desponsation, and by the French fiancailles.
