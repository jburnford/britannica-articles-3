BOIORUM DESERTA

BOIORUM DESERTA, in Ancient Geography, a district of Pannonia, so called from the excision of the Boii by the Getae. Now the Weinerwald, of Lower Austria, towards Styria, to the east of Mount Cetius, or the Hahlenberg, and south of Vindobona or Vienna.
