BLISTER

Cross-references: CANTHARIDES

BLISTER, in Medicine, a thin bladder containing a watery humour, whether occasioned by burns and the like accidents, or by vesicatories applied to different parts of the body for that purpose.—Cantharides, or Spanish flies, applied in the form of a plaster, are chiefly used with this intention. See CANTHARIDES.
