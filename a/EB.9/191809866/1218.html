BLEKING

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BLEKING, a territory in the south part of Sweden, having the Baltic sea on the south, Smaland on the north, and the province of Schonen on the west. Its principal towns are Christianstadt, Elleholm, Ahuys, Roterby, and Christianopole, which last is the capital.
