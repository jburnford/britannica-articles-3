BONARELLI

BONARELLI, Count Guid Ubaldo, an Italian poet, was the son of Count Pietro Bonarelli, minister of the duke of Urbino. He was entrusted with several important negotiations, and was esteemed an able politician and learned philosopher. He was the author of a fine Italian pastoral entitled, Filli di Seiro. He died at Fano, in 1608, aged 45.
