BENAVIDUS

article · 319 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BENAVIDUS, or BONAVITUS (Marcus Mantua), a celebrated civilian, taught civil law with reputation, during 60 years, at Padua the place of his birth; and died in 1582, aged 93. His principal works are, 1. Collectanea super Jus Cesareum. 2. Consiliorum, tom. ii. Problematum legalium. 4. De illustribus Jurisconsultis, &c.
