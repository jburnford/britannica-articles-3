BASSE

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BASSE, or BASS, a town of the French Netherlands, in the county of Flanders, on the confines of Artois, remarkable on account of the many sieges it has sustained; but its fortifications are now demolished. It is seated on a canal which runs as far as Deule. L. Long. 3. o. N. Lat. 50. 53.
