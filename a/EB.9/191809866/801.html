BETULEIUS

article · 392 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BETULEIUS, SIXTUS, an able grammarian, a good Latin poet and philosopher, born at Memmingen in the year 1500; his true name was Birck. He taught the belles lettres and philosophy with reputation; and became principal of the college of Augsburg, where he died on the 16th of June 1554. He published several works in prose; and his dramatic pieces of Joseph, Susannah, and Judith, are esteemed.
