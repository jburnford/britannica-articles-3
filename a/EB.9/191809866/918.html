BILANDER

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BILANDER, in Navigation, a small merchant ship with two masts, distinguished from other vessels of the same kind by the form of the main-sail. Few vessels

Bilbilis are now rigged in the manner of bilanders; the name has been variously applied in different countries.
