BEMBO, PETER

article · 411 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEMBO, PETER, a noble Venetian, secretary to Leo X. and afterwards cardinal, was one of the best writers of the 16th century. He was a good poet both in Italian and Latin; but he is justly censured for the looseness and immodesty of some of his poems. He published, besides these, A History of Venice; Letters; and a book in praise of the Duke and Duchess of Urbino. He died in 1547, in the 72d year of his age.
