Beati BENEDICTION

sub_entry · 166 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Beati BENEDICTION, (benedictio beatifica), is the viaticum given to dying persons. The pope begins all his bulls with this form: Salutem et apostolicam benedictionem.
