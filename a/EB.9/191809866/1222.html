BLEND Water

sub_entry · 399 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BLEND Water, called also morebough, a distemper incident to black cattle, comes either from the blood, from the yellows, or from the change of ground.—In order to cure it, take bole armoniac, and as much charcoal-dust as will fill an egg-shell, a good quantity of the inner bark of an oak, dried and pounded together to a powder, and give it to the beast in a quart of new milk and a pint of earing.
