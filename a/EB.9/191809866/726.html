BERRE

BERRE, a town of France, in the department of the Mouths of the Rhone, seated on a lake of the same name. It is remarkable for the quantity and goodness of the salt that is made there, but the air is very unwholesome. E. Long. 4. 32. N. Lat. 43. 32.
