BETHANY

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BETHANY, in Ancient Geography, a village at the foot of Mount Olivet, on the side, about two miles to the east of Jerusalem, (John, Jerome); where Lazarus dwelt and was raised from the dead; and where happened the ascension of our Saviour.
