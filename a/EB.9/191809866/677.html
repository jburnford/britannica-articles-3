BEREWICHA

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEREWICHA, or BEREWICA, in our old writers, denotes a village or hamlet belonging to some town or manor, situated at some distance therefrom.—The word frequently occurs in Doomsday-book: Iste sunt berewicha ejusdem manerii.
