BEVERAGE

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEVERAGE, in a general sense, signifies a drink: hence nectar is said to be the beverage of the gods. In writers of the middle age, beverage, beveragium, or biberagium, denotes money given to an artificer, or other person, to drink, over and above his hire or wages.
