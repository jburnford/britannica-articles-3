BEATS

article · 160 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: CLOCK

BEATS, in a watch or clock, are the strokes made by the fangs or pallets of the spindle of the balance, or of the pads in a royal pendulum. See CLOCK and WATCH.
