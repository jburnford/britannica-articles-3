BERCARIA

article · 300 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BERCARIA, BERQUERIA, or Berkeria, in middle-age writers, denotes a sheep-fold, sheep-cote, sheep-pen, or other enclosure, for the safe keeping a flock of sheep.—The word is abbreviated from berbucaria; of berbex, detorted from verrex. Hence also a shepherd was denominated berbicarius and berquarius.
