BONY

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BONY, among painters, as to bear a body, a term signifying that the colours are of such a nature, as to be capable of being ground so fine, and mixing with the oil so entirely, as to seem only a very thick oil of the same colour.
