BERAR

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BERAR, a province of Asia, in the dominions of the Great Mogul, near the kingdom of Bengal. It abounds in corn, rice, pulse, and poppies, from which last they extract opium; and sugar-canes grow almost without cultivation. The capital town is called Stoupour.
