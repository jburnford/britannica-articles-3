BONNEVILLE

BONNEVILLE, a town of Savoy, capital of Faucigny, situated on the north side of the river Arve, and subject to the king of Sardinia. It is situated at the foot of a mountain called the Mole, and is 20 miles south of Geneva. E. Long. 6. 10. N. Lat. 46. 18.
