BLOOD-BAND

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BLOOD-BAND is when a trespasser is apprehended in a forest with his hands or other parts bloody; which is a circumstance of his having killed the deer, though he be not found chasing or hunting them.
