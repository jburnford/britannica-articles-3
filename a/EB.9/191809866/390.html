BEAUCÉ

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BEAUCÉ, a late province of France, lying between the Isle of France, Blaisois, and Orleannois. It is very fertile in wheat, that it is called the Granary of Paris. Chartres is the principal town. It now forms the department of Eure and Loire.
