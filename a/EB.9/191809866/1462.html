BOND

article · 302 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOND, in masonry and brick-laying, is when bricks or stones are as it were knit and interwoven; and when they say, make good bond, they mean that the joints are not made over, or upon other joints; but reach at least six inches, both within the wall and on the surface, as the art of building requires.
