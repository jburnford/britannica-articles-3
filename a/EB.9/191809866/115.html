BASE, in Law. Base estate, such as base tenants have in their hands. Base tenure, the holding by villenage, or other customary services; as distinguished from the higher tenures in capite, or by military service. Base fee, is to hold in fee at the will of the lord, as distinguished from foggage tenure. Base court, any court not of record.
