BETIS

article · 352 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BETIS, governor of Gaza under Darius, famous for his valour and loyalty. He defended a place of consequence with a few men against Alexander, who was there shot through the shoulder. Betis thinking him slain, returned triumphantly to the city; but in a second assault he was wounded and brought to Alexander, who cruelly ordered him to be put to death.
