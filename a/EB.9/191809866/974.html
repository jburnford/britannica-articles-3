BION

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BION, furnamed Boryphenites, because he was of Boryphenes, was a philosopher of a great deal of wit, but of very little religion: he flourished about the 120th Olympiad; but, falling sick, he, like other profane persons, became superstitious.
