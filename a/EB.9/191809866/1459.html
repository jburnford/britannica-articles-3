BONAVISTA, an island in the Atlantic ocean, the most easterly and first discovered of the Cape de Verd islands. It is 20 miles long, and 13 broad; has plenty of goats and cotton, and some indigo. The inhabitants are remarkable for slothfulness: they have a town and two roads where ships come to an anchor. W. Long. 23. 6. N. Lat. 16. 5.
