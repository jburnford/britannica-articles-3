BOLSANE, a town of Germany, in the territory
of Tyrol, and circle of Austria. It is very agreeably
situated in the midst of a fine large valley, full of vil-
lages, and abounding in vineyards. The wines in this
valley are the best in all Tyrol; but they must be drunk
the year after that of their growth, otherwise, they be-
come unfit for use. E. Long. 11. 11. N. Lat. 46.
42.
