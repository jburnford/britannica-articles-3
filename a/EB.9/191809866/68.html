BARRIERS

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BARRIERS, signifies that which the French call jeu de barret, i. e. palafra; a martial exercise of men armed and fighting together with short swords, within certain bars or rails which separated them from the spectators: it is now disused in this country.
