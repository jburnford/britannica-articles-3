ORONOKO

article · 289 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ORONOKO, a large river of South America, which rises in Popayan, and enters the Atlantic ocean after a course of 755 leagues, in N. Lat. 9°. So great is its impetuosity that it stems the most powerful tides, and preserves the freshness of its waters to the distance of 36 miles out at sea.
