PARASCENIUM

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: THEATRE

PARASCENIUM, in the Grecian and Roman theatres, was a place behind the scenes whither the actors withdrew to dress and undress themselves. The Romans more frequently called it Poffecumum. See THEATRE.
