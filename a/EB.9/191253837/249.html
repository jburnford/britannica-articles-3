PADAN-ARAM

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PADAN-ARAM (Bible), literally the plains of Aram, or Syria; translated by the Seventy simply Mesopotamia, or Mesopotamia of Syria; by the Vulgate, Syrie; the Syrians on this and on the other side of the Euphrates, not differing remarkably from each other in language and manners, as Josephus allows.
