PALLENE

PALLENE, a small peninsula of Thrace or Macedonia, formerly called Phlegro. It is situated near the bay of Thermæ, and contains five cities, the principal of which is called Pallene. It was famous, according to some of the ancients, for an engagement between the gods and the giants.
