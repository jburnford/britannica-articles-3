PALICATE, a sea port town of India, on this side of the Ganges. It is seated on the coast of Coromandel, in the kingdom of Carnate, 70 miles north of Fort St George. Here the Dutch had a factory, and fort called the Fort of Guelderland. E. Long. 80. 1. N. Lat. 13. 34.
