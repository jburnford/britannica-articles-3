PALÆPAPHOS

PALÆPAPHOS (Strabo, Virgil, Pliny), a town of Cyprus, where stood a temple of Venus; and an adjoining town called Neo Paphos; where St Paul struck Elymas blind, and converted the proconsul Sergius Paulus.
