ORILLON

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: FORTIFICATION

ORILLON, in Fortification, is a small rounding of earth, faced with a wall; raised on the shoulder of those bastions that have casemates, to cover the cannon in the retired flank, and prevent their being dismounted by the enemy. See FORTIFICATION.
