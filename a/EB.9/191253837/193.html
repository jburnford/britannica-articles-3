OUNCE, a weight, the 16th part of a pound avoirdupois, and the 12th part of a pound troy. The word is derived from the Latin, uncia, "the twelfth part of any whole," called as; particularly in geometrical measures, an inch, or 12th part of a foot. See INCH and AS.
