PALLIUM

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALLIUM, in antiquity, an upper garment or mantle worn by the Greeks, as the toga was by the Romans. Each of these was so peculiar to the respective nations, that Palliatus is used to signify a Greek, and Togatus a Roman.
