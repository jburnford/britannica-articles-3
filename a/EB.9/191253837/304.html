PALE

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALE, a little pointed stake or piece of wood used in making enclosures, separations, &c. The pale was an instrument of punishment and execution among the ancient Romans, and still continues so among the Turks. Hence empaling, the passing a sharp pale up the fundament through the body.
