PALLET

article · 368 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALLET, among potters, crucible makers, &c. a wooden instrument, almost the only one they use, for forming, beating, and rounding their works. They have several kinds: the largest are oval, with a handle; others are round, or hollowed triangularly; others, in fine, are in manner of large knives, serving to cut off whatever is superfluous on the moulds of their work.
