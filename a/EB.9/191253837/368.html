PAMPHILUS

article · 332 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PAMPHILUS, a celebrated painter of Macedonia, in the age of Philip. He was founder of the school for painting at Sicyon; and he made a law which was observed not only in Sicyon but all over Greece, that none but children of noble and dignified persons should

be permitted to learn painting. Apelles was one of his Pamphylia, pupils.
