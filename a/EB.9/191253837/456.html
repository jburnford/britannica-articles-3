PARADOXI

Cross-references: MIMI

PARADOXI, a sort of mimes or buffoons among the ancients, who entertained the people with extempore effusions of drollery. They were also called Paradoxologi, Ordonarii, Neanicologi, and Aretologi. See MIMI.
