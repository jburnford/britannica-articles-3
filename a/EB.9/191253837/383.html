PANDA

article · 330 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PANDA, in Mythology, a goddess who was invoked and honoured as the protectress of travellers and navigators. The goddess of peace was also called Pandar, because she opened the gates of cities which were shut in time of war. According to Varro, Panda is a surname of Ceres, derived à pane dando, because she gave bread to mankind.
