OSENEY-ISLAND

article · 316 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OSENEY-ISLAND, in England, is formed by the river Isis, in the meadows near Oxford, where a magnificent abbey was erected, at the instigation of a cos-cubine of King Henry I. to atone for her sins; and the said king built a palace there, wherein King Richard I. was born, which Edward II. converted into a monastery.
