PALL

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PALL, in Heraldry, a figure like a Greek \gamma, about the breadth of a pallet; it is by some heralds called a cross pall, on account of its being looked upon as an archiepiscopal bearing.
