PALENCIA, a town of Spain, in the kingdom of Leon, with a rich archbishop's see. It had an university, but it was removed to Salamanca. It is seated in

a fertile soil, on the river Carion, on the frontiers of Castile, in W. Long. 3. 7. N. Lat. 42. 10. Palermo.
