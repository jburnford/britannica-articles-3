PARBUNCLE

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PARBUNCLE, in a ship, the name of a rope almost like a pair of flings: it is seized both ends together, and then put almost double about any heavy thing that is to be hoisted in or out of the ship; having the hook of the runner hitched into it, to hoist it up by.
