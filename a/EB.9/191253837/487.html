PARASELENE

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PARASELENE, in Natural Philosophy, a mock moon; a meteor or phenomenon encompassing or adjacent to the moon, in form of a luminous ring; wherein are observed sometimes one and sometimes two or more images of the moon.
