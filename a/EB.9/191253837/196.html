GREATER OUSE

sub_entry · 480 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GREATER OUSE, a river which rises near Fitzwell in Oxfordshire, and proceeds to Buckingham, Stony-Stratford, and Newport-Pagenel, in Buckinghamshire; from thence it proceeds to Bedford, and turning north-east it passes on to Huntingdon and Ely, till at length it arrives at Lynn-Regis in Norfolk, and falls into the sea. It is navigable to some distance above Downham, where there is a good harbour for barges; and a considerable trade is carried on by it to Lynn and other towns.
