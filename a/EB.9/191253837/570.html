LEGAL PART

sub_entry · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LEGAL PART, is a division for which we are indebted to the schoolmen. It refers to some universal as its whole; in which sense the species are parts of a genus, and individuals or singulars are parts of the species.
