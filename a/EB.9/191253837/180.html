OVER-RAKE

article · 179 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OVER-RAKE, among seamen: When a ship riding at anchor so overbeats herself into a high sea, that she is walked by the waves breaking in upon her, they say the waves over-rake her.
