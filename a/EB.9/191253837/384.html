PANDATARIA

PANDATARIA (Suetonius, Pliny, Strabo); PANDATERIA (Mela, Tacitus): An island in the Tuscan sea: a place of banishment for the more illustrious exiles. Hither Julia, the daughter of Augustus, was banished for her incontinence. To this island Tiberius banished Agrippina, his daughter-in-law (Suetonius.) It was the place of confinement of Octavia the daughter of Claudius, married to Nero; a fight that affected every eye (Tacitus). Now Santa Maria, situated between Pontia and Ichnia (Hollstenius).
