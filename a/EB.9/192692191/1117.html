DISQUISITION

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DISQUISITION (from dis, and quero, "I inquire"), an inquiry into the nature, kinds, and circumstances of any problem, question, or topic; in order to gain a right notion of it, and to discourse clearly about it.
