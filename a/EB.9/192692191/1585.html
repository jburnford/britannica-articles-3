DWINA, the name of two large rivers; one of which rises in Lithuania, and, dividing Livonia from Courland, falls into the Baltic sea a little below Riga: the other gives name to the province of Dwina in Russia.

Dyck It runs from south to north, and discharges itself into the White sea a little below Archangel.

Dyer. DYCK. See VANDYCK.
