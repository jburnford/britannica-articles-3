CYPHON

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CYPHON, in antiquity, a kind of punishment used by the Athenians. It was a collar made of wood; so called because it constrained the criminal who had this punishment inflicted on him to bow down his head.
