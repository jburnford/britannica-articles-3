CYGNUS, the Swan, in Astronomy, a constellation of the northern hemisphere, between Lyra and Cepheus. The stars in the constellation Cygnus, in Ptolemy's catalogue, are 19; in Tycho's 18; in Hevelius's 47: in the Britannic catalogue 81.
