CUDDY

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CUDDY, in a first rate man of war, is a place lying between the captain-lieutenant's cabin and the quarter-deck; and divided into partitions for the master and other officers. It denotes also a kind of cabin near the stern of a lighter or barge of burden.
