DRIFT-SAIL

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRIFT-SAIL, a sail used under water, veered out right a-head by sheets, as other sails are. It serves to keep the ship's head right upon the sea in a storm, and to hinder her from driving too fast in a current.
