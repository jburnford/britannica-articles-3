DISTICH,

(A) We have retained these observations, as an instance of the speculations and opinions concerning these subjects before the discovery of the present theory of Chemistry with regard to the oxidation of metals and the decomposition of water.

1 DISTICH, a couplet of verses making a complete sense. Thus hexameter and pentameter verses are disposed in distichs. There are excellent morals in Cato's distichs.
