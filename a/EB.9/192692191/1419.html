DREPANUM, -is; or Drepana, -orum; a town and port on the west side of Sicily, and to the west of Mount Eryx: Drepanitani the people. Now Trepamo, a city and port town on the westmost point of Sicily. E. Long. 12. 8. N. Lat. 38. 0.
