DESIGNATION

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DESIGNATION, the act of marking or indicating, and making a thing known. The designation of such an estate is made by the tenants, butments, and boundings. Among the Romans, there were designations of the consuls and other magistrates, some time before their election.
