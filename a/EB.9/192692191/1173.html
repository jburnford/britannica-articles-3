DIVIDEND, in Arithmetic, the number proposed to be divided into equal parts. See ARITHMETIC, No 14.

Dividend of Stocks, is a share or proportion of the interest of stocks erected on the public funds, as the South sea, &c. divided among and paid to the adventurers half yearly.
