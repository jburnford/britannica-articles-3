DIAPEDESIS

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIAPEDESIS, in Medicine, a transudation of the fluids through the sides of the vessels that contain them, occasioned by the blood's becoming too much attenuated, or the pores becoming too patent.
