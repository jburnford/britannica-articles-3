DUSKY BAY

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DUSKY BAY, a bay of the island of New Zealand in the Southern Pacific ocean. The country around is mountainous, and the hills near the sea side are covered with thick forests. It is in S. Lat. 45. 47. and in E. Long. 166. 18.
