DICHOTOMY

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DICHOTOMY, a term used by astronomers for that phasis or appearance of the moon, wherein she is bisected, or shows just half her disk. In this situation the moon is said to be in a quadrate aspect, or to be in her quadrate.
