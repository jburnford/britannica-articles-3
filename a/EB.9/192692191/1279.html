DONATUS, Ælius

sub_entry · 163 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DONATUS, Ælius, a famous grammarian, lived at Rome in 354. He was one of St. Jerome's masters; and composed commentaries on Terence and Virgil, which are esteemed.
