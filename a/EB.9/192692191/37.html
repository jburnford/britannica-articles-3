CUJAVIA

article · 320 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CUJAVIA, a territory of Great Poland, having on the north the duchy of Prussia, on the west the palatinate of Kalisk, and on the south those of Liscici and Rava, and on the west that of Ploczko. It contains two palatinates, the chief towns of which are Inowloez and Brest; as also Uladislaw, the capital of the district.
