DISSIDENTS

article · 315 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DISSIDENTS, a denomination applied in Poland to those of the Lutheran, Calvinistic, and Greek profession. The king of Poland engages by the pacia conventa to tolerate them in the free exercise of their religion, but they have often had reason to complain of the violation of these promises. See (History of) POLAND.
