DESPOUILLE

article · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DESPOUILLE, in Heraldry, the whole case, skin, or slough of a beast, with the head, feet, tail, and all appendages, so that being filled and stuffed it looks like the entire creature.
