DISMISSION of a BILL, in Chancery. If the plaintiff does not attend on the day fixed for the hearing, his bill is dismissed with costs. It may be also dismissed for want of prosecution, which is in the nature of a non suit at law, if he suffers three terms to elapse without moving forward in the cause.
