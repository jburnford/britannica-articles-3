Cross-references: ACOUSTICS

DIACOUSTICS, called also DIAPHONICS, the consideration of the properties of refracted sound, as it passes through different mediums. The word is formed from the Greek δια, per, "through," which intimates a passage; and ακουω, "I hear," q. d. the consideration of the passage of the sounds we hear. See ACOUSTICS.
