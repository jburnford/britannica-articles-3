DISPAUPER

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DISPAUPER. A person suing in forma pauperis, is said to be dispaupered, if, before the suit is ended, he has any lands or other estate fallen to him, or if he has any thing to make him lose his privilege. See the article Forma Pauperis.
