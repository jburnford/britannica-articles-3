DAGHESTAN

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DAGHESTAN, a country of Asia, bounded by Circassia on the north, by the Caspian sea on the east, by Shirvan a province of Persia on the south, and by Georgia on the west. Its chief towns are Tarku and Derbent, both situated on the Caspian sea.
