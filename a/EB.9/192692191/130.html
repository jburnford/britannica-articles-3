CURTEYN

article · 211 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CURTEYN, (Curtana), was the name of Edward the Confessor's sword, which is the first sword carried before the kings of England at their coronation; and it is said the point of it is broken as an emblem of mercy.
