DRENCH

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRENCH, among farriers, a physical potion for horses. The ingredient for this purpose are to be beat coarsely, and either mingled with a decoction or with wine. Then let all infuse about a quarter of an hour, and give it to the horse with a horn after he has been tied up two hours to the rack.
