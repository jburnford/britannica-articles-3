DERIVATION

article · 231 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DERIVATION, in Medicine, is when a humour which cannot conveniently be evacuated at the part affected, is attracted from thence, and discharged elsewhere; thus a blister is applied to the neck to draw away the humour from the eyes.
