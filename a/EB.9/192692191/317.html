DALMATIA, a province of Europe, bounded on the

Dalrymple, the north by Bosnia, on the south by the gulf of Venice, on the east by Servia, and on the west by Morlachia. Spalatto is the capital of that part belonging to the Venetians; and Ragusa, of a republic of that name; the Turks have a third, whose capital is Herzegovina. The air is wholesome, and the soil fruitful; and it abounds in wine, corn, and oil.
