DIANIUM

article · 296 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIANIUM, in Ancient Geography, a town of the Castellani, in the Hither Spain; famous for a temple of Diana, whence the name: now Denia, a small town of Valencia, on the Mediterranean. Also a promontory near Dissium: now El Cabo Martin, four leagues from Denia, running out into the Mediterranean.
