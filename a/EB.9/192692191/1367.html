DRACO, in Astronomy, a constellation of the northern hemisphere; whose stars, according to Ptolemy, are 81; according to Tycho, 32; according to Hevelius, 40; according to Bayer, 33; and according to Mr Flamsteed, 80. See ASTRONOMY, No 406.
