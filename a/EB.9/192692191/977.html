DILATORY PLEAS

Cross-references: PLEA

DILATORY PLEAS, in Law, are such as are put in merely for delay; and there may be a demurrer to a dilatory plea, or the defendant shall be ordered to plead better, &c. The truth of dilatory pleas is to be made out by affidavit of the fact, &c. by stat. 4 and 5 Anne. See PLEA.
