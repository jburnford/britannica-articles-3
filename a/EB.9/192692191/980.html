DILIGENCE

article · 400 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: LAW · ANETHUM

DILIGENCE, in Scotts Law, signifies either that care and attention which parties are bound to give, in implementing certain contracts or trusts, and which varies according to the nature of the contract; or it signifies certain forms of law, whereby the creditor endeavours to operate his payment, either by affecting the person or estate of the debtor.
