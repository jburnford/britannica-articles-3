CYNOSURA, Cynosura, or Cynosuris, in Ancient Geography, a place in Laconia; but whether maritime or inland, uncertain. Here Asclepius, being thunderstruck, was buried (Cicero).

CYNOSURA was also the name of the promontory of Marathon in Attica, opposite to Eubœa.
