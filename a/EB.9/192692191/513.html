DECREMENT

article · 272 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DECREMENT, in Heraldry, signifies the wane of the moon from the full to the new. The moon in this state is called moon decreescent, or in decuris; and when borne in coat armour, faces to the left side of the escutcheon, as she does to the right side when in the increment.
