DEPHLOGISTICATED AIR

article · 312 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: OXYGEN

DEPHLOGISTICATED AIR, is the same with oxygen gas of modern chemistry, and is an invisible elastic fluid, of somewhat greater specific gravity than that of the common atmosphere, and capable of supporting animal life and flame for a much longer time than the air we commonly breathe.
