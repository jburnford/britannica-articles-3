DIÆRESIS

article · 168 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIÆRESIS, in Grammar, the division of one syllable into two, which is usually noted by two points over a letter, as aulai, instead of aula, disfoluenda for disfoluenda.
