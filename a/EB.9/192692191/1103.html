DISPENSARY, or Dispensatory

sub_entry · 463 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DISPENSARY, or Dispensatory, is likewise a magazine or office for selling medicines at prime cost to the poor. The College of Physicians maintain three of these in London; one at the college itself in Warwick-lane; another in St Peter's alley, Cornhill; and a third in St Martin's lane. Dispensaries have also been established in several of the principal towns in Scotland and England; particularly in Edinburgh, Dundee, and Kello; as also at Newcastle upon Tyne.
