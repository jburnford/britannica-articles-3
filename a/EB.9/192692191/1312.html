DOSE

article · 182 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOSE, in Pharmacy, &c. the quantity of a medicine to be taken at one time. The word is formed from the Greek δosis, which signifies gift, or a thing given; from διδωμι, do. "I give."
