DITRIHEDRIA

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DITRIHEDRIA, in Mineralogy, spars with twice three sides, or six planes; being formed of two trigonal pyramids joined base to base, without any intermediate column.

The species of ditrihedria are distinguished by the different figures of these pyramids.
