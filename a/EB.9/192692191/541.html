DEFENCE

article · 310 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: FORTIFICATION

DEFENCE, in Fortification, all sorts of works that cover and defend the opposite posts, as flanks, casements, parapets, and faussebrays. See FORTIFICATION.

Line of DEFENCE, a supposed line drawn from the angle of the curtain, or from any other part in the curtain, to the flanked angle of the opposite bastion.
