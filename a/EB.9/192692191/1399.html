DRAPERY

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: PAINTING

DRAPERY, in Sculpture and Painting, signifies the representation of the clothing of human figures, and also hangings, tapestry, curtains, and most other things that are not carnations or landscapes. See PAINTING, CRAYON, DRAWING, and MINIATURE.
