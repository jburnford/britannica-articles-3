DEPRECATION

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEPRECATION, in Rhetoric, a figure whereby the orator invokes the aid and assistance of some one;

Deprecatory or prayers for some great evil or punishment to befall him who speaks falsely, either himself or his adversary.
