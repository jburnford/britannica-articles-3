DERTONA, DERTON, or Deribon, in Ancient Geography, a colony of the Cispadana; called Julia Augusta on inscriptions and coins; midway between Genoa and Piacentia, and situated to the east of the Tanarus in Liguria. Now Tortona, a city of Milan. E. Long. 9. 12. N. Lat. 45.
