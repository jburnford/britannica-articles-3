DESCENSION

article · 456 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DESCENSION, in Astronomy, is either right or oblique.

Right Descension, is an arch of the equinoctial, intercepted between the next equinoctial point and the intersection of the meridian, passing through the centre of the object, at its setting, in a right sphere.

Oblique Descension, an arch of the equinoctial, intercepted between the next equinoctial point and the horizon, passing through the centre of the object, at its setting, in an oblique sphere.
