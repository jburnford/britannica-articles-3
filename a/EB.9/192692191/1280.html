DONAWERT

article · 275 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DONAWERT, a strong town of Germany, in the circle of Bavaria, on the frontiers of Swabia. It has been taken and retaken several times in the wars of Germany; and was formerly an imperial city, but at present is subject to the duke of Bavaria. E. Long. 10. 32. N. Lat. 48. 32.
