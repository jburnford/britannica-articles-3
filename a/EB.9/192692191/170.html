CUTTING

article · 467 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CUTTING is particularly used in heraldry, where the shield is divided into two equal parts, from right to left, parallel to the horizon, or in the sesto-way.

The word also is applied to the honourable ordinaries, and even to animals and moveables, when they are divided equally the same way; so, however, as that one

Cutting. one moiety is colour, the other metal. The ordinaries are said to be cut, coupé, when they do not come full to the extremities of the shield.
