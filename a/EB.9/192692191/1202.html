DOCTOR

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOCTOR, (Διδάσκαλος), in the Greek church, is a particular officer, appointed to interpret part of the scriptures. He who interprets the gospels, is called doctor of the Gospels; he who interprets St. Paul's Epistles, doctor of the Apostles; he who interprets the Psalms, doctor of the Psalter.
