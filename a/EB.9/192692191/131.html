CURTIN

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CURTIN, CURTAIN, or Courtin, in Fortification, is that part of the rampart of a place which is betwixt the flanks of two bastions, bordered with a parapet five feet high, behind which the soldiers stand to fire upon the covered way and into the moat.
