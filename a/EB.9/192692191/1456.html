DRUM, or Drummer, he that beats the drum; of whom each company of foot has one, and sometimes two. Every regiment has a drum major, who has the command over the other drums. They are distinguished from the soldiers by clothes of a different fashion: their post, when a battalion is drawn up, is on the flanks, and on a march it is betwixt the divisions.

DRUM of the Ear, the same with the tympanum. See ANATOMY INDEX.
