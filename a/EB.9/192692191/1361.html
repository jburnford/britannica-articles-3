DRABS

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRABS, in the salt works, a kind of wooden boxes for holding the salt when taken out of the boiling pan; the bottoms of which are made shelving or inclining forwards, that the briny moisture of the salt may drain off.
