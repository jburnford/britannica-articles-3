DARDANUM PROMONTORIUM

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DARDANUM PROMONTORIUM, (Pliny); Dardanis, (Strabo); a promontory of Troas, near Abydos, running out into the Hellefont; with a cognominal town at it, called also Dardanus, and Dardanum; all which gave name to the Dardanelles.
