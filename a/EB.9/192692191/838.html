DIAL

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIAL, an instrument serving to measure time, which, if effected by the aid of the sun, is called a sun-dial. The word is from the Latin dies, "day," because indicating the hour of the day. The ancients also called it sciatberium, from its effect by the shadow. See the article DIALLING.
