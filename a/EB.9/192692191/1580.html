DUUMVIRI Navales

sub_entry · 296 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DUUMVIRI Navales, were the commissaries of the fleet, first created at the request of M. Decius, tribune of the people, in the time of the war with the Samnites. The duty of their office consisted in giving orders for the fitting of ships, and giving their commissions to the marine officers, &c.
