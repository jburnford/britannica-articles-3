DIFFORM

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIFFORM, Difformis, (from forma, "a shape"), is a word used in opposition to uniform; and signifies, that there is no regularity in the form or appearance of a thing. The botanists use it as a distinction of the flowers of several species of plants.
