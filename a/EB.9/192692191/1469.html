DUCA

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DUCA, a learned Greek, who wrote a history of what passed under the last emperors of Constantinople, till the ruin of that city. This work, which is esteemed, was printed at the Louvre in 1649, with the Latin translation and notes of Boillaud.
