DIONYSIUS the Lesser

sub_entry · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DIONYSIUS the Lesser, a Scythian, became abbot of a monastery at Rome; he was the first who computed time from the birth of Dionysius to Christ, and fixed that great event, according to the vulgar era. He was also a learned canon law writer, and died about the year 540.
