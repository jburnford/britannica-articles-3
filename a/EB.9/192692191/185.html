CYBELLICUM MARMOR

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CYBELLICUM MARMOR, a name given by the ancients to a species of marble dug in a mountain of that name in Phrygia. It was of an extremely bright white, with broad veins of bluish black.
