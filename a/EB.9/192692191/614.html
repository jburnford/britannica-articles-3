DEMADES

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEMADES, a famous Athenian, who from being a mariner, became a great orator, and appeased Philip by his eloquence, after the famous victory over the Athenians at Cheronea, in the 338th year B. C.
