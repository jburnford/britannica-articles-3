DERVENTIO

DERVENTIO, in Ancient Geography, a river of the Brigantes in Britain. Now the Darwent, in the east of Yorkshire, falling into the Ouse. Also a town of the Brigantes on the same river. Now called Aulaby, seven miles from York, to the north-east (Camden).
