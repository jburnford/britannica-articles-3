DALTON, a town of Lancashire, in England. It is seated on the spring-head of a river, in a champaign country, not far from the sea; and the ancient castle is made use of to keep the records, and prisoners for debt in the liberty of Furness. W. Long. 3. o. N. Lat. 54. 18.
