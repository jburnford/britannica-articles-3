CYNTHUS

article · 229 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CYNTHUS, in Ancient Geography, a mountain of the island Delos, so high as to overshadow the whole island. On this mountain Latona brought forth Apollo and Diana: hence the epithet Cynthius (Virgil), and Cynthia, (Lucan, Statius).
