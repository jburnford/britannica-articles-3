DENARIUS

article · 177 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DENARIUS, in Roman antiquity, the chief silver coin among the Romans, worth in our money about sevenpence three farthings. As a weight, it was the seventh part of a Roman ounce.
