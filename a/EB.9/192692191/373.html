DAPHNE

DAPHNE, in Ancient Geography, a small district on the lake Samachonitis, in the Higher Galilee, very pleasant, and plentifully watered with springs, which feed the Lefi Jordan; whence its name seems to arise, probably in imitation of that near Antioch of Syria on the river Orontes.
