DRACHM

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DRACHM, a Grecian coin, of the value of seven-pence three farthings. Drachm is also a weight used by our physicians; containing just sixty grains three scruples, or the eighth part of an ounce.
