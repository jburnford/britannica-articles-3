DESCENT

Cross-references: GRAVITY

DESCENT, in general, is the tendency of a body from a higher to a lower place; thus all bodies, unless otherwise determined by a force superior to their gravity, descend towards the centre of the earth. See GRAVITY and MECHANICS.
