DOSSER

article · 235 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DOSSER, a sort of basket to be carried on the shoulders of men. It is used in carrying the overplus earth from one part of a fortification to another where it is wanted. There are likewise small carts and wheelbarrows for the same use.
