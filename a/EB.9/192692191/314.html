DALEM

DALEM, a town of the united provinces of Holland, and capital of a district of the same name. It was taken by the French in 1672, who demolished the fortifications. It is seated on the river Bervine, five miles north-east of Liege. E. Long. 5. 59. N. Lat. 50. 40.
