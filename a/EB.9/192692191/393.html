DARIORIGUM, in Ancient Geography, a town of the Veneti in Gallia Celtica; called in the Notitia Lugdunenfis, Civitas Venetum, after the manner of the lower age. Now Vannes, or Vennes, in Brittany. W. Long. 2. 37. Lat. 47. 40.
