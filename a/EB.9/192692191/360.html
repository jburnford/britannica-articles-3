DANDOLO

article · 182 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DANDOLO, HENRY, doge of Venice, a brave admiral and politician. With a Venetian fleet he took Constantinople in 1203, and had the moderation to refuse to be emperor. He died in 1250.
