DUMOSÆ

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DUMOSÆ (from dymur, "a bush"), an order of plants in the Fragmenta methodi naturalis of Linnaeus, containing the following genera, viz. Viburnum, Tinus, Opulus, Sambucus, Rondeletia, Bellonia, Cassia, Ilex, Tomax, &c.
