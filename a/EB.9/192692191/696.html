DEPRESSION of the POLE

sub_entry · 284 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

DEPRESSION of the POLE. When a person fails or travels towards the equator, he is said to depress the pole; because as many degrees as he approaches nearer the equator, so many degrees will the pole be nearer the horizon. This phenomenon arises from the spherical figure of the earth.
