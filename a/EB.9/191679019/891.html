ISMARUS

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ISMARUS, in Ancient Geography, a town of the Cicones in Thrace, giving name to a lake. In Virgil it is called Ismara. Servius supposed it to be a mountain of Thrace; on which mountain Orpheus dwelt.
