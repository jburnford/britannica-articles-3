IMPOSTHUME

article · 269 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IMPOSTHUME, or abscess, a collection of matter or pus in any part of the body, either owing to an obstruction of the fluids in that part, which makes them change into such matter, or to a translation of it from some other part where it was generated. See Surgery Index.
