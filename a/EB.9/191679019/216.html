ICHNOGRAPHY

article · 373 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ICHNOGRAPHY, in Architecture, is a transverse or horizontal section of a building, exhibiting the plot of the whole edifice, and of the several rooms and apartments in any story; together with the thickness of the walls and partitions; the dimensions of the doors, windows, and chimneys; the projections of the columns and piers, with every thing visible in such a section.
