INTERCATIA

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INTERCATIA, in Ancient Geography, a town of the Vaccei in the Hither Spain. Here Scipio Æmilius slew a champion of the barbarians in single combat; and was the first who mounted the wall in taking the town. It was situated to the south-east of Asturia; now said to be in ruins.
