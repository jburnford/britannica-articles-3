INCORRUPTIBLES, INCORRUPTIBLES, the name of a sect which sprang out of the Eutychians.—Their distinguishing tenet was, that the body of Jesus Christ was incorruptible; by which they meant, that after and from the time wherein he was formed in the womb of his holy mother, he was not susceptible of any change or alteration; not even of any natural and innocent passions, as of hunger, thirst, &c. so that he ate
