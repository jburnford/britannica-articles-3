JUAN DE PORTO RICO

JUAN DE PORTO RICO, the capital town of the island of Porto Rico, with a good harbour defended by several forts, and a bishop's see. It is seated on the north coast of the island, in W. Long. 65. 35. N. Lat. 18. 39.
