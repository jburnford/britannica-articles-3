JACOBITES

article · 320 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JACOBITES, a term of reproach bestowed on the persons who, vindicating the doctrines of passive obedience and non-resistance with respect to the arbitrary proceedings of princes, disavow the revolution in 1688, and assert the supposed rights, and adhere to the interests, of the late abdicated King James and his family.
