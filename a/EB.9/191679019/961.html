JUDOIGNE

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JUDOIGNE, a town of the Austrian Netherlands, in Brabant. Near this town the duke of Marlborough gained that signal victory over the French in 1706, called the battle of Ramillies. It is seated on the river Gete, 13 miles south-east of Louvain; and 16 north of Namur.
