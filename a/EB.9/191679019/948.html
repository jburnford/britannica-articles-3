JUDENBURG

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JUDENBURG, a handsome and considerable town of Germany, in the circle of Austria, and capital of Upper Syria, with a handsome castle; the public buildings with the square are very magnificent. It is seated on the river Meur. E. Long. 15. 20. N. Lat. 47. 20.
