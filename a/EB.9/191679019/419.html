IMPROMPTU

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IMPROMPTU, or INTROMPTU, a Latin word frequently used among the French, and sometimes in English, to signify a piece made off-hand, or extempore, without any previous meditation, by mere force and vivacity of imagination.
