IGIS, a town of the country of the Grisons, in
Cadder, with a magnificent castle, in which is a cabin-
et of curiosities, and a handsome library; 23 miles
south-west of Chora, and 23 south of Glaris. E. Long.
9. o. N. Lat. 49. 10.
