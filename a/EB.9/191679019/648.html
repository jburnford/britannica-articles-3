INSTALMENT

article · 266 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INSTALMENT, a settling or installing any person in a dignity. The word is derived from the Latin in, and stallum, a term used for a seat in church, in the choir, or a seat or bench in a court of justice, &c. Though Vossius is of opinion the word is of German origin.
