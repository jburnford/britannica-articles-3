INTERSCENDENT

article · 173 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INTERSCENDENT, in Algebra, is applied to quantities, when the exponents of their powers are radical quantities. Thus, x\sqrt{1}, x\sqrt{2}, &c. are interscendent quantities.
