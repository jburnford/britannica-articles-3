JONATHAN MACCABEUS

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JONATHAN MACCABEUS, brother of Judas, a renowned general of the Jews. He forced Baechides the Syrian general, who made war with the Jews, to accept a peace; conquered Demetrius Soter, and afterwards Apollonius, that prince's general; but, being ensnared by Tryphon, was put to death 144 B. C.
