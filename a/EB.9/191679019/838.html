IRON-Sick

Cross-references: SIDEROXYLUM · SIDERITIS

IRON-Sick, in the sea-language, is said of a ship or boat,

Iron-wood boat, when her bolts or nails are so eaten with rust, and so worn away, that they occasion hollows in the planks, whereby the vessel is rendered leaky.

Irritability Iron-Wood, in Botany. See SIDEROXYLUM, BOTANY Index.

Iron-Wort, in Botany. See SIDERITIS, BOTANY Index.
