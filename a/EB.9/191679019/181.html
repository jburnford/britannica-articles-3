JARIMUTH, JARMUTH, or Jerimoth, Josh. xv. a town reckoned to the tribe of Judah, four miles from Eleutheropolis, westward, (Jerome.) Thought to be the same with Ramoth and Remeth, Joshua xix. and Nehem. x. 2. (Reland).
