ISSOUDUN

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ISSOUDUN, a town of France, in Berry, which carries on a trade in wood, cattle, cloth, hats, and stockings; is seated partly on a plain, and partly on an eminence. E. Long. 2. 5. N. Lat. 46. 57.
