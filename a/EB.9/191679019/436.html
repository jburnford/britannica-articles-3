INCAPACITY

article · 275 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INCAPACITY, in the canon-law, is of two kinds: 1. The want of a dispensation for age in a minor, for legitimation in a bastard, and the like: this renders the provision of a benefice void in its original. 2. Crimes and heinous offences, which annul provisions at first valid.
