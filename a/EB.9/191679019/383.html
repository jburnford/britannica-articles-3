IMPEDIMENTS

article · 370 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IMPEDIMENTS, in Law, are such hinderances as put a stop or stay to a person's seeking for his right by a due course of law. Persons under impediments are those under age or coverture, non compos mentis, in prison, beyond sea, &c. who, by a saving in our laws, have time to claim and prosecute their rights, after the impediments are removed, in case of fines levied, &c.
