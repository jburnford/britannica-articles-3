HYPOTRACHELION

article · 302 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYPOTRACHELION, in Architecture, is used for a little frieze in the Tuscan and Doric capital, between the astragal and annulets; called also the colerin and

gorgeron. The word is applied by some authors in a more general sense, to the neck of any column, or that part of its capital below the astragal.
