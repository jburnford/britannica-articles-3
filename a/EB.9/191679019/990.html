IULUS

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

IULUS, a son of Acanthus, born in Lavinium. In the succession to the kingdom of Alba, Aeneas Sylvius, the son of Aeneas and Lavinia, was preferred to him. He was, however, made chief priest.
