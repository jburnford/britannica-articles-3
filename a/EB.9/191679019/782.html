JOINVILLE

article · 204 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JOINVILLE, an ancient and considerable town of France, in Champagne, with the title of a principality, and a large magnificent castle. It is situated on the river Marne, in E. Long. 5. 10. N. Lat. 48. 20.
