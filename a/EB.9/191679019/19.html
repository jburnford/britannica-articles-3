HYGIEINE, ὑγιεινὴ (formed of ὑγις, "found, healthy"), that branch of medicine which considers health, and discovers proper means and remedies, with their use, in the preservation of that state.

The objects of this branch of medicine are, the non-naturals. See DIET, EXERCISE, &c.
