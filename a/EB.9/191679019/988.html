JULIUS VICUS, in Ancient Geography, a town of the Nemetes in Gallia Belgica; situated between the Tres Tabernae and Noviomagus. Now Germerheim, a town of the Lower Palatinate, on the west side of the Rhine. E. Long. 8. 15. N. Lat. 49. 12.
