IRIS, in Physiology, the rainbow. The word is Greek, ἰρίς, supposed by some to be derived from ἰρύ "I speak, I tell;" as being a meteor that is supposed to foretell, or rather to declare rain. See RAINBOW.

Lunar Iris, or Moon-rainbow. See RAINBOW, Lunar.
