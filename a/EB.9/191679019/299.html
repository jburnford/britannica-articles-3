IGLAW, a considerable and populous town of
Germany, in Moravia, where they have a manufactory
of good cloth, and excellent beer. It is seated on the

river Iglaw, 40 miles west of Brin, and 62 south-east of
Prague. E. Long. 15. 42. N. Lat. 47. 8.
