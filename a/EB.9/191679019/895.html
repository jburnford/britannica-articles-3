ISOCHRONAL

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ISOCHRONAL, is applied to such vibrations of a pendulum as are performed in the same space of time; as all the vibrations or swings of the same pendulum are, whether the arches it describes are shorter or longer.
