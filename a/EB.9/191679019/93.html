JABBOK

article · 365 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JABBOK, a brook on the other side of the Jordan, the spring whereof is in the mountains of Gilead. It falls into Jordan pretty near the sea of Tiberias, to the south of this sea. Near this brook the patriarch Jacob wrestled with the angel (Gen. xxxii. 22). The Jabbok separated the land of the Ammonites from the Golanites, and the territories of Og king of Bashan.
