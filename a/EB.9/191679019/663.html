INSUBRIUM AGER

INSUBRIUM AGER, in Ancient Geography, a district of the Transpadana; situated between the Ticinus to the west, the Addua to the east, the Padus to the south, and Orobii to the north. The people called Insubres by Livy, Insubri by Ptolemy, and Isambres by Strabo. Now the duchy of Milan.
