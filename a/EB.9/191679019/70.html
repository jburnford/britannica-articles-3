HYPOGÆUM

article · 154 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYPOGÆUM, ὑπογῶνιον, in Astrology, is a name given to the celestial houses which are below the horizon: and especially the imum cali, or bottom of heaven.
