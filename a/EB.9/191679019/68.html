HYPOCRISY

article · 300 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HYPOCRISY, ὑποκρισία, in Ethics, denotes dissimulation with regard to the moral or religious character. In other words, it signifies one who feigns to be what he is not; and is generally applied to those who assume the appearances of virtue or religion, without having any thing in reality of either.
