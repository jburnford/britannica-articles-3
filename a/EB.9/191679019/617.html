INNOCENT'S DAY

article · 340 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

INNOCENT'S DAY, a festival of the Christian church, observed on December 28. in memory of the massacre of the innocent children by the command of Herod king of Judea. See Jews Christ; and Jews, No 24 par. ult. The Greek church in their kalendar, and the Abyssinians of Ethiopia in their offices, commemorate 14,000 infants on this occasion.
