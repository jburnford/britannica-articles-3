MESENTERY, MESENTERIUM, (formed of mesos, middle, and enteros, intestine), in anatomy, a fatty membranous body, thus called as being placed in the middle of the intestines, which it connects to one another. See ANATOMY, No 94.

MESHES of NETS, the openings or interstices between the threads.
