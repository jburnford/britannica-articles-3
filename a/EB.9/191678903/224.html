MESSUAGE

article · 316 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MESSUAGE, MESSUAGIUM, in Late, a dwelling-house, with some land adjoining assigned for its use. By the name of messuage may a garden, shop, mill, cottage, chamber, cellar, or the like, pass. In Scotland, messuage denotes what is called in England the manor-house, viz. the principal dwelling-house within any barony.
