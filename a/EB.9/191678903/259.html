METRICAL VERSES

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

METRICAL VERSES, are those consisting of a determinate number of long and short syllables; as those of the Greek and Latin poets.—Capellus observes, that the genius of the Hebrew language is incompatible with metrical poetry.
