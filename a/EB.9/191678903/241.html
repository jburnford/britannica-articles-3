METATHESIS

article · 369 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

METATHESIS, in grammar, a species of the metaplasmus; being a figure whereby the letters or syllables of a word are transposed, or shifted out of their usual situations, as pifiris for pifiris, Lybia for Libya, &c.

This word is, by physicians, used with respect to morbid causes, which when they cannot be evacuated, are removed to places where they are less injurious.
