MELANTERIA

MELANTERIA, an old term in Natural History, which seems to have been applied to copper pyrites.

The Greeks used it externally as a gentle escharotic and a flyptic, as an ingredient in their ointments for old ulcers, and also to sprinkle in the form of powder on fresh wounds in order to stop the haemorrhage.
