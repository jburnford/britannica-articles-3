MERIDEN, or MIREDEN, a town of Warwickshire, 97 miles from London on the London road, near Coventry. It is pleasantly situated, though in a wet clayey situation, and is not ill built. The church stands on an elevated spot, and contains some good monuments. There is an inn here, about half way from

from St. Clement's forest to Coventry, one of the finest in this part of England, being built like a nobleman's seat.
