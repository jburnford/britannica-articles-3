MEDIUM

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MEDIUM, in Logic, the mean or middle term of a syllogism, being an argument, reason, or consideration, for which we affirm or deny any thing; or, it is the cause why the greater extreme is affirmed or denied of the less in the conclusion.
