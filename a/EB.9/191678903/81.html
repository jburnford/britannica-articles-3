MELISSA

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MELISSA, in fabulous history, a daughter of Melissus king of Crete, who with her sister Amalthaea fed Jupiter with the milk of goats. She first found out the means of collecting honey; whence it has been fabled that she was changed into a bee, as her name is the Greek word for that insect.
