MERA-DE-ASTA

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MERA-DE-ASTA, formerly a large town of Andalucía, seated on the river Guadaleta, between Arcos and Xeres de la Frontera; but now only a large heap of ruins. Here the Arabs conquered Roderick the last king of the Goths, and by that victory became masters of Spain in 713.
