MELANIPIDES

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MELANIPIDES, in fabulous history, a Greek poet about 520 years before Christ. His grandson of the same name flourished about 60 years after at the court of Perdiccas the Second, of Macedonia. Some fragments of their poetry are still extant.
