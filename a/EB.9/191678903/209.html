MESOLOGARITHMS

article · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MESOLOGARITHMS, according to Kepler, are the logarithms of the co-fines and co-tangents; the former of which were called by Baron Napier anti-logarithms, and the latter differentials.
