MELPOMENE

article · 398 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

M E L P O M E N E, in Fabulous History, one of the muses, daughter of Jupiter and Mnetoynne. She presided over tragedy. Horace has addressed the finest of his odes to her, as to the patroness of lyric poetry. She was generally represented as a young woman with a serious countenance. Her garments were splendid; she wore a buskin, and held a dagger in one hand and in the other a sceptre and crown.
