Geometrical MEDIUM

sub_entry · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Geometrical MEDIUM, called in the schools medium persone, is that where the same ratio is preserved between the first and second as between the second and third terms; or that which exceeds in the same ratio or quota of itself, as it is exceeded: thus 6 is a geometrical medium between 4 and 9.
