MERIDIONAL DISTANCE

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MERIDIONAL DISTANCE, in Navigation, the same with departure, or easting and weeling; being the difference of longitude between the meridian under which the ship now is, and any other meridian which she was under before.
