ECCOPROTICS

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ECCOPROTICS, in Medicine, laxative or loosening remedies, which purge gently, by softening the humours and excrements, and fitting them for expulsion.—The word is composed of the Greek particle ex, and xerxes, excrement.
