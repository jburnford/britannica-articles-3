EJECTION

article · 343 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EJECTION, in Scots Law, is the turning out the possessor of any heritable subject by force; and is either legal or illegal. Legal ejection is where a person having no title to possess, is turned out by the authority of law. Illegal ejection is one person's violently turn-

ing another out of possession without lawful authority. Ejection, Ekron.
