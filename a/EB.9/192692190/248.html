ELECTRIC

Cross-references: ELECTRICITY

ELECTRIC, derived from ἤλεκτρον, "amber," in physics, is a term applied to those substances, in which the electric fluid is capable of being excited, and accumulated without transmitting it, and therefore called non-conductors. See ELECTRICITY.
