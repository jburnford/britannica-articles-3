EFFENDI

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

EFFENDI, in the Turkish language, signifies ma-

ster; and accordingly is a title very extensively applied; as to the muti and emirs, to the priests of mosques, to men of learning, and of the law. The grand chancellor of the empire is called reit-effendi.
