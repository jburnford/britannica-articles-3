ELECT

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ELECT, (from eligo, "I choose") CHOSEN, in the Scriptures, is applied to the primitive Christians, in which sense, the elect are those chosen and admitted to the favour and blessing of Christianity.
