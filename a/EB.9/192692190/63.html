ECALESIA

article · 152 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ECALIESIA, Εκαλισία, in antiquity, a festival kept in honour of Jupiter, surnamed Hecalus, or Hecaleus, from Hecale, one of the borough towns in Attica.
