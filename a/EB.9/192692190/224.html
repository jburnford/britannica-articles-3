ELBE

article · 363 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ELBE, a large river in Germany, which, rising on the confines of Silesia, runs through Bohemia, Saxony, and Brandenburg; and afterwards dividing the duchy of Luxemburg from that of Mecklenburg, as also the duchy of Bremen from Holstein, it falls into the German ocean, about 70 miles below Hamburg. It is navigable for great ships higher than any river in Europe.
