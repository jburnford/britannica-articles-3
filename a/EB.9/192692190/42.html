EAST, one of the four cardinal points of the world; being that point of the horizon where the sun is seen to rise when in the equinoctial.

The word east is Saxon. In Italy, and throughout the Mediterranean, the east wind is called the levante: in Greek, ανατολὴ and αἰόλιτος, because it comes from the side of the sun, αὖ ἔλθω; in Latin currit.
