CROWN-OFFICE, an office belonging to the king's bench court, of which the king's coroner or attorney is commonly master. In this office, the attorney general and clerk of the crown severally exhibit informations for crimes and misdemeanours at common law, as in the case of batteries, conspiracies, libelling, &c. on which the offender is liable to pay a fine to the king.
