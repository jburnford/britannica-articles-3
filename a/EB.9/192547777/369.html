CORAL FISHERY. Red coral is found in the Mediterranean, on the shores of Provence; from Cape de la Couronne to that of St Tropez; about the isles of Majorca and Minorca; on the south of Sicily; on the coasts of Africa; and, lastly, in the Ethiopic ocean, about Cape Negro.
