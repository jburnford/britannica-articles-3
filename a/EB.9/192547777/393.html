Magical CORD

sub_entry · 540 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Magical CORD, an instrument in great use among the Laplanders, and by them supposed to be endowed with a number of virtues. It is a cord or rope with three knots tied in it. They use many magical rites and ceremonies in the tying of this cord; and, when thus prepared, it is supposed to have power over the winds; and they will sell, by means of it, a good wind, or at least the promise of one, to a ship. If they untie only one of these knots, a moderate gale succeeds; if two, it is much stronger: and if three, a storm is sure to follow.
