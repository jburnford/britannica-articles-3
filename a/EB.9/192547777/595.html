COTA

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COTA, RODRIGUEZ, a Spanish poet in the 16th century, was the author of the Tragi-comedia de Calisto y Melibea, which has been translated into Latin by Gasper Barthius, and into French by James de Lavardin. The Spaniards set a great value on this performance.
