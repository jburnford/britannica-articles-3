CRAMPONEE

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRAMPONEE, in Heraldry, an epithet given to a cross which has at each end a cramp or square piece coming from it; that from the arm in chief towards the sinister angle, that from the arm on that side downwards, that from the arm in base towards the dexter side, and that from the dexter arm upwards.
