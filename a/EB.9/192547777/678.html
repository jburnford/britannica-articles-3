COUNTER-TIME

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTER-TIME, in the manège, is the defence or resistance of a horse that interrupts his cadence, and the measure of his manège, occasioned either by a bad horseman or by the malice of a horse.
