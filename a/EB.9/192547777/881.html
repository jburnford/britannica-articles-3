CRITHOMANCY

article · 329 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRITHOMANCY, a kind of divination, performed by considering the dough or matter of the cakes offered in sacrifice, and the meal strewed over the victims to be killed. Hence, in regard they ordinarily used barley-meal in these ceremonies, this kind of divination was called crithomancy, from kritha, barley, and manom, divination.
