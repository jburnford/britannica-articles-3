CRATO

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRATO, a small town of Portugal, in the province of Alentejo, with a rich priory. It is the chief commandery which the knights of Malta have in Portugal. W. Long. 8. 12. N. Lat. 38. 50.
