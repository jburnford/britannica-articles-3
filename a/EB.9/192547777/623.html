COUCHE

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUCHE, in Heraldry, denotes any thing lying along: thus chevron-couche, is a chevron lying side-wise, with the two ends on each side of the shield, which should properly rest on the base.
