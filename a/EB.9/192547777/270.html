CONTROLLER of the Household

sub_entry · 380 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONTROLLER of the Household, the second officer under the lord steward. The name of his office comes from the French word contreroller. His office is to control the accounts and reckonings of the Green Cloth, of which board he is always a member. He carries a white staff, and is always one of the privy-council. He has 1071. 17s. 6d. a-year wages, and 10921. 2s. 6d. board wages.
