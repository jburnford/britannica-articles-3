COWES, a town and harbour on the north-east coast of the Isle of Wight, in Hampshire. It has no market, but is the best place for trade in the whole island; but as it lies low, the air is accounted unhealthy.

It is eight miles south-east of Portsmouth. W. Long. 1. 25. N. Lat. 50. 45.
