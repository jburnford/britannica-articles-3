CORAL-STONE

article · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORAL-STONE, a name for a kind of red and white agate which breaks in veins, and is found in Italy and some parts of Saxony. That of Rochlitz in Saxony is the most celebrated, and is found in globules which have a kind of crust about them.
