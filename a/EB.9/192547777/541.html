CORUNNA

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORUNNA, or GROYNE, a port-town of Galicia in Spain, situated on a fine bay of the Atlantic ocean, about 32 miles north of Compostella, and 20 south-west of Ferrol. W. Long. 9. o. N. Lat. 43. o.
