CONTRIBUTION, the payment of each person's quota of the part he is to bear in some imposition, or common expense. See CONTINGENT, &c.—Contributions are either involuntary, as those of taxes and imposts; or voluntary, as those of expenses for carrying on some undertaking for the interest of the community.
