CRATER

Cross-references: VOLCANO

CRATER, Cup, in Astronomy, a constellation of the southern hemisphere; whole stars, in Ptolemy's catalogue, are seven; in Tycho's, eight; in Hevelius's ten; in the Britannic catalogue, thirty-one.

CRATER is also used to signify the mouth or opening of a volcano or burning mountain, from whence the fire is discharged. See VOLCANO.
