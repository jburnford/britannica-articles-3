CROYDON

article · 334 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CROYDON, a town in Surrey in England. Its situation is low, near the spring-head of the river Wandel, and it is in a manner surrounded with hills. It is pretty large, and is chiefly noted for being the seat of the archbishop of Canterbury. It has a large handsome church, an hospital, and a free school. W. Long. 0. 5. N. Lat. 51. 22.
