CORCELET

article · 333 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORCELET, in Natural History, that part of the fly-clas which is analogous in its situation to the breast in other animals. Many have called it the breast in

these also, but improperly; because the breast of other animals is the place of the lungs and trachea, but these organs are in the fly-clas distributed through the whole body.
