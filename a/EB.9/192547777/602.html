COTERIE

COTERIE, a term adopted from the French trading associations or partnerships, where each person advances his quota of stock, and receives his proportion of gain; and which retains its original meaning when applied to little assemblies or companies associated for mirth and good humour, where each one furnishes his

* Blackf.
Comment.

quota of pleasantry. Here they coin new words not understood elsewhere, but which it becomes fashionable for others to use; and they are thought ridiculous who are ignorant of them. It has been sometimes used to signify a club of ladies.
