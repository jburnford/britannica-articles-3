COUNTER-POTENT

article · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTER-POTENT (contre-potent), in Heraldry, is reckoned a sur as well as vaire and ermine; but composed of such pieces as represent the tops of crutches, called in French potences, and in old English potents.
