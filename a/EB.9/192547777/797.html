CRAZE-MILL, or GRINDING Mill

sub_entry · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRAZE-MILL, or GRINDING Mill, a mill in all respects like a grist-mill to grind corn, and so called by the tin miners, who use it to grind their tin, which is yet too great after trambling.
