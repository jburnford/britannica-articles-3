CRUCITA, in Botany: A genus of the digynia or-

der, belonging to the tetrandria class of plants; and in the natural method ranking with those the order of which is doubtful. The interior calyx is tetraphyllous, the exterior calyx triphyllous; there is no corolla, and only one seed.
