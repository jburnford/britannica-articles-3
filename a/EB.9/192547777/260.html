CONTRAVALLATION

CONTRAVALLATION, or the Line of CONTRAVALLATION, in Fortification, a trench guarded with a parapet, and usually cut round about a place by the besiegers, to secure themselves on that side, and to stop the falls of the garrison. See FORTIFICATION.
