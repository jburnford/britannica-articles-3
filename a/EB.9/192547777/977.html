CRUSADO

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CRUSADO, in commerce, a Portuguese coin, struck under Alphonsus V. about the year 1457, at the time when Pope Calixtus sent thither the bull for a crusade against the infidels. This coin has a cross on one side and the arms of Portugal on the other.
