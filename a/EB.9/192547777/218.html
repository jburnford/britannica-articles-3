CONTEMPORARY

article · 152 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONTEMPORARY, or COTEMPORARY, a person or thing that existed in the same age with another. Thus, Socrates, Plato, and Aristophanes, were contemporaries.
