COUSU

article · 237 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUSU, in Heraldry, signifies a piece of another colour or metal placed in the ordinary, as if it were sewed on, as the word imports. This is generally of colour upon colour, or metal upon metal, contrary to the general rule of heraldry.
