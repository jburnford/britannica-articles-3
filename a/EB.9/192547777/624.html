COUCHER

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUCHER, or COURCHER, in our statutes, is used for a factor, or one that continues in some place or country for traffic; as formerly in Gascoign, for the buying of wines. Anno 37 Edw. III. c. 16.
