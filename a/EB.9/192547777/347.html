COPPER, one of the metals, called by the alchemists Venus, on account of its facility of uniting with a great number of different metallic substances. Its colour, when pure, is pale red, and its specific gravity from 8.7 to 9.3, which depends not only on its degree of purity, but also on its condensation by hammering. See CHEMISTRY Index.
