CORBEILS

article · 332 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORBEILS, in Fortification, little baskets about a foot and a half high, eight inches wide at the bottom and twelve at the top; which being filled with earth, are frequently set one against another upon the parapet or elsewhere, leaving certain port holes, from whence to fire upon the enemy under covert without being seen by them.
