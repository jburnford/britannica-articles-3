COUPLE CROSS

article · 185 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUPLE CROSS, in Heraldry, the fourth part of a chevron, never borne but in pairs, except there be a chevron between them, saith Guillim, though Bloom gives an instance to the contrary.
