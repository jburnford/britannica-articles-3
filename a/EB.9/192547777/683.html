COUNTORS, COUNTOURS, or COUNTERS

sub_entry · 300 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTORS, COUNTOURS, or COUNTERS, has been used for sergeants at law, retained to defend a cause, or to speak for their client in any course of law.

It is of these Chaucer speaks:

— A sheriff had he been, and a countor,
Was nowhere such a worthy vavafour.

They were anciently called serjeant countors.
