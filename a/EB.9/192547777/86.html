CONGÉ

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONGÉ, in Architecture, a mould in form of a quarter round, or a cavetto, which serves to separate two members from one another; such as that which joins the shaft of the column to the cincture, called also apophyge.
