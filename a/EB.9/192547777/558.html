CORYMBIUM: A genus of plants belonging to the syngenesia class; and in the natural method ranking under the 49th order, Compositæ. See BOTANY INDEX.—The calyx is diphyllous, uniflorous, and prismatical; the corolla monopetalous and regular; there is one woolly seed below each floret.
