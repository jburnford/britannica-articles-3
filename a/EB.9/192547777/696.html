COUPLET, a division of a hymn, ode, song, &c.

Courge wherein an equal number, or equal measure, of verses,
Courayer. is found in each part; which divisions, in odes, are
called strophes.—Couplet, by an abuse of the word, is
frequently made to signify a couple of verses.
