COURTESAN

article · 529 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COURTESAN, a woman who prostitutes herself for hire, especially to people of superior rank. Lais the famous Theban courtesan, stands on record for requiring no less than 10,000 crowns for a single night. Of all places in the world, Venice is that where courtesans abound the most. It is now 300 years since the senate, which had expelled them, was obliged to recall them; in order to provide for the security of women of honour, and to keep the nobles employed, left they should turn their heads to make innovations in the state.
