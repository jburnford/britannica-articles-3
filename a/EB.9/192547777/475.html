CORONA

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CORONA, among Botanists, the name given by some to the circumference or margin of a radiated compound flower. It corresponds to the radius of Linnaeus; and is exemplified in the flat, tongue-shaped petals which occupy the margin of the daily or sun-flower.
