CONSTERNATION

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONSTERNATION is defined by ethical writers to be an excess of horror, owing to the ill government of our admiration and fear: or such an immoderate degree of fear as confounds the faculties, and incapacitates a person for consultation and execution.
