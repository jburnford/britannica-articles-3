COUNTER-SCARP

article · 339 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTER-SCARP, in Fortification, is properly the exterior talus or slope of the ditch; but it is often taken for the covered way and the glacis. In this sense we say, the enemy have lodged themselves in the counter-scarp. Angle of the counter-scarp, is that made by two sides of the counter-scarp, meeting before the middle of the curtain.
