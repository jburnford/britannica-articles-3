COUNTERS IN A SHIP

article · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

COUNTERS IN A SHIP are two. 1. The hollow arching from the gallery to the lower part of the straight piece of the stern, is called the upper-counter. 2. The

lower counter is between the transom and the lower part of the gallery.
