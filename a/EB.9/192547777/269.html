CONTROLLER of the Hanaper

sub_entry · 370 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CONTROLLER of the Hanaper, an officer who attends the lord chancellor daily, in term and in feal-time, to take all things sealed in leather bags from the clerks of the hanaper, and to mark the number and effect thereof, and enter them in a book, with all the duties belonging to the king and other officers for the same, and to charge the clerk of the hanaper with them.
