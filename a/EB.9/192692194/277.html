Rock RUBY

sub_entry · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Rock RUBY, the amethystozonitas of the ancients, is found in Syria, Calcutta, Cananor, Cambaya, and Ethiopia. It is the most valued of all the varieties of garnets, and is frequently sold as a ruby under the name of rubinus Rufficum.
