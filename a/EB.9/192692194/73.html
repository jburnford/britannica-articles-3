RIO-GRANDE

article · 221 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RIO-GRANDE, a river of Africa, which runs from east to west through Negroland, and falls into the Atlantic ocean, in 11 degrees of latitude. Some take it to be a branch of the Niger, of which there is not the least proof.
