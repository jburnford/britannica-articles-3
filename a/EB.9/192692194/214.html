ROSSOLIS

ROSSOLIS, Sauvage, an agreeable spirituous liquor, composed of burnt brandy, sugar, cinnamon, and milk-water; and sometimes performed with a little milk. It has its name from being at first prepared wholly of the juice of the plant ros solis, or drosera. See DIOSCORI, BOTANY INDEX.
