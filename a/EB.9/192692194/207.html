ROSIERS-AUX-SALINES

article · 224 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ROSIERS-AUX-SALINES, a town of France, in the department of Meurthe, famous for its salt-works. The works that King Stanislaus made here are much admired. It is seated on the river Meurthe, in E. Long. 6. 27. N. Lat. 48. 35.
