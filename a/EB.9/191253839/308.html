MYSORE

MYSORE, or MYSOREAN DOMINIONS, a kingdom of Asia, in the East Indies, including the territories usurped or subdued by Hyder Ali, and transmitted to his son Tippoo Saib, but now subject to the British government. For an account of the conquest of which, see INDIA.
