NAGRACUT

NAGRACUT, a town of India, the capital of a kingdom of the same name in the dominions of the Great Mogul, with a rich temple to which the Indians go in pilgrimage. It is seated on the river Ravi. E. Long. 78. 10. N. Lat. 33. 12.
