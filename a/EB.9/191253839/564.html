NEUTRAL Salts

sub_entry · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEUTRAL Salts, in Chemistry, compounded of an acid with any other substance capable of uniting with it and destroying its acidity, as sulphuric acid and soda, or Glauber's salt, muriatic acid and soda, or common salt.
