MOSELLE

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MOSELLE, a river of Germany, which rises in the mountains of Vosges in Lorraine, and falls into the Rhine at Coblenz.

MOSELLE is also the name of a department of France, which includes part of the late province of Lorraine.
