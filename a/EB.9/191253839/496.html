NEMAUSIS, or NEMAUSUM, in Ancient Geography, the capital of the Arecomici in Gallia Narbonensis; a colony, (Coin), with the surname Augusta, (Inscription). In it stands a Roman amphitheatre, which is still almost entire. Now Nismes in Languedoc.
