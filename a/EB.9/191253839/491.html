NEHOW

NEHOW, one of the Sandwich islands, discovered by Captain Cook in his last voyage to the Pacific ocean: these islands are eleven in number, and are situated from 18^{\circ} 44' to 22^{\circ} 15' N. Lat. and from 154^{\circ} 56' to 165^{\circ} 24' W. Long.
