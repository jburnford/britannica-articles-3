NEMOURS

article · 159 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEMOURS, a town of the Isle of France, in the Gatinois, formerly with the title of a duchy. It is seated on the river Loing, in E. Long. 2. 45. N. Lat. 48. 15.
