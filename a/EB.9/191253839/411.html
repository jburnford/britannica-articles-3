NAUCRATIS

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NAUCRATIS, a city of Egypt on the left side of the Canopic mouth of the Nile. It was celebrated for its commerce, and no ship was permitted to land at any other place, but was obliged to sail directly to the city, there to deposit its cargo. It gave birth to Athenæus.
