GOLDSMITHS MOULDS

GOLDSMITHS MOULDS. The goldsmiths use the bones of the cattle fish to make moulds for their small works; which they do by pressing the pattern between two bones, and leaving a jet or hole to convey the metal through, after the pattern has been taken out.
