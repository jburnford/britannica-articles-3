MUTIUS Scavola

sub_entry · 733 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MUTIUS Scavola, Q. surnamed the Augur, was an excellent civilian, and instructed Cicero in the laws. He was made praetor in Asia; was afterwards consul, and performed very important services for the republic.

He ought not to be confounded with Quintus Mutius Scavola, another excellent civilian, who was praetor.
