MURRAY

article · 302 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MURRAY, or MORAY, the name of a district in the north of Scotland, which, in a former division of the kingdom, was denominated a province. This district includes the counties of Banff, Elgin and Nairn. The county of Elgin, the middle division of this district, is still known by the name of Morayshire.
