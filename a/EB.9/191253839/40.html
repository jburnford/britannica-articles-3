MORNING star

sub_entry · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MORNING star, is the planet Venus, when a little to the westward of the sun; that is, when she rises a little before. In this situation she is called by the Greeks Phosphorus; by the Latins Lucifer, &c.
