NEREUS

article · 346 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEREUS, in fabulous history, a marine deity, was son of Oceanus and Thetis. He settled in the Ægean sea, was considered as a prophet, and had the power of assuming what form he pleased. He married his sister Doris, by whom he had 50 daughters called the Nereids, who constantly attended on Neptune, and when he went abroad surrounded his chariot.
