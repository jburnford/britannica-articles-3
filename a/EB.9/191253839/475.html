NEEDS

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEEDS, or St Neots, six miles from Huntingdon, 58 miles from London, so called from the monument of a saint of that name in it, who was burnt by the Danes, is a large well built town, having a handsome strong church, with a very fine steeple, and a stone bridge over the Ouse.
