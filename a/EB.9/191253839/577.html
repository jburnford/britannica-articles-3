NEWEL

article · 212 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEWEL, in architecture, is the upright post which a pair of winding stairs turn about; this is properly a cylinder of stone, which bears on the ground, and is formed by the end of the steps of the winding stairs.
