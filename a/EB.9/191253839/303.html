MYRTOUM MARE

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MYRTOUM MARE, a part of the Ægean sea, lying between Euboea, Peloponnesus, and Attica. It receives this name from Myrto a woman, or from Myrtos a small island in the neighbourhood, or from Myrtus the son of Mercury who was drowned there, &c.
