NABOB

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NABOB, properly NAVAB, the plural of Nab, a deputy. As used in Bengal, it is the same as NAZIM. It is a title also given to the wives and daughters of princes, as well as to the princes themselves.
