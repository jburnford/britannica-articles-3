NEGOMBO

article · 181 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NEGOMBO, a sea port town of Asia, on the west coast of Ceylon. It has a fort built by the Portuguese, which was taken from them by the Dutch in 1640. E. Long. 80. 25. N. Lat. 17. 0.
