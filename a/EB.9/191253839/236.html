MUSHROOM. See FUNGI, BOTANY Index.

To try the quality of mushrooms:—Take an onion, and strip the outer skin, and boil it with your mushrooms: if the onion become blue or black, there are certainly dangerous ones amongst them; if it remain white, they are good.
