NATIVITY

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NATIVITY, in Astrology, the theme or figure of the heavens, and particularly of the twelve houses, at the moment when a person was born; called also the horoscope.

Calling the nativity, or by calculation seeking to know how long the queen should live, &c. was made felony, an. 23 Eliz. c. 2.
