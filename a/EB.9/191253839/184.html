MUNDINGOS

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MUNDINGOS, the name of a people who live on the sides of the river Gambia in Africa, and who are of a jet black colour, strong, and well made. They have a priest sent over every year from one of the Cape de Verd islands to christen and marry.
