GYARUS

article · 177 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GYARUS, in Ancient Geography, one of the Cyclades, 12 miles in compass, lying to the east of Delos. It was a desert island, and allotted for a place of banishment by the Romans.
