GUELDRS

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUELDRS, a strong town of the Netherlands, in the duchy of the same name. It was ceded to the king of Prussia, by the peace of Utrecht, and is seated among marshes. E. Long. 6. N. Lat. 51. 30. It surrendered to the French in 1794.
