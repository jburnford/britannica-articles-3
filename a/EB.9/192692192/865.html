HELLENISTIC LANGUAGE

article · 313 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HELLENISTIC LANGUAGE, that used by the Grecian Jews who lived in Egypt and other parts where the Greek tongue prevailed. In this language it is said the Septuagint was written, and also the books of the New Testament; and that it was thus denominated to show that it was Greek filled with Hebraisms and Syracisms.
