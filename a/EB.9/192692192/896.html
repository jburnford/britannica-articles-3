HEMICYCLE

Cross-references: KEY

HEMICYCLE is particularly applied, in Architecture, to vaults in the cradle form; and arches or sweeps of vaults, constituting a perfect semicircle. To construct an arch of hewn stone, they divide the hemicycle into so many voussours; taking care to make them an uneven number, that there be no joint in the middle, where the key-stone should be.
