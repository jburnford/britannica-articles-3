HENNEBON

article · 171 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HENNEBON, a town of France, in Bretagne, in the diocese of Vannes. It is inhabited by rich merchants, and is seated on the river Blavet, in W. Long. 2. 13. N. Lat. 47. 48.
