GUICCIARDINI, Lewis

sub_entry · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUICCIARDINI, Lewis, his nephew, composed a history of the Low Countries, and memoirs of the affairs of Europe, from 1530 to 1560. He wrote with great spirit against the persecution of the duke d'Alva, for which he imprisoned him. Died in 1583.
