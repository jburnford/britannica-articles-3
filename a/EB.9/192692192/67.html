GRAND GUSTO

article · 336 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GRAND GUSTO, among painters, a term used to express that there is something in the picture very great and extraordinary, calculated to surprise, please, and instruct.—Where this is found, they say, the painter was a man of grand gusto; and they use the words sub-

lime and marvellous, when they speak of a picture, in much the same sense.
