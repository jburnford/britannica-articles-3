HECATOMPYLOS

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HECATOMPYLOS, in Ancient Geography, the metropolis of Parthia, and royal residence of Arsaces, situated at the springs of the Araxes. Thebes in Egypt had also the same name from its 100 gates.
