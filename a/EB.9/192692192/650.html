HARTLAND

article · 273 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HARTLAND, a town in Devonshire, near the Bristol channel, with a market on Saturdays, much frequented by the people of Cornwall, who come hither in boats. It gives its name to a point, called Hartland Point, at the entrance of Bristol channel W. Long. 4. 45. N. Lat. 51. 9.
