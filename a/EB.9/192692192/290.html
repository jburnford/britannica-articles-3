GUAXACA

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUAXACA. a province in the audience of Mexico, in New Spain, which is very fertile in wheat, Indian corn, cochineal, and cassia. It is bounded by the gulf of Mexico on the north, and by the South sea on the south. It contains mines of gold and silver. Guaxaca is the capital town.
