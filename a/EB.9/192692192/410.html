HABSBURG, or HABSBURG, an ancient castle of Switzerland, in the canton of Bern. It is the place where the ancient counts of Hapsburg resided, and is seated near the lake of Lucern, and to the east of the town of that name. E. Long. 8. 10. N. Lat. 47. 22.
