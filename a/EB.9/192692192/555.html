HANCES

article · 281 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HANCES, HANCHES, HAUNCHES, or HANSES, in Architecture, certain small intermediate parts of arches between the crown and the spring at the bottom, being probably about one-third of the arch, and placed nearer to the bottom than the top, which are likewise denominated the spandrels.
