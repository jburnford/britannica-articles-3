HAVEL

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HAVEL, a river of Brandenburg, which proceeds from a lake in the duchy of Mecklenburg, and running through the middle Marche, and through Brandenburg and other towns, runs north, and falls into the Elbe.
