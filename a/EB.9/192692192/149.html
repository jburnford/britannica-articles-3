GREGORIAN CALENDAR

GREGORIAN CALENDAR, that which shows the new and full moon, with the time of Easter, and the moveable feasts depending thereon, by means of epacts, disposed through the several months of the Gregorian year. See CHRONOLOGY, No 26.
