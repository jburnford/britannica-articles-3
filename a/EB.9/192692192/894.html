HEMICRANIA

article · 174 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HEMICRANIA, in Medicine, a species of cephalalgia, or head-ach; wherein only one side of the head is affected; and owing to a congestion of blood in the vessels of that half.
