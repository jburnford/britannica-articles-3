HEMITRITÆUS

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HEMITRITÆUS, in Medicine, a kind of fever, denoting the same as semi-tertian, returning twice every day. The word is Greek, and compounded of hēmis, "half," and tertian, "third or tertian."
