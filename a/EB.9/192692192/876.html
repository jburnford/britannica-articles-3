HELSINGIA

article · 352 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HELSINGIA, a province of Sweden, bounded on the north by Jemteland and Medelpad, on the east by the Bothnic gulf, and on the south and west by Dalecarlia and Götlien. It is full of mountains and forests, and the inhabitants are almost constantly employed in hunting and fishing. It has no cities; the principal towns are, Hudwickvuld, Alta, and Dillto.
