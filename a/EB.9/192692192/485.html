HALLEIN, a town of Germany, in the circle of
Bavaria, and archbishopric of Salzburg; seated on
the river Salza, among the mountains, wherein are
mines of salt, which are the chief riches of the town
and country. E. Long. 12. 15. N. Lat. 47. 33.
