GUNWALE

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUNWALE, or GUNNEL, is the uppermost wale of a ship, or that piece of timber which reaches on either side from the quarter-deck to the forecastle, being the uppermost bend which finishes the upper works of the hull, in that part in which are put the flashions which support the waste-trees.
