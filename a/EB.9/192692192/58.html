GRANADILLOES

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GRANADILLOES, the name of some islands of the Caribbees, in America, having St Vincent to the north and Granada to the south. They are so inconsiderable that they are quite neglected; but were ceded to England by the treaty of peace in 1763.
