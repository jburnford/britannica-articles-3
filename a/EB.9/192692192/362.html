GUST

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUST, a sudden and violent squall of wind, bursting from the hills upon the sea so as to endanger the shipping near the shore. These are peculiar to some coasts, as those of South Barbary and Guinea.
