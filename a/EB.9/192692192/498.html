HALSTEAD

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HALSTEAD, a town of Essex in England, seated on the river Coln, 45 miles from London. The town consists of about 600 houses, and the inhabitants are about 4000 in number. Here is a good manufactory of fays, bays, callimaneoes, &c. and its market is noted for corn.
