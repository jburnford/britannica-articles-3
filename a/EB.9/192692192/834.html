HELICOID PARABOLA

article · 348 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HELICOID PARABOLA, or the parabolic spiral, is a curve arising from the supposition that the common or Apollonian parabola is bent or twisted, till the axis comes into the circumference of a circle, the ordinates still retaining their places and perpendicular positions with respect to the circle, all these lines still remaining in the same plane.
