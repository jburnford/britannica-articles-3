HALLE, a handsome and considerable town of Ger-
many, in the circle of Upper Saxony, and in the duchy
of Magdeburg, with a famous university and salt-works.
It belongs to the king of Prussia; and is seated in a
pleasant plain on the river Saale, in E. Long. 12. 33.
N. Lat. 51. 36.
