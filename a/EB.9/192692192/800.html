HEELER

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HEELER, or Bloody-Hare Cock, a fighting cock, that strikes or wounds much with his spurs.

The masters know such a cock, even while a chicken, by the striking of his two heels together in his going.
