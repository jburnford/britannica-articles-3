GROCERS

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GROCERS, anciently were such persons as engrossed all merchandise that was vendible; but now they are incorporated, and make one of the companies of the city of London, which deals in sugar, foreign fruits, spices, &c.
