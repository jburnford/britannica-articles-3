HALMOTÉ

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: MOT

HALMOTÉ, or HALIMOTÉ, is the same with what is now called a court-baron, the word implying a meeting of the tenants of the same hall or manor. The name is still retained at Luffon, and other places in Herefordshire. See MOTÉ.
