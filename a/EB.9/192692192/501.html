HALTER-CASE

article · 295 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

HALTER-CASE, is an excoriation of the pattern, occasioned by the halter's being entangled about the foot, upon the horse's endeavouring to rub his neck with his hinder feet. For the cure of this, anoint the place, morning and evening, with equal quantities of linseed oil and brandy, well mixed.
