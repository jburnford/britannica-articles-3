GUADALQUIVER

article · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GUADALQUIVER, one of the most famous rivers of Spain, rises in Andalusia, near the confines of Granada, and running quite through Andalusia, by the towns of Baiza, Andaxar, Cordova, Seville, and St. Lucar, falls at last into the bay of Cadiz.
