CAVEAT

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAVEAT, in Law, a kind of process in the spiritual courts, to stop the proving of a will, the granting titles of administration, &c. to the prejudice of another.

It is also used to stop the institution of a clerk to a benefice.
