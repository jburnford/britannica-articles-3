CENTRIFUGAL FORCE

CENTRIFUGAL FORCE, that force by which all bodies that move round any other body in a curve endeavour to fly off from the axis of their motion in a tangent

Centrifugal-Machine. tangent to the periphery of the curve, and that in every part of it. See MECHANICS.
