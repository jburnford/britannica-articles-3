CARAMANIA

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARAMANIA, a considerable province of Turkey in Asia, in the south part of Natolia. Bajazet united this province to his empire about the year 1488, and since that time it has continued in the possession of the Turks. Satalia was the capital city, but is now much decayed.
