CANTHI

article · 234 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANTHI, in Anatomy, cavities at the extremities of the eyelids, commonly called the corners of the eye: the greater of them, or the greater canthus, is next the nose; the lesser of them, or the little canthus, lies towards the temple.
