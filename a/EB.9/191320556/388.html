CALES

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALES, in Ancient Geography, a municipal city of some note in Campania, at no great distance from Capulinum. The epithet Calenus is by Horace and Juvenal applied to a generous wine which the territory produced.
