CALDERON DE LA BARCA

article · 320 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALDERON DE LA BARCA, DOM PEDRO, a Spanish officer, who after having signalized himself in the military profession, quitted it for the ecclesiastical, and then commenced dramatic writer. His dramatic works make 9 vols. in 4to. and some Spanish authors have compared him to Shakespeare. He flourished about the year 1640.
