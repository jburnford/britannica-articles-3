CAMPITAE

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMPITAE, in church history, an appellation given to the Donatists, on account of their assembling in the fields for want of churches. For a similar reason, they were also denominated Montenses and Rupitani.
