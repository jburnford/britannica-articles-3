CARVING

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARVING, in a general sense, the art or act of cutting or fashioning a hard body, by means of some sharp instrument, especially a chisel. In this sense carving includes statuary and engraving, as well as cutting in wood.
