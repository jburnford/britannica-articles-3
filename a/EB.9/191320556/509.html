CAMARCUM, in Ancient Geography, the capital of the Nervii, a people of Gallia Belgica, (Antonine, Peutinger); before whose time no mention was made of it. Now Cambray, capital of the Cambresis, in French Flanders. E. Long. 3. 15. Lat. 50. 15.
