CANUSIUM

article · 307 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANUSIUM, in Ancient Geography, a town of Apulia, on the right or south side of the Aufidus, to the west of Cannæ, whither the Romans fled after the defeat sustained there. It was famous for its red shining wool; whence those who wore clothes made of it were called Canusinati. Now called CANOSA; which see.
