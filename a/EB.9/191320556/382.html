CALENDER of Monteith, a district in the south-west corner of Perthshire in Scotland, from which a branch of the ancient family of Livingstone had the title of earl. The chief seat of the family near Falkirk is also called Calender. Both estate and title were forfeited in consequence of the possessor being engaged in the rebellion 1715.
