CAPITANEATE

article · 233 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPITANEATE, in a general sense, the same with capitania. Capitaneates, in Prussia, are a kind of noble feuds or estates, which, besides their revenue, raise their owners to the rank of nobility. They are otherwise called starosties.
