CASTELVETRO

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTELVETRO, LEWIS, a native of Modena, of the 16th century, famous for his Comment on Aristotle's Poetics. He was prosecuted by the inquisition for a certain book of Melancthon, which he had translated into Italian. He retired to Basil, where he died.
