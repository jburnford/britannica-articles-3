CARPOBALSAM

article · 401 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARPOBALSAM, in the Materia Medica, the fruit of the tree which yields the true oriental balsam. The carpobalsam is used in Egypt, according to Proper Alpinus, in all the intentions in which the balsam itself is applied: but the only use the Europeans make of it is in Venice treacle and mithridate; and in these not a great deal, for cubebs and juniper-berries are generally substituted in its place.
