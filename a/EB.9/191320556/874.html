CAPRALA

article · 231 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPRALA, an isle of Italy, in the Tuscan sea, to the north-east of Corsica, on which it depends. It is pretty populous, and has a strong castle for its defence. It is about 15 miles in circumference. E. Long. 11. 5. N. Lat. 43. 15.
