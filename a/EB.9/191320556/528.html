CAMBRASINES

article · 201 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMBRASINES, in commerce, fine linen made in Egypt, of which there is a considerable trade at Cairo, Alexandria, and Rosetta, or Raschit. They are called cambrasines from their resemblance to cambrics.
