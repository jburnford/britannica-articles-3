CANONIST

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANONIST, a person skilled in or who makes profession of the study and practice of the canon law. Canonists and civilians are usually combined in the same persons: and hence the title of doctor juris utriusque, or legum doctor, usually expressed in abbreviature, L. L. D. or J. U. D.
