CAMELFORD

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMELFORD, a borough town of Cornwall in England, consisting of about 100 houses, badly built; but the streets are broad and well paved. W. Long. 5. 4. N. Lat. 50. 40. It sends two members to parliament; and gives title of baron to Thomas Pitt, elder brother of the great earl of Chatham.
