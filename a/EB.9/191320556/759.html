CANTAZARO

CANTAZARO, an episcopal city of Italy, in the kingdom of Naples, and in the territory of Calabria Ulterior. It is the residence of the governor of the province, and is seated near the sea, in E. Long. 17. o. N. Lat. 38. 59.
