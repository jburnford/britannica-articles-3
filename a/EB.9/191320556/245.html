CAERFILLY

CAERFILLY, a town of Glamorgan-shire in South Wales, seated between the rivers Trawff and Llaneyn, in a moorish ground among the hills. It is thought the walls, now in ruins, were built by the Romans; there being often Roman coins dug up there. W. Long. 3. 12. N. Lat. 51. 25.
