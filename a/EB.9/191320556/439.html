CALLIPOLIS

article · 180 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALLIPOLIS, in Ancient Geography, the name of several cities of antiquity, particularly one upon the Hellepont, next the Propontis, and opposite to Lampacus in Asia. Now GALLIPOLI.
