CANDISH

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANDISH, a considerable province of Asia, in the dominions of the Great Mogul, bounded by Chytor and Malva on the north, Orixa on the east, Decan on the south, and Guzerat on the west. It is populous and rich; and abounds in cotton, rice, and indigo. Brampor is the capital town.
