CAMMA

article · 677 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMMA, and GONZI, two provinces of the kingdom of Loango in Africa. The inhabitants are continually at war with each other. The weapons they formerly used in their wars were the short pike, bows and arrows, sword and dagger; but since the Europeans have become acquainted with that coast, they have supplied them with fire-arms. The chief town of Gobbi lies about a day's journey from the sea.— Their rivers abound with a variety of fish; but are infested with sea horses, which do great mischief both by land and water. The principal commerce with the natives is in logwood, elephants teeth and tails, the hair of which is highly valued, and used for several curious purposes.
