CHALCIDICE

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHALCIDICE, in Ancient Geography, an eastern district of Macedonia, stretching northwards between the Sinus Toroneus and Singiticus. Formerly a part of Thrace, but invaded by Philip of Macedon. Named from the city Chalcis near Olynthus.
