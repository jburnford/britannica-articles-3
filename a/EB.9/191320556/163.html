CABINDA

article · 281 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CABINDA, the chief port of the kingdom of Angola in Loango in Africa. It is situated at the mouth of a river of the same name about five leagues north of Cape Palmerino, on the north side of the mouth of the river Zaire. The bay is very commodious for trade, wooding, and watering.
