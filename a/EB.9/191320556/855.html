CAPITOLINUS, JULIUS

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPITOLINUS, JULIUS, an historian in the beginning of the fourth age under Diocletian, to whom he inscribed the Lives of Verus, Antoninus Pius, Claudius Balbinus, Macrinus, the Maximins, and the Gordians. He wrote other lives, which are most of them lost.
