CANDELARES

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANDELARES, (from candela, a candle), the name of an order in the former editions of Linnaeus's Fragments of a natural method, consisting of these three genera, rhizophora, nyssa and minusopt. They are removed, in the latter editions, into the order HOLOKACER.
