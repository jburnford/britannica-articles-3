CÆLIUS, Aurelianus, an ancient physician, and the only one of the sect of the Methodists of whom we have any remains. He was of Sicca, a town of Numidia; but in what age he lived, cannot be determined: it is probable, however, that he lived before Galen: since, though he carefully mentions all the physicians before him, he takes no notice of Galen.
