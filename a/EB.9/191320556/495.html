CALYDON

article · 276 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALYDON, in Ancient Geography, a town of Ætolia, situated seven miles and a half from the sea, and divided by the river Evenus; the country was anciently called Æolis, from the Æolians its inhabitants. This country was famous for the story of Meleager and the Calydonian boar.
