CHAPPE

article · 325 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHAPPE, in Heraldry, the dividing an escutcheon by lines drawn from the centre of the upper edge to the angles below, into three parts, the sections on the sides being of different metal or colour from the rest.

CHAPPEL in FAIR, a market town of Derbyshire, about 26 miles north-west of Derby. W. Long. 1. 50. N. Lat. 53. 22.
