CALLIRRHÖE

article · 349 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALLIRRHÖE, in Ancient Geography, furnished Enneacrunos, from its nine springs or channels; a fountain not far from Athens, greatly adorned by Pilsistratus, where there were several wells, but this the only running spring. Callirrhoe was also the name of a very fine spring of hot water beyond Jordan near the Dead Sea, into which it empties itself.
