CAUTIONER

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAUTIONER, in Scots Law, that person who becomes bound for another to the performance of any deed or obligation. As to the different kinds and effects of Cautionry, see LAW, Part III. No clxxv. 19.
