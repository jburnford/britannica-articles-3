CALLIOPE

article · 325 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALLIOPE, in the Pagan mythology, the Muse who presides over eloquence and heroic poetry. She was thus called from the sweetness of her voice, and was reckoned the first of the nine sisters. Her distinguishing office was to record the worthy actions of the living; and accordingly she is represented with tablets in her hand.
