CASTALIUS VONS

article · 300 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTALIUS VONS (Strabo, Pausanias); Castalia, (Pindar, Virgil); A fountain at the foot of Mount Parnassus, in Phocis, near the temple of Apollo, or near Delphi; sacred to the Muses, thence called Castalides. Its murmurs were thought prophetic, (Nonnus, Lucian.) See the articles DELPHI and PARNASSUS.
