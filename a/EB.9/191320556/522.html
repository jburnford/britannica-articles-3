Figured CAMBLETS

sub_entry · 326 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Figured CAMBLETS, are those of one colour, whereon are stamped various figures, flowers, foliage, &c. by means of hot irons, which are a kind of moulds, passed together with the stuff under a press. These are chiefly brought from Amiens and Flanders; the commerce of these was anciently much more considerable than at present.
