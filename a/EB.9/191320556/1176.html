CASA-NOVA

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASA-NOVA, MARC ANTONY, a Latin poet, born at Rome, succeeded particularly in epigrams. The poems he composed in honour of the illustrious men of Rome are also much esteemed. He died in 1527.
