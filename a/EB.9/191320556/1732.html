CHALCONDYLAS, LAONICUS

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHALCONDYLAS, LAONICUS, a famous Greek historian of the 15th century, was born at Athens; and wrote an excellent history of the Turks, from Ottoman, who reigned about the year 1300, to Mahomet II. in 1453.
