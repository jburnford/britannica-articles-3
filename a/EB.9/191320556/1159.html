CARUS

CARUS, Marcus Aurelius, was raised from a low station, by his great merit, to be emperor of Rome in 282. He showed himself worthy of the empire; subdued its enemies; and gave the Romans a prospect of happy days, when he was unfortunately killed by lightning in 284.
