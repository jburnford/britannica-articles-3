CELLS

CELLS, in Anatomy, little bags, or bladders, where
fluids or other matters are lodged; called loculi, cellu-
la, &c. Thus the cellula adiposa are the little cells
where the fat is contained; cellula in the colon, are
spaces wherein the excrements are detained till void-
ed, &c.
