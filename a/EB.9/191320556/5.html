BURLINGTON

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BURLINGTON, a sea-port town in the east riding of Yorkshire, situated on the German ocean, about 37 miles north-east of York. E. Long. 0. 10. and N. Lat. 54. 15. It gave the title of earl to a branch of the noble family of Boyle, but the earldom is now extinct.
