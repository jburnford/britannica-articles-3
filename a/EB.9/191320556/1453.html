CAVIL

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAVIL (cavillatio), is defined by some a fallacious kind of reason, carrying some resemblance of truth, which a person, knowing its falsehood, advances in dispute for the sake of victory. The art of framing sophisms or fallacies is called by Boethius cavillatoria.
