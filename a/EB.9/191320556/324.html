CALAMUS Scriptorinus

sub_entry · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALAMUS Scriptorinus, in antiquity, a reed or rush to write with. The ancients made use of styles to write on tables covered with wax; and of reed, or rush, to write on parchment, or Egyptian paper.
