CAPNICON

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPNICON, in Antiquity, chimney money, or a tax which the Roman emperors levied for smoke, and which of consequence was due from all, even the poorest, who kept a fire. This was first invented by Nicephorus.
