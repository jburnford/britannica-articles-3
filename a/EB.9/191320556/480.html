CALVISIUS

CALVISIUS, SETH, a celebrated German chronologer in the beginning of the 17th century. He wrote Elechus calendarii Gregoriani, et duplex calendarii melioris forma, and other learned works, together with some excellent treatises on music. He died in 1617, aged 61.
