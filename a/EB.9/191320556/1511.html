CELASTRUS. See BOTANY INDEX.

In Senegal the negroes use the powder of the root of this plant as a specific against gonorrhœas, which it is said to cure in eight or sometimes in three days. An infusion of the bark of a species of staff tree, which grows in the ile of France, is said to possess the same virtues.
