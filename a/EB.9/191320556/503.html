CAMAIEU

article · 408 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMAIEU is also frequently applied to any kind of gem, wherein figures may be engraven either indentedly or in relief. In this sense the lapidaries of Paris are called in their statutes, cutters of camayeux.

A society of learned men at Florence undertook to procure all the cameos or camayeux and intaglios in the great duke's gallery to be engraven; and began to draw the heads of divers emperors in cameos.
