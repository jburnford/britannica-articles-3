CAPITULUM

article · 281 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPITULUM, in ecclesiastical writers, denoted part of a chapter of the Bible read and explained. In which sense they said, ire ad capitulum, "to go to such a lecture." Afterwards the place or apartment where such theological exercises were performed was denominated domus capituli.
