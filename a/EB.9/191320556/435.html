CALDINUS

article · 189 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALDINUS of Ephesus, a very ancient Greek poet, inventor of elegiac verse; some specimens of which are to be found in the collection of Stobæus. He flourished about 776 years before Christ.
