CARSE

CARSE, or Garre of Gorty, a district of Perthshire in Scotland. It lies on the north side of the Tay, and extends 14 miles in length from Dundee to Perth, and is from two to four in breadth. It is a rich plain country, cultivated like a garden, and producing as good harvests of wheat as any in Great Britain. It abounds with all the necessaries of life: but from its low damp situation, the inhabitants are subject to agues, and the commonalty are in great want of firing. In this district, not far from the Tay, stands the house of Errol, which formerly belonged to the earls of that name, the chiefs of the ancient family of Hay, hereditary constables of Scotland.
