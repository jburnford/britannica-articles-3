CAPITA

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAPITA, DISTRIBUTION BY, in Law, signifies the appointing to every man an equal share of a personal estate; when all the claimants claim in their own rights, as in equal degrees of kindred, and not jure representationis.
