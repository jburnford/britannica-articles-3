CALLE

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALLE, in Ancient Geography, a town of Hither Spain, situated on an eminence which hangs over the river Darius; whose port was at the mouth of the river. Now Porto, Oporto, or Port a Port.
