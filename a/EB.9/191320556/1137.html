CARTMEL, a town of Lancashire in England. It is seated among the hills called Cartmel-fells, not far from the sea, and near the river Kent; adorned with a very handsome church, built in the form of a cross like a cathedral. The market is well supplied with corn, sheep, and fish. W. Long. 2. 43. N. Lat. 54. 15.
