CARIATI

CARIATI, a town of Italy, in the kingdom of Naples, and province of Hither Calabria, with a bishop's see, and the title of a principality. It is two miles from the gulf of Taranto, and 37 north-east of Cosenza. E. Long. 17. 19. N. Lat. 30. 38.
