CALATAJUD, a large and handsome town of Spain, in the kingdom of Arragon; situated at the confluence of the rivers Xalon and Xiloca, at the end of a very fertile valley, with a good castle on a rock. W. Long. 2. 9. N. Lat. 41. 22.
