CANZONETTA

article · 320 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANZONEITA, a diminutive of canzone, denoting a little short song. The canzonette Neapolitane has two strains, each whereof is sung twice over, as the vaudevilles of the French. The canzonette Siciliane is a species of jig, the measure whereof is usually twelve eighths, and six eighths, and sometimes both, as rondeaus.
