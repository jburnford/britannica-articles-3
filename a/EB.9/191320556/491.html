CALYCANTHEMÆ, in Botany, an order of plants in the Fragmenta methodi naturalis of Linnaeus, in which are the following genera, viz. epilobium, cenothera, jussiaea, ludwigia, oldenlandia, isnarda, &c. See BOTANY, Natural Orders.
