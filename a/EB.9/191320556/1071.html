CARPENTRAS

CARPENTRAS, an episcopal town of France, in the department of Vaucluse, and capital of Venaissin. It is subject to the pope; and is seated on the river Auzon, at the foot of a mountain. E. Long. 5. 6. N. Lat. 44. 4.
