CALVITES

Cross-references: ALOPECIA

CALVITES, or CALVITUM, in Medicine, baldness, or a want of hair, particularly on the scaput, occasioned by the moisture of the head, which should feed it, being dried up, by some disease, old age, or the immoderate use of powder, &c. See ALOPECIA.
