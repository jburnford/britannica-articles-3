CANCERIZANTE

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANCERIZANTE, or CANCERIZATO, in the Italian music, a term signifying a piece of music that begins at the end, being the retrograde motion from the end of a song, &c. to the beginning.
