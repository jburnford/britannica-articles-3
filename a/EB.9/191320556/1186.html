CASEMENT

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASEMENT, or CASEMATE, in Architecture, a hollow moulding, which some architects make one-sixth of a circle, and others one fourth.

CASEMENT is also used in building, for a little moveable window, usually within a larger, being made to open or turn on hinges.
