CANTABRUM

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CANTABRUM, in Antiquity, a large kind of flag used by the Roman emperors, distinguished by its peculiar colour, and bearing on it some words or motto of good omen, to encourage the soldiers.
