CHAFFERY

article · 424 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHAFFERY, in the iron works, the name of one of the two principal forges. The other is called the finery. When the iron has been brought at the fi-

nery into what is called an ancony, or square mass, Chaffinch hammered into a bar in its middle, but with its two ends rough, the business to be done at the chaffery is the reducing the whole to the same shape, by hammering down these rough ends to the shape of the middle part.
