CAMPDEN

article · 186 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CAMPDEN, a small town of Gloucestershire in England, containing about 200 houses. It gives title of Viscount, by courtesy, to the earl of Gainborough his son. W. Long. 1. 50. N. Lat. 52.
