CEDRENUS

CEDRENUS, GEORGE, a Grecian monk, lived in the 11th age, and wrote "Annals, or an abridged History, from the beginning of the World to the Reign of Isaac Comnenus emperor of Constantinople, who succeeded Michael IV. in 1057." This work is no more than an extract from several historians. There is an edition of it, printed at Paris in 1647, with the Latin version of Xylander, and the notes of Father Goar a Dominican.
