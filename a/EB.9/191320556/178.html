CABO DE ISTRIA

article · 247 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CABO DE ISTRIA, the capital town of the province of Istria, in the territory of Venice; and the see of a bishop. It is seated on a small island in the gulf of Venice, and is joined to the main land by draw-bridges. E. Long. 14. 22. N. Lat. 45. 49.
