BUYING the Small-Pox

sub_entry · 361 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUYING the Small-Pox, is an appellation given to a method of procuring that disease by an operation similar to inoculation; frequent in South Wales, where it has obtained time out of mind. It is performed either by rubbing some of the pue taken out of a pustule of a various person on the skin, or by making a puncture in the skin with a pin dipped in such pus.
