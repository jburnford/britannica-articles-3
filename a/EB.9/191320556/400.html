CALIDUCT

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALIDUCT, in Antiquity, a kind of pipes or canal disposed along the walls of houses or apartments, used by the ancients for conveying heat to several remote parts of the house from one common furnace.
