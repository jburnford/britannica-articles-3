CALEDONIA

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CALEDONIA, the name of a settlement made by the Scots on the west side of the gulf of Darien, in 1698; out of which they were starved at the request of the East India Company; for the English government prohibited the other colonies sending them any provisions; so they were obliged to leave it in 1700.
