BUSTUARIE MOCHNE

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUSTUARIE MOCHNE, according to some, women that were hired to accompany the funeral and lament the loss of the deceased: but others are of opinion, that they were rather the more common prostitutes, that stood among the tombs, graves, and other such lonely places.
