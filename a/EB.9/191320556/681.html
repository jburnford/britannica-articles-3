CANINA

CANINA, the north part of the ancient Epirus, a province of Greece, which now belongs to the Turks, and lies off the entrance of the gulf of Venice. The principal town is of the same name, and is seated on the sea coast, at the foot of the mountains of Chimera. E. Long. 19. 25. N. Lat. 40. 55.
