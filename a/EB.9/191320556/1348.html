CATANZARO

article · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CATANZARO, a city in the kingdom of Naples, the capital of Calabria Ulterior, with a bishop's see. It is the usual residence of the governor of the province, and is seated on a mountain, in E. Long. 18. 20. N. Lat. 48. 58.
