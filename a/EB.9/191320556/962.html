CARDINAL

article · 362 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARDINAL, in a general sense, an appellation given to things on account of their pre-eminence. The word is formed of the Latin cardo, a hinge; it being on these fundamental points that all the rest of the same kind are supposed to turn. Thus, justice, prudence, temperance, and fortitude, are called the four cardinal virtues, as being the basis of all the rest.
