CHAFFERCONNERS

article · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CHAFFERCONNERS, in commerce, printed linens manufactured in the Great Mogul's dominions. They are imported by the way of Surat, and are of the number of those linens prohibited in France.
