CENTO

article · 302 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CENTO, in poetry, a work wholly composed of verses or passages promiscuously taken from other authors, only divided in a new form and order.—Proba Falconia. Attent the life of Jesus Christ in centos taken from Virgil. Alexander Ross has done the like in his Christiados, and Stephen de Pleure the same.
