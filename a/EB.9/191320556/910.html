CARUS Mortuum

sub_entry · 387 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CARUS Mortuum, a Latin name given to fixed and exhausted residuum remaining in retorts after distillations. As these residuum are very different, according to the substances distilled, and the degree of heat employed, they are by the more accurate modern chemists particularly specified by adding a term denoting their qualities; as earthy residuum, cherry residuum, saline residuum, &c.
