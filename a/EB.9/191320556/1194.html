CASHIER

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASHIER, the cash-keeper; he who is charged with the receiving and paying the debts of a society.—In the generality of foundations, the cashier is called treasurer.

CASHIERS of the Bank, are officers who sign the notes that are issued out, examine and mark them when returned for payment, &c.
