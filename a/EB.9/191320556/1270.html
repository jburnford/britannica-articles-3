CASTELLORUM OPERATIO

article · 327 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

CASTELLORUM OPERATIO, castle work, or service and labour done by inferior tenants for the building and upholding of castles of defence; towards which some gave personal assistance, and others paid their contributions. This was one of the three necessary charges to which all lands among the Anglo-Saxons were expressly subject.
