PLESSIS-LES-TOURS

article · 321 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PLESSIS-LES-TOURS, formerly a royal palace of France, within half a league of Tours. It was built by Louis XI. and in it he died in the year 1483. It is situated in a plain surrounded by woods, at a small distance from the Loire. The building is yet handsome, though built of brick, and converted to purposes of commerce.
