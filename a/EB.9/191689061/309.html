PLANTAGO, PLANTAIN; a genus of plants belonging to the tetrandria class. See BOTANY Index.—Of the plantain there are the following species: The common broad-leaved plantain, called waybred, or way-

born; because it commonly grows by the wayside; the great hoary plantain, or lamb-tongue; the narrow-leaved plantain, or ribwort.
