PI-HAHIROTH

article · 288 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PI-HAHIROTH, (Moses); understood to be a mouth or narrow pass between two mountains, called Chiroth, or Eiroth, and lying not far from the bottom of the western coast of the Arabian gulf; before which mouth the children of Israel encamped, just before their entering the Red sea, (Wells).
