PLASTER

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PLASTER, or EMPLASTER, in Pharmacy, an external application of a harder consistency than an ointment; to be spread, according to the different circumstances of the wound, place, or patient, either upon linen or leather.
