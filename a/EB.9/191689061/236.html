PISCIDIA Erythrina

sub_entry · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PISCIDIA Erythrina, or Dogwood-tree, is employed to

intoxicate fish. For this purpose, the bark is pounded small, put into bags, and soaked in salt water. The juice, which is of a red colour, makes the fish stupid, so that they are easily taken.
