PLEXUS

Cross-references: ANATOMY

PLEXUS, among anatomists, a bundle of small vessels interwoven in the form of net-work; thus a congeries of vessels within the brain is called plexus choroides, reticularis, or retiformis. See ANATOMY.

A plexus of nerves is the union of two or more nerves, forming a sort of ganglion or knot.
