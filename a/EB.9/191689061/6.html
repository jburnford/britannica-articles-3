PHOCILIDES

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHOCILIDES, a Greek poet and philosopher of Miletus, flourished about 540 years before the Christian era. The poetical piece now extant, attributed to him, is not of his composition, but of another poet who lived in the reign of Adrian.
