PHUL

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHUL, or PUH, king of Assyria, is by some historians said to be Nimus under another name, and the first founder of that monarchy: A renowned warrior. He invaded Israel in the reign of Menabem, who became tributary to him, and paid him 1000 talents of silver for a peace. Flourished 771 B. C.
