TEXT

article · 259 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TEXT, a relative term, contradicting to gloss or commentary, and signifying an original discourse exclusive of any note or interpretation. This word is particularly used for a certain passage of scripture, chosen by a preacher to be the subject of his sermon.
