THALIA

article · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

THALIA, in Pagan mythology, one of the nine muses. She presided over Comedy; and is represented crowned with a garland of ivy, holding a mask in her hand, and wearing bulkins on her feet.
