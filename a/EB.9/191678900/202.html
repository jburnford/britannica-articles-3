SYNODICAL

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SYNODICAL, something belonging to a synod. Thus, synodical epistles are circular letters written by the synods to the absent prelates and churches; or even those general ones directed to all the faithful, to inform them of what had passed in the synod.
