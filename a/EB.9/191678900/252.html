TAFFETY

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TAFFETY, or TAFFETA, in Commerce, a fine smooth silken stuff, remarkably glossy. There are taffeties of all colours, some plain, and others striped with gold, silver, &c. others chequered, others flowered, &c. according to the fancy of the workmen.
