A detailed technical illustration of Herschel's Great Telescope, showing its massive wooden structure. The telescope is mounted on a large, triangular wooden frame. The frame is supported by a base with wheels. The telescope tube is positioned at an angle, with a platform and stairs leading to it. The illustration includes various labels: 'H' at the top right, 'C' near the telescope tube, and 'H' near the platform. The base of the structure is labeled 'I' and 'II'.
