SWEEP

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SWEEP, in the sea-language, is that part of the mould of a ship where she begins to compass in the rung-heads; also when the hauer is dragged along the bottom of the sea to recover any thing that is sunk, they call this action sweeping for it.
