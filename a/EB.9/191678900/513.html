TEWKESBURY

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TEWKESBURY, a town in Gloucestershire, formerly noted for its monastery, and now containing about 500 houses, with a magnificent church. It is seated at the confluence of the rivers Severn and Avon, has a cotton manufactory, and sends two members to parliament. W. Long. 2. 13. N. Lat. 52. 0.
