TERM

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TERM, in Law, is generally taken for a limitation of time or estate; as, a lease for term of life or years.

Term, however, is more particularly used for that time

wherein our courts of justice are open; in opposition to which, the rest of the year is called vacation.
