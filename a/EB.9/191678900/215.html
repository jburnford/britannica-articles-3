SYSTEM

SYSTEM, in general, denotes an assemblage or

chain of principles and conclusions, or the whole of any doctrine, the several parts whereof are bound together, and follow or depend on each other; in which sense we say a system of philosophy, system of divinity, &c. The word is formed from the Greek συστασις "composition, compages."
