THEOGNIS

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

THEOGNIS, an ancient Greek poet of Megara in Achaia, flourished about the 50th Olympiad, 144 B. C. We have a moral work of his extant, containing a summary of precepts and reflections, usually found in the collections of the Greek minor poets.
