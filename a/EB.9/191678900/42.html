SUPERFETATION

article · 392 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUPERFETATION, in Physiology, a second or after-conception, happening when the mother, already pregnant, conceives of a latter coition; so that she bears at once two foetuses of unequal age and bulk, and is delivered of them at different times. We meet with instances of superfetations in Hippocrates, Aristotle, Du Laurens, &c.: but they are said to be much more frequent in hares and swine.
