TERMINI

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TERMINI, in Architecture, denote a kind of statues or columns, adorned on the top with the figure of a man's, woman's, or satyr's head, as a capital; and the lower part ending in a kind of sheath or scabbard.
