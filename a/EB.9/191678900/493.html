TESTUDO

article · 331 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TESTUDO, was also a kind of large wooden tower which moved on several wheels, and was covered with bullock-bides, serving to shelter the soldiers when they approached the walls to mine them, or to batter them with rams. It was called testudo, from the strength of its roof, which covered the workmen as the shell does the tortoise.
