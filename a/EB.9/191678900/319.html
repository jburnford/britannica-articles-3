TARE

article · 279 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TARE, is an allowance for the outside package that contains such goods as cannot be unpacked without detriment; or for the papers, threads, bands, &c. that inclose or bind any goods imported loose; or though imported in casks; chests, &c. yet cannot be unpacked and weighed neat.
