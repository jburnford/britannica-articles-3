VISHNOU

Cross-references: POLYTHEISM

VISHNOU, that person in the triad of the Bramins who is considered as the preserver of the universe. Brahma is the creator, and Siva the destroyer; and these two, with Vishnou, united in some inexplicable manner, constitute Brahme, or the supreme nomen of the Hindus. See POLYTHEISM, No 36.
