Asb-WEDNESDAY

sub_entry · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Asb-WEDNESDAY, the first day of Lent, so called from the custom observed in the ancient Christian church of penitents expressing their humiliation at this time, by appearing in sack-cloth and ashes.
