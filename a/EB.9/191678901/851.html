WAAL

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WAAL, a river of the United Netherlands, being one of the branches of the Rhine, which runs from east to west, through Guelderland; passing by Nimeguen, Tiel, Bommel, and Gorcum; and, uniting with the Maes, falls into the German sea below the Briel.
