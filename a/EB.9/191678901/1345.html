ZUILA

article · 331 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ZUILA, a town in the territory of Fezzan, in Africa, which stands on a space of about a mile in circuit, but was formerly of much greater extent. The environs are level, well supplied with water, and fertile, planted with groves of date trees, and the inhabitants pay much attention to agriculture. N. Lat. 27. 29. E. Long. 16. 39.
