WAIGATS STRAITS

article · 190 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WAIGATS STRAITS, situated between Nova Zembla and Russia, through which the Dutch failed to the north, as high as 75°, in order to discover a north-east passage to China and the East Indies.
