ZELL

ZELL, a city of Germany in the circle of Lower Saxony, capital of the duchies of Zell and Lunenburg, situated at the confluence of the rivers Aller and Fuhle, 30 miles north of Hanover, and 40 south of Lunenburg. E. Long. 10. 12. N. Lat. 52. 49.
