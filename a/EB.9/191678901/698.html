VISTULA

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VISTULA, or WEISEL, a large river of Poland, which taking its rise in the mountains south of Silesia, visits Cracow, Warsaw, &c. and continuing its course northward, falls into the Baltic sea below Dantzic.
