WITENA mot

sub_entry · 340 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WITENA mot, or WITENA Gemot, among the Anglo-Saxons, was a term which literally signified the assembly of the wise men; and was applied to the great council of the nation, of latter days called the parliament.

WITHERS of a HORSE, the juncture of the shoulder-bones at the bottom of the neck and mane, towards the upper part of the shoulder.
