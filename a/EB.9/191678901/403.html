TULL, JETHRO

TULL, JETHRO, an Oxfordshire gentleman who farmed his own land, and introduced a new method of culture, to raise repeated crops of wheat from the same land without the necessity of manure: the principles of which he published about 30 years since, in a Treatise on Horse-hoeing Husbandry.
