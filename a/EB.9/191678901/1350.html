ZUYDER-ZEE

article · 351 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ZUYDER-ZEE, a great gulf or bay of the German ocean, which extends from south to north in the United Provinces, between Friesland, Over-Yssel, Guelderland, and Holland. It is so called from its situation towards the south. It is said that the Zuyder-zee was formerly a lake, and that the land is swallowed up which united North-Holland with Friesland.
