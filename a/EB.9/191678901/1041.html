WHIP

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WHIP, or WHIP-STAFF, in a ship, a piece of timber, in form of a strong staff, fastened into the helm, for the steerman, in small ships, to hold in his hand, in order to move the rudder, and direct the ship.
