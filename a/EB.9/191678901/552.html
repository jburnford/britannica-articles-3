VELEZ-DE-GOMARA

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VELEZ-DE-GOMARA, a town of Africa, in the kingdom of Fez, and in the province of Eriff. It is the ancient ACARTH. With a harbour and a handsome castle, where the governor resides. It is seated between two high mountains, on the coast of the Mediterranean sea. W. Long. 4. 0. N. Lat. 35. 10.
