TIERCED, in Heraldry

sub_entry · 182 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TIERCED, in Heraldry, denotes the shield to be divided by any part of the partition-lines, as party, coupy, tranchy, or tailly, into three equal parts of different colours or metals.
