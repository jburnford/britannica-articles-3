UNDERWALDEN, a canton of Switzerland, and the sixth in rank. It is bounded on the north by the canton of Lucern and by the lake of the Four Cantons, on the east by the high mountains which separate it from the canton of Bern, and on the west by the canton of Bern. The religion of this canton is the Roman Catholic.
