VEHICLE

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VEHICLE, in general, denotes any thing that carries or bears another along; but is more particularly used in pharmacy for any liquid serving to dilute some medicine, in order that it may be administered more commodiously to the patient.
