VOORN

article · 368 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VOORN, one of the islands of Holland, bounded by the river Maas, which divides it from the continent and the island of IJermunde, on the north; by the sea called Bies-bocht, on the east; by another branch of the Maas, which divides it from the islands of Goree and Overstakke, on the south; and by the German sea on the west; being about 24 miles long, and five broad.
