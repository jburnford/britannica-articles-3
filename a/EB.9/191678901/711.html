VIVIPAROUS

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VIVIPAROUS, in Natural History, an epithet applied to such animals as bring forth their young alive and perfect; in contradistinction to those that lay eggs, which are called oviparous animals.
