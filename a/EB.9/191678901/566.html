VENIRE FACIAS

article · 226 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VENIRE FACIAS, in Law, is a judicial writ lying where two parties plead and come to issue, directed to the sheriff, to cause 12 men of the same neighbourhood to meet and try the same, and to say the truth upon the issue taken.
