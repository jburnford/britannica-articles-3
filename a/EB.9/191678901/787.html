VOMITING

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VOMITING, a retrograde spasmodic motion of the muscular fibres of the oesophagus, stomach, and intestines, attended with strong convulsions of the muscles of the abdomen and diaphragm; which when gentle, create a nausea; when violent, a vomiting.
