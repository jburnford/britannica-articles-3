WELL of a Fishing-vessel

sub_entry · 428 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WELL of a Fishing-vessel, an apartment in the middle of the hold, which is entirely detached from the rest, being lined with lead on every side, and having the bot-

tom thereof penetrated with a competent number of small holes passing also through the ship's floor; so that the salt-water running into the well is always kept as fresh as that in the sea, and yet prevented from communicating itself to the other parts of the hold.
