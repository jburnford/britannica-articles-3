TOPSHAM

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TOPSHAM, a town in Devonshire, in England, seated on the river Exmouth, five miles south-east of Exeter, to which place the river was formerly navigable; but in time of war was choked up deignedly, so that ships are now obliged to load and unload at Topsham. W. Long. 3. 26. N. Lat. 50. 39.
