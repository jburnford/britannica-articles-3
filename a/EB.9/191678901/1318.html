ZICLAG

ZICLAG, or ZIKLAG, in Ancient Geography, a town of the tribe of Simeon, on the borders of the Philistines (Joshua xv. and xix.), but in the hands of the Philistines till David's time (1 Sam. xxvii. and xxx.).
