TREASURER of the Household

sub_entry · 386 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: HOUSEHOLD

TREASURER of the Household, is an officer who, in the absence of the lord-steward, has power, with the comptroller and other officers of the green-cloth and the steward of the Marshalsea, to hear and determine treasons, felonies, and other crimes committed within the king's palace. See HOUSEHOLD.

There is also a treasurer belonging to the establishment of her majesty's household, &c.
