WEIGH

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WEIGH, a weight of cheese, wool, &c. containing 256 pounds avoirdupois. Of corn, the weigh contains 40 bushels; of barley or malt, six quarters. In some places, as Essex, the weigh of cheese is 300 pounds.
