WESTRINGIA

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WESTRINGIA, a genus of plants, formed from eunilia fruticosa, which was discovered by Dr Solander in New Holland. Dr Smith describes it as approaching nearer to rosemary, and places it after teucrium in the class didynamia.
