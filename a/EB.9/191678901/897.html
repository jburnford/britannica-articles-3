WARDEN

article · 272 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

WARDEN, or GUARDIAN, one who has the charge or keeping of any person, or thing, by office. Such is the warden of the Fleet, the keeper of the Fleet prison; who has the charge of the prisoners there, especially such as are committed from the court of chancery for contempt.
