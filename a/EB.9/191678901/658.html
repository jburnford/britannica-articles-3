VILLA FRANCA

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

VILLA FRANCA, the name of several towns; one in Piedmont, three miles east of Nice; another of Catalonia, 18 miles west of Barcelona; a third, the capital of St Michael, one of the Azores; and a fourth, a town of Estremadura in Spain, 57 miles south east of Salamanca.
