TYRANT

article · 302 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TYRANT, among the ancients, denoted simply a king or monarch; but the ill use which several persons invested with that sacred character made of it, has altered the import of the word; and tyrant now conveys the idea of an unjust or cruel prince, who rules in a more despotic manner than the laws allow.
