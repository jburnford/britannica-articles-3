YUCCA

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

YUCCA, ADAM'S NEEDLE, a genus of plants of the class hexandria. The species of this plant are all exceedingly curious in their growth, and are therefore much cultivated in gardens. The Indians make a kind of bread from the roots of this plant.
