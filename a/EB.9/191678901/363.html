TROOP

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

TROOP, a small body of horse or dragoons, about 50 or 60, sometimes more, sometimes less, commanded by a captain, lieutenant, cornet, quarter-master, and three corporals, who are the lowest officers of a troop.
