FENTON, SIR GEOFFROY

article · 347 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FENTON, SIR GEOFFROY, privy counsellor and secretary in Ireland during the reigns of Queen Elizabeth and King James I. is well known for his translation of Guicciardini's History of the Wars of Italy, dedicated to Queen Elizabeth in 1579. He died at Dublin in 1608; after having married his daughter to Mr Boyle, afterward the great earl of Corke.
