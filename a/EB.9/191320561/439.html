FLAIL

article · 373 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FLAIL, an instrument for thrashing corn. It consists of the following parts. 1. The hand-staff, or piece held in the thrasher's hand. 2. The swiple, or that part which strikes out the corn. 3. The caplins, or strong double leathers, made fast to the tops of the hand-staff and swiple. 4. The middle band, being the leather thong or fish skin that ties the caplins together.
