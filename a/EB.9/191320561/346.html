FILLET

article · 213 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FILLET is also used among painters, gilders, &c. for a little rule or reglet of leaf gold, drawn over certain mouldings; or on the edges of frames, panells,

&c. especially when painted white, by way of enrichment.
