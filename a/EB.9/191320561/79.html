FARMER

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FARMER, he that tenants a farm, or is lessee thereof. Also generally every lessee for life, years, or at will, is called farmer. As this word implies no mystery, except it be that of husbandry, husbandman is the proper addition for a farmer.
