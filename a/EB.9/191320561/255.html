FEU-DUTY

article · 265 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FEU-DUTY, in Scots Law, is the annual rent or duty which a vassal, by the tenor of his right, becomes bound to pay his superior.

Feu-Holding, in Scots Law, is that particular tenure by which a vassal is taken bound to pay an annual rent or feu-duty to his superior.
