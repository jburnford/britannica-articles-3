FISHING-LINE

article · 360 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FISHING-LINE, is either made of hair twisted; or silk; or the Indian grass. The best colours are the sorrel, white, and gray; the two last for clear waters, the first for muddy ones. Nor is the pale watery green despicable; this colour is given artificially, by steeping the hair in a liquor made of alum, soot, and the juice of walnut leaves, boiled together.
