FILANDERS

article · 252 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FILANDERS, in falconry, a disease in hawks, &c. consisting of filaments or strings of blood coagulated; and occasioned by a violent rupture of some vein, by which the blood, extravasating, hardens into these figures, and incommodes the reins, hips, &c.
