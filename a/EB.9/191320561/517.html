FLOUNDER, FLUKE, or But. See PLEURONECTES, ICHTHYOLOGY Index.

Flounders may be fished for all day long, either in a swift stream, or in the still deep water; but best in the stream in the months of April, May, June, and July: the most proper baits are all sorts of worms, wasps, and gentles.
