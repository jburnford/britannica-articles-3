FESSE WAYS

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FESSE WAYS, or in FESSE, denotes any thing borne after the manner of a fesse; that is, in a rank across the middle of the shield.

Party per FESSE, implies a parting across the middle of the shield, from side to side, through the fesse point.
