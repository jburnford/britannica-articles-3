PELO DE SE

article · 188 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PELO DE SE, in Law, a person that lays deliberately violent hands on himself, and is the occasion of his untimely death, whether by hanging, drowning, stabbing, shooting, or any other way.
