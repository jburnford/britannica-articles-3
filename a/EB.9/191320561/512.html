FLORY, FLOWERY, or Fleury

sub_entry · 209 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FLORY, FLOWERY, or Fleury, in Heraldry, a cross that has flowers at the end circumflex and turning down; different from the potence, in as much as the latter stretches out more like that which is called patee.
