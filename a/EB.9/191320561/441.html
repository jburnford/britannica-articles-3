FLAKE

article · 215 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FLAKE, in the cod fishery, a sort of scaffold or platform, made of hurdles, and supported by stanchions, used for drying cod fish in Newfoundland. These flakes are usually placed near the shores of fishing harbours.
