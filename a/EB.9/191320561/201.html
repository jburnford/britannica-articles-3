FERETRIUS

article · 254 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FERETRIUS, a surname of Jupiter, à ferendo, because he had assisted the Romans: or à feriendo, because he had conquered their enemies under Romulus. He had a temple at Rome built by Romulus. It was there that the spoils called spolia were always carried.
