FILE

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FILE, in the art of war, a row of soldiers, standing one behind another, which is the depth of the battalion or squadron. The files of a battalion of foot are generally three deep; as are sometimes those of a squadron of horse. The files must be straight and parallel one to another.
