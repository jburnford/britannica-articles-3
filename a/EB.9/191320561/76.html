FARLEU

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FARLEU, money paid by the tenants in the west of England, in lieu of a heriot. In some manors of Devonshire, farleu is often distinguished to be the best goods, as heriot is the best beast, payable at the death of a tenant.
