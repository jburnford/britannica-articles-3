FORAMEN

article · 263 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FORAMEN, in Anatomy, a name given to several apertures or perforations in divers parts of the body; as, 1. The external and internal foramina of the cranium or skull. 2. The foramina in the upper and lower jaw. 3. Foramen lachrymale. 4. Foramen membranae tympani.
