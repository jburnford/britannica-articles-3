FANTASIA

article · 356 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FANTASIA, in the Italian music, signifies fancy and is used for a composition, wherein the composer ties himself to no particular time, but ranges according as his fancy leads, amidst various movements, different airs, &c. This is otherwise called the capricious style: before sonatas were used, there were many of this kind, some of which remain even now.
