FILLET

article · 297 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FILLET, or FILLET, in Architecture, denotes a little square member or ornament used in divers places, and on divers occasions, but generally as a sort of corona over a greater moulding.

The fillet is the same with what the French call reglet, bande, and bandelette; the Italians lista or listella.
