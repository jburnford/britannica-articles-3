FORCER

article · 313 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FORCER, in Mechanics, is properly a piston without a valve. For, by drawing up such a piston, the air is drawn up, and the water follows; then pushing the piston down again, the water, being prevented from descending by the lower valve, is forced up to any height above, by means of a side branch between the two.
