FILICACIA

article · 424 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FILICACIA, VINCENT, a celebrated Italian poet, was born at Florence in 1642. He was a member of the Academy della Crusca and of that of the Arcadi, and became secretary to the duke of Tuscany. He died in 1707. His poems are much esteemed for the delicacy and nobleness of their sentiments. Scipio de Filicacia, his son, had them all printed together, under the title of Poesie Fosiano di Vincenzo da Filicacia, in 1707, 4to.
