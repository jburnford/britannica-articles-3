FALSE Imprisonment

sub_entry · 388 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FALSE Imprisonment, is a trespass committed against a person, by arresting and imprisoning him without just cause, contrary to law; or where a man is unlawfully detained without legal process: and it is al-

so used for a writ which is brought for this trespass. If a person be any way unlawfully detained, it is false imprisonment; and considerable damages are recoverable in those actions.
