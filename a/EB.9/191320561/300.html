FIERI FACIAS

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FIERI FACIAS, in Law, a writ that lies where a person has recovered judgment for debt or damages in the king's courts against one, by which the sheriff is commanded to levy the debt and damages on the defendant's goods and chattels.
