RECKONING

Cross-references: NAVIGATION

RECKONING, or a Ship's RECKONING, in Navigation, is that account whereby at any time it may be known where the ship is, and on what course or courses she is to steer, in order to gain her port; and that account taken from the log-board is called the dead reckoning. See NAVIGATION.
