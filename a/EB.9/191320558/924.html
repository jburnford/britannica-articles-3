RESUSCITATION

article · 307 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RESUSCITATION, the same with resurrection and revivification. See the preceding article and REANIMATION.

The term resuscitation, however, is more particularly used by chemists for the reproducing a mixed body from its ashes; an art to which many have pretended, as to reproduce plants, &c. from their ashes.
