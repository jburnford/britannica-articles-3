REAR

article · 268 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REAR, a term frequently used in composition, to denote something behind, or backwards, in respect of another; in opposition to van.

REAR of an Army, signifies, in general, the hindermost part of an army, battalion, regiment, or squadron; also the ground behind either.
