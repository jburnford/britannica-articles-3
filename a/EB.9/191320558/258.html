PYRMONT

article · 443 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PYRMONT, a town of Lippe in Germany, in the circle of Westphalia, and capital of a country of the same name. It has a castle, kept by a governor, who is under the counts of Waldeck. At a small distance from hence there are mineral waters, which are much esteemed. The Protestants have here the free exercise of their religion. It is seated on the confines of the duchy of Brunswick, 40 miles south-west of Hanover. E Long. 9. 0. N. Lat. 52. 0.
