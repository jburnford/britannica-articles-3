RETRIBUTION

article · 228 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RETRIBUTION, a handsome present, gratuity, or acknowledgement, given instead of a formal salary or hire, to persons employed in affairs that do not so immediately fall under estimation, nor within the ordinary commerce in money.
