RELIGIOUS

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RELIGIOUS, is also used substantially for a person engaged by solemn vows to the monastic life; or a person shut up in a monastery to lead a life of devotion and austerity, under some rule or institution. The male religious we popularly call monks and friars; the female, nuns and canonesses.
