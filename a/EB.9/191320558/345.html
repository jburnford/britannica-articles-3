QUATUORVIR

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

QUATUORVIR, in antiquity, formerly written IIII. VIR, a Roman magistrate, who had three colleagues joined with him in the same administration, and had the care of conducting and settling the colonies sent into the provinces. There were also quatuorviri appointed to inspect and take care of repairs, &c.
