PSEUDO

article · 299 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PSEUDO, from ψεύδος, a Greek term used in the composition of many words to denote false or spurious: as the pseudo-acacia, or bastard acacia; pseudo-fumaria, or bastard-fumitory; pseudo-ruta, or bastard-rue, &c.

We also say, a pseudo-apostle or false apostle; a pseudo-prophet, or false prophet, &c.
