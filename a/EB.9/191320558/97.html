Grand PROVOST of the Constable

sub_entry · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Grand PROVOST of the Constable, a judge who manages proceedings against the soldiers in the army who have committed any crime.

He has four lieutenants distributed throughout the army, called provosts of the army, and particularly provosts in the several regiments.
