PYRENEAN MOUNTAINS

article · 471 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PYRENEAN MOUNTAINS, or PYRENEES, are the mountains which divide France from Spain, and are the most celebrated in Europe, except the Alps. They reach from the Mediterranean sea as far as the ocean, and are about 212 miles in length. They have different names, according to the different places wherein they stand. Some think they are as high as the Alps; but the passages over them are not so difficult, whatever some travellers may think who have not crossed the former.
