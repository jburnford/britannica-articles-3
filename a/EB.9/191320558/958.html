REVELS

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REVELS, entertainments of dancing, masking, acting comedies, farces, &c. anciently very frequent in the inns of court and in noblemen's houses, but now much disused. The officer who has the direction of the revels at court is called the MASTER of the Revels.
