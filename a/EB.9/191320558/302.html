QUANG-PING-FOU

article · 382 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

QUANG-PING-FOU, a city in China, is situated in the northern part of the province of Pe-tcheli, between the provinces of Chang-tong and Ho-nan, and has nine cities of the third class dependent on it; all its plains are well watered by rivers. Among its temples, there

is one dedicated to those men who, as the Chinese pretend, discovered the secret of rendering themselves immortal.
