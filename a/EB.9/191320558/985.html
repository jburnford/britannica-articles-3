RHABDOLOGY

article · 370 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RHABDOLOGY, or RHABDOLOGY, in arithmetic, a name given by Napier to a method of performing some of the more difficult operations of numbers by means of square little rods. Upon these are inscribed the simple numbers; then by shifting them according to certain rules, those operations are performed by simply adding or subtracting the numbers as they stand upon the rods.
