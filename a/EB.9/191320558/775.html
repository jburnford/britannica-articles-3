REGULBIUM

REGULBIUM, or REGULIVUM, (Novitia Imperii); mentioned nowhere else more early: a town of the Cantii, in Britain. Now Reculver, a village on the coast, near the island Thanet, towards the Thames, to the north of Canterbury, (Camden).
