REST, in Poetry

Cross-references: POETRY

REST, in Poetry, is a short pause of the voice in reading, being the same with the caesura, which, in Alexandrine verses, falls on the sixth syllable; but in verses of 10 or 11 syllables, on the fourth. See POETRY, Part III.
