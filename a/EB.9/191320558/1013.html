RHINFELD

article · 527 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RHINFELD, a castle of Germany, in the circle of the Lower Rhine, in a county of the same name. It is

looked upon as one of the most important places seated on the Rhine, as well in regard to its strength as situation. It is near St Gaur, and built on a craggy rock. This fortress commands the whole breadth of the Rhine, and those who pass are always obliged to pay a considerable toll. In the time of war it is of great importance to be masters of this place. It was taken by the French in 1794. E. Long. 7. 43. N. Lat. 50. 3.
