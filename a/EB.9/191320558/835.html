REVERSE

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

REVERSE, INVERTED, in Heraldry, is when any thing is set with the head downwards, or contrary to its natural way of standing. Thus, a chevron reverse is a chevron with the point downwards. They use also the same term when a beast is laid on its back.
