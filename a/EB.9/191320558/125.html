PSATYRIANS

article · 271 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PSATYRIANS, a sect of Arians, who, in the council of Antioch, held in the year 360, maintained that

that the Son was not like the Father as to will; that he was taken from nothing, or made of nothing; and that in God, generation was not to be distinguished from creation.
