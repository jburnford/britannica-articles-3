RESIGNATION

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: MORAL PHILOSOPHY

RESIGNATION, in general, signifies the implicit submission of ourselves, or of something we possess, to the will of another. In a religious sense it signifies a perfect submission, without discontent, to the will of God. See MORAL PHILOSOPHY, No 119.
