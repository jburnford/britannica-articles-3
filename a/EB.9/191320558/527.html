RANGER

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RANGER, a sworn officer of a forest, appointed by the king's letters patent; whose business is to walk through his charge, to drive back the deer out of the park, &c. and to prevent all trespasses within his jurisdiction at the next forest court.
