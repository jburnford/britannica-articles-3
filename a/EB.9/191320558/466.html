RAGULED

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RAGULED, or RAGGED, in Heraldry, jagged or knotted. This term is applied to a cross formed of the trunks of two trees without their branches, of which they show only the stumps. Reguled differs from indented, in that the latter is regular, the former not.
