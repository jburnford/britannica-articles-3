RACONI, a populous town of Italy, in Piedmont, seated in a pleasant plain, on the road from Savillan to Turin, on the rivers Grana and Macra. It belongs to the prince of Carignano, who has a handsome castle here. It is six miles from Savillan, and six from Carignano. E. Long. 7. 46. N. Lat. 44. 39.
