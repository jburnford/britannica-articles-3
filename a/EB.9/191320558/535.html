RAPHAIM

article · 236 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

RAPHAIM, or REPHAIM, (Moses), a name signifying Giants, as they really were, and an actual people too, situated in Babel or Batanea, beyond Jordan, separated from the Zanzumim by the river Jabbok. Also a valley near Jerusalem; Joshua x.
