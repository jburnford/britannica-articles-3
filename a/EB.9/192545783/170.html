ANACROSIS

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANACROSIS, in Antiquity, denotes a part of the Pythian song, wherein the combat of Apollo and Pythion are described.—The anacrosis was the first part, and contained the preparation to the fight.
