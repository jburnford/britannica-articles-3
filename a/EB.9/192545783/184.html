ANAGOGY

article · 230 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANAGOGY, in a more particular sense, denotes the application of the types and allegories of the Old Testament to subjects of the New; thus called, because the veil being here drawn, what before was hidden is exposed to open sight.
