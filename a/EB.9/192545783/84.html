AMPHIMACER

article · 165 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMPHIMACER, in Ancient Poetry, a foot consisting of three syllables, whereof the first and last are long, and that in the middle short; such is the word [ἀμφίμακος].
