AMPHIPRORÆ

article · 179 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMPHIPRORÆ, in the naval affairs of the ancients, vessels with a prow at each end. They were used chiefly in rapid rivers and narrow channels, where it was not easy to tack about.
