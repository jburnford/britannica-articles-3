ANHOLT

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANHOLT, an island of Denmark, in North Jutland, lying in the Categat, eight miles from the coast of Jutland, ten from Seeland, and seven from Halland. It is dangerous for seamen, for which reason there is a lighthouse.
