ANDROMACHE

article · 277 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANDROMACHE, the wife of the valiant Hector, the mother of Astyanax, and daughter of Eëtion king of Thebes in Cilicia. After the death of Hector and the destruction of Troy, she married Pyrrhus; and afterwards Helenus the son of Priam, with whom she reigned over part of Epirus.
