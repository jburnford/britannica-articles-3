ANGELO

ANGELO, Sr., a small but strong town of Italy,
in the Capitanata. There are several other towns and
castles of the same name in Italy, and particularly the
castle of St. Angelo at Rome. E. Long. 15. 36. N.
Lat. 41. 43.
