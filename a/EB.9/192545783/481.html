ANNUAL RENT

article · 311 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANNUAL RENT is used, in Scotts Law, to denote a yearly profit due by a debtor in a sum of money to a creditor for the use of it.

Right of Annuities, in Scotts Law, the original method of burdening lands with a yearly payment for the loan of money, before the taking of interest for money was allowed by statute.
