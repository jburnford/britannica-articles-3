ANADUOMENE VENUS

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANADUOMENE VENUS, in the Grecian Mythology, answered to the Sea Venus in the Roman, and was the appellation given to one of the chief deities of the sea. The most celebrated picture in all antiquity

was that of this goddess by Apelles; and the famous Venus of Medicis is a Sea Venus.
