ANACONDO

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANACONDO, in Natural History, is a name given in the isle of Ceylon to a very large and terrible snake, which often devours the unfortunate traveller alive, and is itself accounted excellent and delicious fare. It is probably the Boa Constrictor.
