ANDROGEUS

article · 388 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANDROGEUS, in Fabulous History, the son of Minos king of Crete, was murdered by the Athenian youth and those of Megara, who envied his being always victor at the Attic games. But Minos having

taken Athens and Megara, obliged the inhabitants to send him an annual tribute of seven young men and as many virgins, to be devoured by the Minotaur; but Theseus delivered them from that tribute.
