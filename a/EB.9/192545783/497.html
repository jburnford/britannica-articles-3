ANOINTERS

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANOINTERS, a religious sect in some parts of England, so called from the ceremony they used of anointing all persons before they admitted them into their church. They founded their opinion of anointing upon the 5th of James, ver. 14. and 15.
