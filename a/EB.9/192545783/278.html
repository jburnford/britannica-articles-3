ANCLAM

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANCLAM, a strong town of Germany, in the circle of Upper Saxony, and duchy of Pomerania, remarkable for its excellent pastures. It is seated on the river Pene. E. Long. 14. 5. N. Lat. 54. 10.
