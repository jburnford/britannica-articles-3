ANASSUS

ANASSUS, or ANAXUS, in Ancient Geography, a river in the territory of Venice, (Pliny); now the Piave, which rising from the mountains of Tyrol, not far from the borders of Carinthia, runs from north to south, through the territories of Cadorina, Belluno, Feltre, and, after running from west to east, through Trevigi, falls into the Adriatic, 13 miles to the south-east of Venice.
