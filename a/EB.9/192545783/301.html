ANDELY

ANDELY, a town of Normandy in France, parted in two by a paved causeway. Here is a fountain to which pilgrims flock from all parts, to be cured of their disorders, on the feast day of the saint to which it is dedicated. It is 20 miles south-east of Rouen,

and five north-west of Paris. E. Long. 1. 30. N. Lat. 49. 20. Andena Anderfon.
