ANNULET is also a narrow flat moulding, which is common to divers places of the columns, as in the bases, capitals, &c. It is the same member which Vitruvius calls a fillet; Palladio, a listil or cinture; Scamozzi, and Mr Brown, a supercilium, list, tinea, eyebrow, square, rabbit. See ARCHITECTURE.
