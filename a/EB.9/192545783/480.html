ANNUAL Leaves

sub_entry · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANNUAL Leaves, are such leaves as come up afresh in the spring, and perish in winter. These stand opposed to Evergreens.

Annual Plants, called also simply annuities, are such as only live their year, i. e. come up in the spring and die again in the autumn; and accordingly are to be recruited every year.
