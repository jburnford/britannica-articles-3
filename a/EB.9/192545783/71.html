AMPELUSIA

AMPELUSIA, in Ancient Geography, a promontory of Mauritania Tingitana, called Cotes by the natives, which is of the same signification with a town of the same name not far from the river Lixus, near the straits of Gibraltar; now Cape Spartel. W. Long. 6. 30. Lat. 36. o.
