AMERSFORT

article · 556 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMERSFORT, a city in the Netherlands, in the province of Utrecht, seated on the river Rens, E. Long. 5. 20. N. Lat. 52. 14. The most remarkable objects are, the town-house; the grand palace, which is triangular; the public walk, planted with trees; and the great church, dedicated to St. George. The land to the east and south of this city is very fruitful; on the north there is nothing but pasture ground, and on the west it is woody. Not far from hence is a mountain called Amersfort-berg, on which is planted a villa of trees, which reaches to Utrecht.
