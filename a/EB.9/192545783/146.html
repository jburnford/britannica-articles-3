ANABOA

ANABOA, a small island situated near the coast of Loango in Africa, in E. Long. 9°, N. Lat. 1°. Here are several fertile valleys, which produce plenty of bananas, oranges, pine apples, lemons, citrons, tamarinds, cocoa nuts, &c. together with vast quantities of cotton. In this island are two high mountains, which, being continually covered with clouds, occasion frequent rains.
