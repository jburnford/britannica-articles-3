ANASTOMOSIS

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANASTOMOSIS, in Anatomy, the opening of the mouths of vessels, in order to discharge their contained fluids. It is likewise used for the communication of two vessels at their extremities; as the inoculation of a vein with a vein, of an artery with an artery, or of an artery with a vein.
