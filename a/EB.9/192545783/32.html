AM-KAS

article · 326 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AM-KAS, in History, a name given to a spacious falcon in the palace of the Great Mogul, where he gives audience to his subjects, and where he appears on solemn festivals with extraordinary magnificence. His throne is supported by six large steps of massy gold, set with rubies, emeralds, and diamonds, estimated at 60,000,000.
