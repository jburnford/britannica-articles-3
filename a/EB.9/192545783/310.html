ANDETRIUM; ANDRETIUM

article · 365 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANDETRIUM; ANDRETIUM (Strabo): ANDRECIUM; ANDRECIUM (Ptolemy): An inland town of Dalmatia. The genuine name is Andetrium (Inscription). It is described as situated near Salonne, on a naturally strong and inaccessible rock, surrounded with deep valleys, with rapid torrents; from which it appears to be the citadel now called Clissa. E. Long. 17. 46. N. Lat. 43. 20.
