ANAMIM, the second son of Mizraim (Gen. x. 13.) Ananim, if we may credit the paraphrast Jonathan the son of Uzziel, peopled the Marcotis; or the Pentapolis of Cyrene, according to the paraphrast of Jerusalem. Bocchart is of opinion, that these Anamims were the people that dwelt in the parts adjacent to the temple of Jupiter Ammon, and in the Nataminitis. Calmet thinks the Anamians and Garamantes to be descended from Ananim.
