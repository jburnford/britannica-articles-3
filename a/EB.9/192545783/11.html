AMETHYSTE, AMETHYST. See BOTANY Index.

AMETHYSTINE is applied, in Antiquity, to a kind of purple garment dyed of the hue of amethyst. In this sense amethystine differed from Tyrian as well as from byacinthine purple, being a kind of medium between both.
