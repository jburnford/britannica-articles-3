ANGUSTICLAVIA

article · 153 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANGUSTICLAVIA, in Roman Antiquity, a tu-
nica embroidered with little purple studs. It was worn
by the Roman knights, as the laticlavia was by the
senators.
