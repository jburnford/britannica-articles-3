ANASUS

article · 302 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANASUS, or ANISUS, in Ancient Geography, now the Ens, a river of Germany; which rising on the borders of the territory of Salzburg; then separating Upper Styria from Upper Austria, and washing the town of Ens, falls, at the distance of a mile below it, into the Danube, in a course from south to north.
