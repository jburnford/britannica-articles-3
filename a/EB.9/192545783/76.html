AMPHIBRACHYS

article · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMPHIBRACHYS, in Greek and Latin Poetry, the name of a foot consisting of three syllables, whereof that in the middle is long, and the other two short; such are the words [ἀβρίχ, ἀμάριχ.]
