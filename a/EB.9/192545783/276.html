ANCIENTY

article · 251 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANCIENTY, in some ancient statutes, is used for eldership or seniority. The elder sister can demand no

Ancillon, more than her other sisters, beside the chief mesne, by reason of her ancienly. This word is used in the statute of Ireland, 14 Henry III.
