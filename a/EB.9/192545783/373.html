ANGERMANNIA

article · 340 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANGERMANNIA, or ANGERMANLAND, a province of Sweden, bounded on the north by Lapland and Bothnia, on the east by the gulf of Bothnia and Medelpad, and on the west by Jemtland and Herjed. It is full of rocks, mountains, and forests; and there is one very high mountain called Scull. It has excellent iron works, and lakes abounding with fish.
