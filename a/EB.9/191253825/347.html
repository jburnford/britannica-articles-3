BRAVO, one of the Cape de Verd islands on the coast of Africa, remarkable for its excellent wines, and inhabited by Portuguese. The land is very high, and consists of mountains which look like pyramids. It abounds in Indian corn, gourds, water melons, potatoes, horses, asses, and hogs. There is also plenty of fish on the

the coast, and the island produces salt-petre. W. Long. 25. 35. N. Lat. 14. 0.
