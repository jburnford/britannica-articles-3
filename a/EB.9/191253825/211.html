BOXHORNUS

article · 394 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOXHORNUS, MARC ZUERUS, a learned critic born at Bergen-op-Zoom in 1612, was professor of eloquence at Leyden, and at length of politics and history in the room of Heinus. He published, 1. Theatrum urbium Hollandiae. 2. Scriptores historiae Augustae, cum notis. 3. Poetae satyrici minores, cum commentis. 4. Notes on Justin, Tacitus; and a great number of other works. He died in 1653, aged 41.
