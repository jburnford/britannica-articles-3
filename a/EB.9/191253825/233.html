BOZOLA, a town of Italy, in the duchy of Mantua, capital of a territory of the same name, and subject to the house of Austria. E. Long. 10. 25. N. Lat. 45. 9.

B QUADRO, QUADRATO, or Durale, in music, called by the French b quarre, from its figure \frac{1}{4}. This is what we call B natural or sharp, in distinction to B mol or flat. See FLAT and SHARP.

If the flat \flat be placed before a note in the thorough bass, it intimates, that its third is to be minor; and if placed with any cypher over a note in the bass, as \flat 6, or \flat 5, &c. it denotes, that the fifth or sixth thereto are to be flat. But if the quadro \frac{1}{4} be placed over any note, or with a cypher, in the thorough bass, it has the contrary effect; for thereby the note or interval thereto is raised to its natural order.
