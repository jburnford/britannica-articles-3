BRENNAGE

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRENNAGE, BRENNAGIUM, in middle age writers, a kind of tribute paid in lieu of bran, or bran itself, which the tenants were obliged to furnish for the support of the lord's hounds. The word is also written brennage, brennagium, and brennagie, bernagium, brennaticum, and brennaticum.
