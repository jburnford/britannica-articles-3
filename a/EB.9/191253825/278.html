BRAE-MAR

article · 174 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRAE-MAR, a mountainous territory of Scotland, in the shire of Aberdeen, where the last earl of Mar began to raise a rebellion in 1715. It is 27 miles north-west of Aberdeen.
