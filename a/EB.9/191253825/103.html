BOSSÉ

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOSSÉ, ABRAHAM, an able engraver, born at Tours, was well skilled in perspective and architecture. He wrote two treatises, which are esteemed; the one on the manner of designing, and the other upon engraving.
