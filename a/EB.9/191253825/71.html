BOROZAIL

article · 280 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOROZAIL, or the zeal of the Ethiopians, a disease epidemic in the countries about the river Senegal. It principally affects the pudenda, but is different from the lues venerea. It owes its rise to excessive venery; in the men this distemper is called asab, and in women asabatus.
