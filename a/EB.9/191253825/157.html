BOULUKE

article · 295 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BOULUKE, in the military orders of the Turks, a body of the janizaries, with an officer in the place of a colonel at their head, sent upon some particular enterprise; they are selected out of the body for this, and as soon as the business is over, are received again into their former companies.
