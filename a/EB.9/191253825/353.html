BRAY

BRAY, a port town of Ireland, in the county of Wicklow, and province of Leinster, seated on St George's channel, eight miles south of Dublin. W. Long. 6. 16. N. Lat. 53. 8.

BRAY fur Seine, a town of France, in Champagne, and in Senonnois, on the confines of Brie. E. Long. 2. 15. N. Lat. 48. 35.
