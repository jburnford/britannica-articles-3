POLERON, one of the Banda or Nutmeg islands in the East Indies. This was one of those space islands which put themselves under the protection of the English, and voluntarily acknowledged James I. king of England for their sovereign; for which reason the natives of this and the rest of the islands were murdered or driven thence by the Dutch, together with the English.
