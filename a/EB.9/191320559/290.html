POULADUFF

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POULADUFF, two large and remarkable cavities, about a mile west of Ross, in the county of Cork, and province of Munster, in Ireland, 80 yards deep, in which the sea flows by subterraneous passages. They are called East and West Pouladuff.
