PREPENSED

article · 256 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PREPENSED, in Law, denotes fore-thought. In which sense we say prepenst malice, &c. If, when a man is slain upon a sudden quarrel, there were malice prepenst formerly between them, it makes it murder; and, as it is called in some statutes, prepenst murder.
