PRISTIS

Cross-references: SQUALUS

PRISTIS, the sawfish, is generally considered as a species of the squalus or shark genus, comprehending under it several varieties. See SQUALUS, ICHTHOLOGY Index. But Mr Latham is of opinion that it ought to be considered as a distinct genus, and that the characteristics of the several varieties are sufficient to constitute distinct species.
