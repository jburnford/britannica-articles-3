PRÆSIDIUM

PRÆSIDIUM (Notitia), a town of the Cornavii in Britain. Now thought to be Warwick (Camden).—Another of Corsica (Antonine), 30 miles to the south of Aleria.—A third Præſidium furnished Julium, in Bætica (Pliny).
