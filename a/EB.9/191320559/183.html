PORENTRU

article · 310 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PORENTRU, is a town of Switzerland, in Elsgaw, and capital of the territory of the bishop of Basle, which is distinguished only by its castle and cathedral. The bishop was formerly a prince of the empire. It is seated on the river Halle, near Mount Jura, 22 miles south of Basle. E. Long. 7. 2. N. Lat. 47. 34.
