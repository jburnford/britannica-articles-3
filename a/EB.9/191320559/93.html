POLYGALA, MILKWORT: a genus of plants belonging to the diadelphia class; and in the natural me-

thod ranking under the 33d order, Lomentaceae. See Polygonum BOTANY Index.

POLYGAMIA (πολυ many, and γαμια marriage), is a term expressing an intercommunication of sexes, and is applied by Linnaeus both to plants and flowers. A polygamous plant is that which bears both hermaphrodite flowers, and male or female, or both.
