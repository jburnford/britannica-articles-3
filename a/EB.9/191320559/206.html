PORTA-AUGUSTA

PORTA-AUGUSTA, in Ancient Geography, mentioned only by Ptolemy; a town of the Vaccei in the Hither Spain; thought by some to be Torre Quemada, in Old Castile; by others Los Valones, a village between Burgos and Torre Quemada.
