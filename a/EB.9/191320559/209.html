PORTATE

article · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PORTATE, or a Croft PORTATE, in Heraldry, a cross which does not stand upright, as crosses generally do; but lies across the escutcheon in bend, as if it were carried on a man's shoulder.
