POLESIA

article · 254 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POLESIA, a province of Poland, bounded by Prussia and Proper Lithuania on the north, and by Volhnia on the south. It is one of the palatinates of Lithuania, and is commonly called Brescia, and its capital is of this name. It is full of forests and lakes.
