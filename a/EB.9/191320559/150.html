PONT St ESPRIT

sub_entry · 531 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PONT St ESPRIT, is a town of France, in Languedoc, in the diocese of Uzès. It is seated on the river Rhone, over which is one of the finest bridges in France. It is 840 yards long, and consists of 26 arches. Each pier is pierced with an aperture, in order to facilitate the passage of the water when the river is high. The town is large, but the streets are narrow and ill built. It formerly contained several churches and convents. It is 17 miles south of Viviers, and 55 north-east of Montpellier. E. Long. 4. 46. N. Lat. 44. 13.
