POPA-MADRE

POPA-MADRE, is a town of South America, in Terra Firma. In this place there is a convent and chapel dedicated to the Virgin Mary, to whose image the Spaniards in those parts go in pilgrimage, particularly those who have been at sea. It is seated on a high mountain, 30 miles east of Carthagena. W. Long. 74. 32. N. Lat. 10. 15.
