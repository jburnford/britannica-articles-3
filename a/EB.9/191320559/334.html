PREBEND

PREBEND, the maintenance a prebendary receives out of the estate of a cathedral or collegiate church. Prebends are distinguished into simple and dignitary: a simple prebend has no more than the revenue for its support; but a prebend with dignity has always a jurisdiction annexed to it.
