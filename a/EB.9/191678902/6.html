MATRICES

article · 360 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MATRICES, used in coining, are pieces of steel in form of dies, wherein are engraved the several figures, arms, characters, legends, &c. wherewith the species are to be stamped. The engraving is performed with several puncheons, which being formed in relievo, or prominent, when struck on the metal, make an indented impression, which the French call en creux.
