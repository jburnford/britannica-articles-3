MAXIMUM

article · 381 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAXIMUM, in Mathematics, denotes the greatest quantity attainable in any given case.

If a quantity conceived to be generated by motion increases or decreases till it arrives at a certain magnitude or position, and then, on the contrary, grows greater or lesser, and it be required to determine the said magnitude or position, the question is called a problem de maximis et minimis.
