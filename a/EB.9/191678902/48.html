MAXIMILIAN I

article · 405 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAXIMILIAN I. emperor of Germany, signified himself against the French while he was king of the Romans, and after he was emperor entered into the army of Henry VIII. of England as a volunteer against that nation: he was a protector of learned men, and abolished an iniquitous tribunal, styled Judicium occultum Westphalia; he composed some poems, and the memoirs of his own life. He died in 1519, aged 60.
