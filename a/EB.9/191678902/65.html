MAZAGAN

article · 287 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

MAZAGAN, a strong place of Africa in the kingdom of Morocco, and on the frontiers of the province of Daguela. It was fortified by the Portuguese, and besieged by the king of Morocco with 200,000 men in 1562, but to no purpose. It is situated near the sea. W. Long. 8. 15. N. Lat. 33. 12.
