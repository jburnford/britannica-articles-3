MEDIASTINUM

Cross-references: ANATOMY

MEDIASTINUM, in Anatomy, a double membrane, formed by a duplicature of the pleura; serving to divide the thorax and the lungs into two parts, and to sustain the viscera, and prevent their falling from one side of the thorax to the other. See ANATOMY, No 117.
