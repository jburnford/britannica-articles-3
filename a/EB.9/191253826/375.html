BUNDLE

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUNDLE, a collection of things wrapped up together. Of baste-ropes, harness-plates, and glovers knives, ten make a bundle; of Hamburg yarn, twenty skeans; of basket rods, three feet the band.
