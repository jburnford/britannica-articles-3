BREVIATOR

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BREVIATOR, an officer under the eastern empire, whose business it was to write and translate briefs.—At Rome those are styled breviators, or abbreviators, who dictate and draw up the pope's briefs.
