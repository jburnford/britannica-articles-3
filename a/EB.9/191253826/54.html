BRIGANTES

BRIGANTES, (Tacitus), a people of Britain, reaching from sea to sea, the whole breadth of the island (Ptolemy). Now Yorkshire, Lancashire, Durham, Westmorland, and Cumberland (Camden). Also a people of Ireland, of uncertain position.
