BUNIAS, in Botany: A genus of the 39th natural order, Siliqueosae, belonging to the tetradymania class of plants, for which there is no English name. The filicula is deciduous, four-sided, mucronate, or flagellated with unequal pointed angles. There are eight species; all of them annual plants, but none of them possessed of any remarkable property.
