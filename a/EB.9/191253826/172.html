POOR BROTHERS

sub_entry · 620 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

POOR BROTHERS, in the charity-house, a denomination give to decayed gentlemen, to the number of 80, who are subsisted with diet, clothing, and lodging, on the establishment. The poor brothers are to be gentlemen by descent, come to poverty, or decayed merchants, soldiers, or officers of the king's household. The conditions of admission are, that they have no estate.
