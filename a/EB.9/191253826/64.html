BRIGNOLES

BRIGNOLES, a town of France, in the department of Var, formerly Provence, famous for its prunes. It is seated among mountains, in a pleasant country, 275 miles S. S. E. of Paris. E. Long. 6. 15. N. Lat. 43. 24.
