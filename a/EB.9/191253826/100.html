BRITANNICUS

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BRITANNICUS, son of the emperor Claudius by Messalina, was excluded from the empire after his father had married Agrippina; who put her son Nero on the throne, and caused Britannicus to be poisoned A. D. 55.
