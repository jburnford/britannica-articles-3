BURGAUDINE

article · 298 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BURGAUDINE, the name given by the French artificers to what we call mother-of-pearl. In their works, they do not use the common naere-shell for this, but the lining of the American burgau. Hence some

call the mother-of-pearl burgaudine, and others the burgaudine mother-of-pearl. Parplett
