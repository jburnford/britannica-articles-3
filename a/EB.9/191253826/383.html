BUNTZEI

BUNTZEI, or BUNTZLAU, a town of Silesia, in the duchy of Jauer. The greatest part of the houses are built with stone, and there were formerly rich mines in the neighbourhood. It is in the common road to Leipzig; and the trade is in earthen ware, of which great quantities are made. E. Long. 15. 30. N. Lat. 51. 12.
