BUDINUS

article · 223 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUDINUS, in Ancient Geography, a mountain of Sarmatia Europaea, from which the more northern spring of the Borysthenes is said to take its rise, according to Ptolemy. But this is contradicted by later accounts. Now Podolia.
