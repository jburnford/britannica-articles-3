FULMINATION

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FULMINATION, in the Romish canon law, a sentence of a bishop, official, or other ecclesiastical appointed by the pope, by which it is decreed that some bull sent from the pope shall be executed.
