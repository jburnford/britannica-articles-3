To FRASER a Battalion

sub_entry · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

To FRASER a Battalion, is to line the musqueteers round with pikes, that in case they should be charged with a body of horse, the pikes being presented, may cover the musqueteers from the shock, and serve as a barricade.
