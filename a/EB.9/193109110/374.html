FURNITURE

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FURNITURE, a term in dialling, which denotes certain additional points and lines drawn on a dial, by way of ornament, such as the signs of the zodiac, length of days, parallels of declination, azimuths, points of the compass, meridians of chief cities, Babylon, Jewish, or Italian hours, &c.
