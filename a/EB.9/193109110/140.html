FRANCFORT ON THE ODER, a rich and handsome town of Germany, in the middle marche of Brandenburg, formerly imperial, but now subject to the king of Prussia. It is remarkable for three great fairs, and a celebrated university. It lies about 45 miles south east of Berlin, and 72 south of Stetin. E. Long. 14. 39. N. Lat. 52. 23.
