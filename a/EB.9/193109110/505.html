GALLINACEUS LAPIS

article · 195 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALLINACEUS LAPIS, a glossy mineral substance which is supposed by some to be produced by the operation of volcanic fires; and is thought to be the same with the lapis obsidianus of the ancients.
