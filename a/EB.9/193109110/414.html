GAD, in Ancient Geography, a district of the Transjordan Palestine, situated between Gilead and the kingdom of Bashan to the north, and the kingdom of Amoritites, to the south; having the Jordan to the west, and

bounded by various peoples on the east; so called from a tribe of that name.
