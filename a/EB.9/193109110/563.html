GARCON

article · 370 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GARCON, or GARSOON, a French term, literally signifying a boy or male child any time before his marriage.—It is also applied to divers inferior officers, among us called groom, gargones. Thus all the servants in the French king's chambers, wardrobe, &c. who do the lesser offices thereof under the proper officers, are called garçons de la chambre, de la garde-robe, &c.
