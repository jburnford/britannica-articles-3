FRYING-PAN

article · 264 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FRYING-PAN, a dangerous shoal, which has received this appellation from its figure. It is situated at the entrance of Cape Fear river, in North Carolina, the southern part of which is in 33° 22' N. Lat. 24 miles south-east by south of the light-house on Bald Head.
