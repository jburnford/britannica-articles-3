GADARENORUM AGER, in Ancient Geography, the country of the Gadarenes, called by Matthew the country of the Gergesenes, because it was a district that lay between Gadara and Gergesa, otherwise called Gergesa, both which lay within the Decapolis on the other side Jordan.
