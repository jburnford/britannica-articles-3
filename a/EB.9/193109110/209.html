FREEDOM of the Will

sub_entry · 317 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FREEDOM of the Will, that power or faculty of the mind, whereby it is capable of acting or not acting, choosing or rejecting whatever it judges proper. Of this every man must be sensible, who finds in himself a power to begin or forbear, continue or end several actions, barely by a thought or preference of the mind.
