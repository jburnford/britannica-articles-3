FURTHCOMING, in Law, the name of an action competent

Furuncle competent to any person who has used arrestment in the hands of his debtor's creditor, for having the subject arrested declared his property.

Furuncle, or Bull, in Surgery, a small resisting tumour, with inflammation, redness, and great pain, arising in the adipose membrane, under the skin. See Surgery Index.
