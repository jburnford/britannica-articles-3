GALLICISM

article · 227 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALLICISM, a mode of speech peculiar to the French language, and contrary to the rules of grammar in other languages. With us it is used to denote such phrases or modes of speech in English as are formed after the French idiom.
