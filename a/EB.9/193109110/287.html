FRONTISPICE

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FRONTISPICE, in Architecture, the principal face of a fine building. The word is formed of the Latin frontispicium, q.d. frontis hominis inspectio. Hence also, by a figure, we say, the frontispiece of a book; meaning an ornament with an engraven title on the first page.
