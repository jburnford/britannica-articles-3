FORGE

article · 451 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FORGE, in the train of artillery, is generally called a travelling forge, and may not be improperly called a portable smith's shop: at this forge all manner of smith's work is made, and it can be used upon a march as well as in camp. Formerly they were very ill contrived, with two wheels only, and wooden supporters to prop the forge for working when in the park. Of late years they are made with four wheels, which answers their purpose much better.
