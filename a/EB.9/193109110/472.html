GALICIA

article · 445 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALICIA, a province of Spain, bounded on the north and west by the ocean, on the south by Portugal, and on the east by Asturias and the kingdom of Leon. The air is temperate along the coast; but, in other places, it is cold and moist. It is but thin of people; and the produce is wine, flax, and citrons: here also are good pastures, copper, and lead; and the forests yield wood for building of ships. St Jago di Compostella is the capital town.
