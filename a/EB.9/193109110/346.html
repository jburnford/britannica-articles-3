FUNCTION

FUNCTION, a term used in analytics for an algebraical expression any how compounded of a certain letter or quantity with other quantities or numbers; and the expression is said to be a function of that letter or quantity. Thus a=4x, or a \times 3x^2, or 2x-a\sqrt{a^2-x^2}, or x^6, or x^8, is each of them a function of the quantity x.
