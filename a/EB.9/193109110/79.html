FOSSA MAGNA

Cross-references: ANATOMY

FOSSA MAGNA, or NAVICULARIS, is an oblong cavity, forming the inside of the pubendum muliebre, and which presents itself upon opening the labia; and in the middle whereof are the caruncula myrtiformes. See ANATOMY.
