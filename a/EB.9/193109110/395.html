FUSTIGATIO

article · 174 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FUSTIGATIO, in the Roman customs, a punishment inflicted by beating with a cudgel. This punishment was peculiar to freemen; for the slaves were scourged or lashed with whips.
