FRIGORIFIC

Cross-references: COLD

FRIGORIFIC, in Physiology, small particles of matter, which, according to Gassendus and others, being actually and essentially cold, and penetrating other bodies, produce in them that quality which is called cold, or, according to others, merely the absence or diminution of the particles of heat.
