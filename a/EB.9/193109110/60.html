(A) The hours of the Chinese are double ours: the hour yn begins at three in the morning and ends at five; ouer begins at three in the afternoon and ends at five.

Formulary: two quantities whose sum is s, and difference d; and
\frac{1}{2}s - \frac{1}{2}d is the formula, or general value, for the less quantity. Also \sqrt{dx-x^2}, is the formula, or general value, of the ordinate to a circle, whose diameter is d, and absciss x.
