GALENA

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALENA, a name given by mineralogists to a species of lead ore. It was also the original name given by Andromachus to the theriaca, from its effect in bringing on a pleasing calm over the blood and spirits on taking it.
