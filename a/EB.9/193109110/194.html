FREDERICKSBURG, a fort and colony of Brandenburg, on the gold coast of Guinea, in Africa, near Cape Threepoints, and about 75 miles from

Cape Coast. It mounts 46 pieces of cannon on four Frederick-batteries; and formerly belonged to the Prussians, but is now subject to Denmark. W. Long. 1. 15. N. Lat. 4. 30. Frederickshall
