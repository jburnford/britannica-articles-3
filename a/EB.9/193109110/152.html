FRANK, or Franc, an ancient coin, either of gold or silver, struck and current in France. The value of the gold franc was something more than that of the gold crown: this coin has been long out of use, though the term is still retained as the name of a money of account; in which sense it is equivalent to the livre, or 20 sols.
