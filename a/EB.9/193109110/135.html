FRAME

Cross-references: FOUNDRY

FRAME, among founders, a kind of ledge enclosing a board; which, being filled with wetted sand, serves as a mould to cast their works in. See FOUNDRY.

FRAME is more particularly used for a sort of loom, wherein artificers stretch their linens, silks, stuffs, &c. to be embroidered, quilted, or the like.
