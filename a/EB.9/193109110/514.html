GALLO

article · 338 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

GALLO, an island of the South sea, near the sea-coast of Peru, in South America, which was the first place possessed by the Spaniards when they attempted the conquest of Peru; it is also the place where the hucañiers used to come for wood and water, and to refit their vessels when they were in these parts. W. Long. 88. 0. N. Lat. 2. 30.
