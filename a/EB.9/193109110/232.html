FRETTS

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FRETTS, in Mineralogy, a term used by our miners to express the worn side of the banks of the rivers in mine countries, where they search for the flood stones or grews walked down from the hills, in order from thence to trace out the running of the flood up to the mine.
