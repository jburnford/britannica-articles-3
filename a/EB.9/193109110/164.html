FRANKFORT

FRANKFORT, the name of several townships in different places of North America; such as Frankfort, a township in Hancock, and district of Maine, with a few

houses regularly built. It contains 891 inhabitants, and lies about 238 miles north-east of Boston. Frankfort, a thriving village in Philadelphia; the name of another in Hampshire, of one in Virginia, and the name of the metropolis of Kentucky.
