FRIGATOON

article · 163 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FRIGATOON, a Venetian vessel, commonly used in the Adriatic, built with a square stern, and without any foremast, having only a mainmast, mizzenmast, and bowsprit.
