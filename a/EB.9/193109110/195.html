FREDERICKSHALL

article · 272 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FREDERICKSHALL, or FREDERICKSTADT, a strong town of Norway, in the prefecture of Agerhuys, where Charles XII. king of Sweden was killed by a mulket ball in 1718, when he was besieging this town. It is seated on the coast of the Catagate, in E. Long. 10. 45. N. Lat. 59. 2.
