SAGE, Alain Rene, an ingenious French romance-writer, was born at Ruys in Brittany in the year 1667. He had a fine flow of imagination, was a complete master of the French and Spanish languages, and wrote several admired romances in imitation of the Spanish authors.

Sage. thors. These were, The Bachelor of Salamanca, 2 vols. 12mo; New Adventures of Don Quixote, 2 vols. 12mo; The Devil on Two Sticks, 2 vols. 12mo; and Gil Blas, 4 vols. 12mo. He produced also some comedies, and other humorous pieces. This ingenious author died in year 1747, in the vicinity of Paris, where he supported himself by writing.
