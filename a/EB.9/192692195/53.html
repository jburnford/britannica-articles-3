SACRE, or SAKER, in Ornithology, the name of a species of falcon, called by authors falco sacer, and differently described by different authors, but by all agreed to be an extremely bold and active bird. It is a native

Sacred Sacrifice. of the northern regions of Europe; and a variety called by some writers the speckled partridge hawk is found at Hudson's bay, North America.
