SASHES

article · 355 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SASHES, in military dress, are badges of distinction worn by the officers of most nations, either round their

waist or over their shoulders. Those for the British army were made of crimson silk; for the Imperial army crimson and gold; for the Prussian army black silk and silver; the Hanoverians yellow silk; the Portuguese crimson silk with blue tassels.
