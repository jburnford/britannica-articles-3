SARCOCELE, in Surgery, a spurious rupture or hernia, wherein the testicle is considerably tumefied or

indurated, like a scirrhus, or much enlarged by a fleshy excrescence, which is frequently attended with acute pains, so as to degenerate at last into a cancerous disposition. See SURGERY.
