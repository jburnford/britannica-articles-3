SANTA CRUZ DE LA SIERRA

SANTA CRUZ DE LA SIERRA, a town of South America, and capital of a province of that name in Peru, and in the audience of Los Charcas, with a bishop's see. It is seated at the foot of a mountain, in a country abounding in good fruits, on the river Guapay. W. Long. 59. 35. S. Lat. 20. 40.
