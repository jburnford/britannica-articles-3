SANTAREN

article · 317 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SANTAREN, a handsome town of Portugal in Estremadura, seated on a mountain near the river Tajo, 55 miles N. E. from Lisbon, in a country very fertile in wheat, wine, and oil. They get in their harvest here two months after they have sown their corn. It was taken from the Moors in 1447. W. Long. 8. 25. N. Lat. 39. 2.
