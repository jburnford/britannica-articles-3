SAGITTARIA, ARROW-HEAD, a genus of plants belonging to the monococia class; and in the natural method ranking under the fifth order, Tripetaloides. See BOTANY Index.—A bulb which is formed at the lower part

Sagittarius part of the root of a species of this plant, constitutes a considerable part of the food of the Chinese; and upon that account they cultivate it.
