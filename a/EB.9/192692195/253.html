SAPPERS

article · 424 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SAPPERS, are soldiers belonging to the royal artillery, whose business it is to work at the saps, for which they have an extraordinary pay. A brigade of sappers generally consists of eight men, divided equally into two parties;
