SANTERRE

article · 244 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SANTERRE, the former name of a small territory of France, in Picardy; bounded on the north by Cambresis, on the east by Vermandois, on the west by Amiens, and on the south by the river Somme. It is very fertile, and the capital town is Peronne.
