SALSES

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SALSES, a very strong castle of France, in Roussillon, on the confines of Languedoc. It was taken from the Spaniards by the French in 1642; and is seated on a lake of the same name, among mountains, 10 miles north of Perpignan. E. Long. 3. 0. N. Lat. 43. 35.
