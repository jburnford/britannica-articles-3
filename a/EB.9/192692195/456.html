SCIROCHO

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCIROCHO, or SIROCCO, a name generally given in Italy to every unfavourable wind. In the south-west it is applied to the hot suffocating blasts from Africa, and in the north-east it means the cold bleak winds from the Alps.
