SCHWARTZEMBERG

SCHWARTZEMBERG, a town of Germany, in the circle of Franconia, and capital of a principality of the same name. The castle is seated on the river Lec, 5 miles north-west of Nuremberg, and 20 east of Wurtzburg, subject to its own prince. E. Long. 10. 27. N. Lat. 49. 43.
