SANIZO, RAPHAEL. See RAPHAEL.
SAO, a territory, called a kingdom, of Africa, on the Gold coast of Guinea, hardly two miles in length along the shore. It produces abundance of Indian corn, yams, potatoes, palm-wine, and oil. The inhabitants are very treacherous, and there is no dealing with them without great caution. It contains several villages, of which Sabo is the principal.
