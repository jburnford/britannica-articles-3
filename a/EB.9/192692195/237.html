SANTEN

article · 430 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SANTEN, a town of Germany, in the circle of Westphalia, and in the duchy of Cleves. It is seated on the Rhine, 15 miles S. E. from Cleves. It has a handsome church belonging to the Roman Catholics, wherein is an image of the Virgin Mary, which it is pretended performs a great many miracles. Here the fine walks begin that run as far as Wekel, from which it is five miles distant to the north-west. E. Long. 6. 33. N. Lat. 51. 38.
