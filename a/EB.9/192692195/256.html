SAPPHO

article · 442 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SAPPHO, a famous poetess of antiquity, who for her excellence in her art has been called the Tenth Muse, was born at Mitylene, in the isle of Lesbos, about 610 years before Christ. She was contemporary with Sestichorus and Alceus; which last was her countryman, and some think her suitor. A verse of this poet, in which he insinuates to her his passion, is preserved in Aristotle, Rhet. lib. i. cap. 9. together with the fair damsel's answer.
