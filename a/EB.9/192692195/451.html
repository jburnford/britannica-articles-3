SCIOPTIC

article · 246 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SCIOPTIC, or SCIOPTIC BALL, a sphere or globe of wood, with a circular perforation, where a lens is placed. It is so fitted, that, like the eye of an animal, it may be turned round every way, to be used in making experiments of the darkened room.
