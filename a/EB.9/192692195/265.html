SARCASM

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SARCASM, in Rhetoric, a keen bitter expression which has the true point of satire, by which the orator scoffs and insults his enemy: such as that of the Jews to our Saviour; "He saved others, himself he cannot save."
