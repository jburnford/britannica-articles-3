SARPLAR OF WOOL

SARPLAR OF WOOL, a quantity of wool, otherwise called a pocket, or half-sack; a sack containing 80 tod; a tod two stone; and a stone 14 pounds. In Scotland it is called farplath, and contains 80 stone.
