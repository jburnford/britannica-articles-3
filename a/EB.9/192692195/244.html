SAONE

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SAONE, a considerable river of France, which has its source in Mount Vosgues, near Darney; runs through the Franche Comte, Burgundy, Beaujolais; and falls into the Rhone at Lyons. It passes by Gray, Chalons, and Malcon.
