SARRASIN

article · 194 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SARRASIN, or SARRAZIN, in fortification, a kind of portcullis, otherwise called a herse, which is hung with ropes over the gate of a town or fortifications, to be let fall in case of a surprise.
