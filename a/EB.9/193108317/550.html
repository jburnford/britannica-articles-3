FOOT-STICKS

article · 285 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

FOOT-STICKS, in Printing, slips of wood that lie between the foot of the page and the ches, to which they are wedged fast by the quoin, to keep the form firm, in conjunction with the side-sticks, which are placed at the side of the page, and fixed in the same manner by means of quoin.
