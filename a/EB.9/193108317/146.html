SOLOMON

article · 314 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SOLOMON, the son of David king of Israel, renowned in Scripture for his wisdom, riches, and magnificent temple and other buildings. Towards the end of his life he fulfilled all his former glory by his apostasy from God; from which cause vengeance was denounced against his house and nation. He died about 975 B. C.
