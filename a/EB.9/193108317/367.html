SPRING

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SPRING, in Mechanics, denotes a thin piece of tempered steel, or other elastic substance, which being wound up serves to put machines in motion by its elasticity, or endeavours to unbend itself; such is the spring of a watch, clock, or the like.
