SUBPOENA ad testificandum

sub_entry · 351 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUBPOENA ad testificandum, a writ or process to bring in witnesses to give their testimony. If a witness on being served with this process does not appear, the court will issue an attachment against him; or a party, plaintiff or defendant, injured by his non-attendance, may maintain an action against the witness. See Blackst. Com. vol. viii. p. 369.
