SPHERE

SPHERE, is a solid contained under one uniform round surface, every point of which is equally distant from a certain point in the middle called its centre; and is formed by the revolution of a semicircle about its diameter. See GEOMETRY.

Projection of the SPHERE. See PROJECTION.
