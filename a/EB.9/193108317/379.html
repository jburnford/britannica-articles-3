SPUNGING

article · 197 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SPUNGING, in Gunnery, the cleaning of the inside of a gun with a sponge, in order to prevent any sparks of fire from remaining in it, which would endanger the life of him that should load it again.
