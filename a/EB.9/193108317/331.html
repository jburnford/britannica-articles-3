SPIRE

article · 231 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SPIRE, in Architecture, was used by the ancients for the base of a column, and sometimes for the atragal or tore; but among the moderns it denotes a sloop that continually diminishes as it ascends, whether conically or pyramidally.
