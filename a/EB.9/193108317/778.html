SUCCOTH

article · 267 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SUCCOTH, in Ancient Geography, a town which lay between the brook Jabbok and the river Jordan, where Jacob fixed his tents. There was another Succoth, where the Israelites first encamped after their departure from Ramefes towards the Red sea. Succoth signifies tents.
