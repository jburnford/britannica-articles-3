STIMULI

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STIMULI, in Botany, a species of armature or offensive weapon, with which some plants, as nettle, castan-

da, scalypha, and tragia, are furnished. Their use, says Linnaeus, is by their venomous punctures to keep off naked animals that would approach to hurt them.
