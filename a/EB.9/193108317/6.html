SLEUT-HOUND

article · 250 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SLEUT-HOUND, the ancient Scots name of the blood-hound. The word is from the Saxon flot, "the impression that a deer leaves of its foot in the mire," and hound, "a dog"; so they derive their name from following the track. See the article BLOOD-HOUND.
