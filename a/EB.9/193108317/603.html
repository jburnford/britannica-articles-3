STOLBERG

STOLBERG, a small town of Germany, in the circle of Upper Saxony, and territory of Thuringia, of which it is the capital place. It is situated between two mountains, 50 miles north-west of Leipzig. E. Long. 11. S. N. Lat. 51. 42.
