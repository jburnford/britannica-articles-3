STEREOMETRY, Στερεομετρία, formed of στερεο solid, and μετρεο measure, that part of geometry which teaches how to measure solid bodies, i. e. to find the solidity or solid contents of bodies; as globes, cylinders, cubes, vessels, ships, &c.
