STALACTITES

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STALACTITES, in Mineralogy, crystalline spars formed into oblong, conical, round, or irregular bodies, composed of various crusts, and usually found hanging in form of icicles from the roofs of grottoes, &c.
