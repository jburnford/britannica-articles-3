SOFFITA, or Soffiti

sub_entry · 398 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SOFFITA, or Soffiti, is also used for the underside or face of an architrave; and more particularly for that of the corona or larmier, which the ancients called lacunar, the French plafond, and we usually the drap. It is enriched with compartments of roses; and in the Doric order has 18 drops, disposed in three ranks, six in each, placed to the right of the guttæ, at the bottom of the triglyphs.
