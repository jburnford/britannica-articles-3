STERNOCOSTALES

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STERNOCOSTALES, commonly called the muſculi triangulares ſterni, in Anatomy, are five pairs of fleſhy planes, diſpoſed more or leſs obliquely on each ſide the ſternum, on the inſides of the cartilages of the ſecond, third, fourth, fifth, and ſixth true ribs.
