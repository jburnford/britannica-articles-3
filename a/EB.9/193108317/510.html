STEMSON

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

STEMSON, in a ship, an arching piece of timber fixed within the apron, to reinforce the scarf thereof, in the same manner as the apron supports the scarf of the stern. In large ships it is usually formed of two pieces.
