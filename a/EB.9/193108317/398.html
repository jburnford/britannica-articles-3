SQUARE

article · 377 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SQUARE, among Mechanics, an instrument consisting of two rules or branches, fastened perpendicularly at one end of their extremities, so as to form a right angle. It is of great use in the description and mensuration of right angles, and laying down perpendiculars.

T SQUARE, or Tee Square, an instrument used in drawing, so called from its resemblance to the capital letter T.
