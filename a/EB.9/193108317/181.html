SORBONNE

article · 319 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SORBONNE, is also used in general for the whole faculty of theology at Paris; as the assemblies of the whole body are held in the house of the Sorbonne; and the bachelors of the other houses of the faculty, as the house of Navarre, &c. come hither to hold their sorbonique, or act for being admitted doctor in divinity.
