SOLEMN

article · 218 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

SOLEMN, something performed with much pomp, ceremony, and expence. Thus we say, solemn feasts, solemn funerals, solemn games, &c.—In law, solemn signifies something authentic, or what is clothed in all its formalities.
