APOPHASIS

article · 249 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APOPHASIS, a figure in Rhetoric, by which the orator, speaking ironically, seems to wave what he would plainly insinuate: as, Neither will I mention those things; which, if I should, you notwithstanding could neither consult, nor speak against them.
