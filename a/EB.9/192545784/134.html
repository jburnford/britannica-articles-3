ANTIMERIA

article · 187 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTIMERIA, in Grammar, a figure whereby one part of speech is used for another: e. g. celle suam cuique est, for voluntas sua cuique est; also, populus late rex, for populus late regnans.
