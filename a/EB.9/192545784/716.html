ARGENTUM ALBUM

article · 452 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARGENTUM ALBUM, in our old customs, silver coin, or pieces of bullion that anciently passed for money. By Doomsday tenure, some rents to the king were paid in argento albo, common silver pieces of money; other rents in libris ipsis et pensatis, in metal of full weight and purity: in the next age, that rent which was paid in money, was called blancs fcaum, and

Argenta and afterwards white rent; and what was paid in provisions, was termed black mail.
