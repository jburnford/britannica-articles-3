ARCHIVE

article · 362 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARCHIVE, or ARCHIVES, a chamber or apartment wherein the records, charters, and other papers and evidences, of a state, house, or community, are preserved, to be consulted occasionally.

We say, the archives of a college, of a monastery, &c. The archives of ancient Rome were in the temple of Saturn; the archives of the court of chancery are in the rolls office.
