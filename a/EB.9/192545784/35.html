ANTENATI

article · 378 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTENATI, in Modern English History, is chiefly understood of the subjects of Scotland, born before King James the First's accession to the English crown, and alive after it. In relation to these, those who were born after the accession were denominated Postnati. The antenati were considered as aliens in England,

whereas the postnati claimed the privilege of natural subjects.
