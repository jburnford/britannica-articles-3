ASSEMBLAGE

article · 253 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASSEMBLAGE, the uniting or joining of things together; or the things themselves so united or joined. It is also used, in a more general sense, for a collection of various things so disposed and diversified, that the whole produces some agreeable effect.
