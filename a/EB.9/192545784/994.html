ARUCI, in Ancient Geography, a town of the Celtici, in the north of Lusitania, (Antonine, Inscription); called also Aruci Novum, to distinguish it from the following: Now supposed to be Moura, a small city of Portugal, near the confluence of the Arda and Guadalquivir.
