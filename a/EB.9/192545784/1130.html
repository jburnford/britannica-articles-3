ASSAI

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: ALLEGRO

ASSAI, in Music, signifies quick; and, according to others, that the motion of the piece be kept in a middle degree of quickness or slowness. As, assai allegro, assai presto. See ALLEGRO and PRESTO.
