ARPENT

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARPENT, signifies an acre or furlong of ground; and according to the old French account in Doomsday-book, 100 perches make an arpent. The most ordinary acre, called l'arpent de France, is 100 perches square: but some account it but half an acre.
