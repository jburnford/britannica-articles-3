ASSITHMENT

article · 483 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASSITHMENT, a wiregeld, or compensation, by a pecuniary mulct; from the preposition ad, and the Sax. fitte, vice; quod vice supplicii ad expiandum delictum fovetur.
