ARLEUX

ARLEUX, an ancient town of the Netherlands, in Cambresis, with a castle. It was taken by the French in 1645, and retaken by the Allies in 1711; but the French got possession again the same month. E. Long. 3. 16. N. Lat. 59. 17.
