ARPINUM

ARPINUM, a town of the Volsci, a little to the east of the confluence of the rivers Liris and Fibrenus, in the Terra di Lavoro; now decayed, and called Arpino. It was the native place of Cicero, and of Caius Marius, (Sallust.)
