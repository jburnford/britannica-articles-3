ANTIOPE

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTIOPE, in Fabulous History, the wife of Licus, king of Thebes, who, being deformed by Jupiter in the form of a satyr, brought forth Amphion and Zethus.—Another Antiope was queen of the Amazons; and, with the assistance of the Scythians, invaded the Athenians; but was vanquished by Theseus.
