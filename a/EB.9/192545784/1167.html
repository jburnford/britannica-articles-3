ASSIGNING

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASSIGNING, in a general sense, implies the mak-
ing over the right of one person to another. In a
particular sense, it signifies the pointing out of some-
thing; as, an error, false judgment, &c.
