ASSETS

article · 959 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASSETS, in Law, signifies goods enough to discharge that burden which is cast upon the executor or heir, in satisfying the debts and legacies of the testator or ancestor. Assets are real or personal. Where a man hath lands in fee simple, and dies seised thereof, the lands which come to his heir are assets real; and where he dies possessed of any personal estate, the goods which come to the executors are assets personal. Assets are also divided into assets by descent, and assets in hand.
