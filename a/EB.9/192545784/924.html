ARRIERE Fee

sub_entry · 360 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARRIERE Fee, or Fief, is a fee dependent on a superior one. These fees commenced, when the dukes and counts, rendering their governments hereditary in their families, distributed to their officers parts of the royal domains which they found in their respective provinces, and even permitted those officers to gratify the soldiery under them in the same manner.
