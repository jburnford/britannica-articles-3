ARNON

article · 327 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARNON, in Ancient Geography, a brook running between the borders of the Moabites and Ammonites on the other side Jordan (Moses, Joshua): Josephus calls it a river rising on the borders of Arabia, and at length falling into the Dead sea. It is also called the river of Gzd, as appears 2 Sam. xxv. 5, compared with 2 Kings x. 33.
