ANTEROS

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTEROS, in Mythology, one of the two Cupids who were the chief of the number. They are placed at the foot of the Venus of Medicis; this is represented with a heavy and sulken look, agreeably to the poetical description of him, as the cause of love's ceasing. The other was called Eros.
