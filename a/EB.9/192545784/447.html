APPRISING

article · 462 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APPRISING, in Scots Law, the name of that action by which a creditor formerly carried off the estate of his debtor for payment. It is now abolished, and adjudications are appointed in place of it. Adjudications, charter, resignation, clere constat, cognition of heirs, heritable right, confirmation, novodamus, principal and original instrument of surrender, return, VOL. II. Part II.
