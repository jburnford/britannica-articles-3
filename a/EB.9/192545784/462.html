APRON

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APRON, in Naval Architecture, is a piece of curved timber fixed behind the lower part of the stern, immediately above the foremost end of the keel.

APRON is also a name given to a platform or flooring of plank, raised at the entrance of a dock, against which the dock gates are shut.
