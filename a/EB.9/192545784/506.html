AQUINUM, in Ancient Geography, a large municipal town, and a Roman colony on the borders of the Samnites, washed by the river Melphia (Strabo). The

inhabitants are called Aquinales. Now Aquino, but Aquitania almost in ruins, in the territory of Lavoro. E. Long. 17. 11. N. Lat. 41. 35.
