APHONIA

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APHONIA, among Physicians, signifies a suppression or total loss of voice. It is never a primary disease; but a consequence of many different disorders. The cure is to be effected by removing the disorder from whence the aphonia proceeds.
