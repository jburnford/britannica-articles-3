APONEUROISIS

article · 203 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APONEUROISIS, among Physicians, a term sometimes used to denote the expansion of a nerve or tendon in the manner of a membrane; sometimes for the cutting off a nerve; and, finally, for the tendon itself.
