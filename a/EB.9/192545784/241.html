APAGOGE

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APAGOGIE, in the Athenian Law, the carrying a criminal taken in the fact to the magistrate. If the accuser was not able to bring him to the magistrate, it was usual to take the magistrate along with him to the house where the criminal lay concealed, or defended himself.
