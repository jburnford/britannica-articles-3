ASPERIFOLIAE PLANTAE

article · 372 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASPERIFOLIAE PLANTAE, rough-leaved plants; the name of a class in Hermannus, Boerhaave, and Ray's methods, consisting of plants which have four naked seeds, and whose leaves are rough to the touch.

In Tournefort's System, these plants constitute the third section or order of the second class; and in Linnaeus's Sexual Method, they make a part of the pentandria monogynia.
