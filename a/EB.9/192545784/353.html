APOLLYON

article · 375 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APOLLYON, a Greek word that signifies the despoiler, and answers to the Hebrew Abaddon. St John in the Revelation (ix. 11.) says, that an angel having opened the bottomless pit, a thick smoke issued out of it; and with this smoke locusts, like horses, prepared for battle, and commanded by the angel of the bottomless pit, called in Hebrew Abaddon, but in the Greek Apollyon.
