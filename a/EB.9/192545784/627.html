ARCHIMANDRITE

article · 202 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARCHIMANDRITE, in Ecclesiastical History, was a name given by the ancient Christians to what we now call an abbot. Father Simon observes, that the word mandrite is Syriac, and signifies a solitary monk.
