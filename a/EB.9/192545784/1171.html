ASSISIO

ASSISIO, an episcopal town of Italy, in the duchy of Spoleto, built on the side of a very high mountain. The cathedral of St Francis is very magnificent, and composed of three churches, one above another. E. Long. 13. 35. N. Lat. 43. 4.
