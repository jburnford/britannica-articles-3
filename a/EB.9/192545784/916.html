ARRESTO FACTO SUPER BONIS

article · 176 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARRESTO FACTO SUPER BONIS, &c. a writ brought by a denizen against the goods of aliens found within this kingdom, as a recompense for goods taken from him in a foreign country.
