ARION, an excellent musician and poet, inventor of dithyrambics. Periander entertained him at his court, where getting an estate, and returning to Corinth, the sailors, for lucre of his money, threw him into the sea; when, according to the poets, a dolphin, charmed with his music, took him on her back and carried him safe to shore.
