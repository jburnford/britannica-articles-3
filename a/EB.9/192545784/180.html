ANTISCII

article · 292 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTISCII, in Geography, people who live on different sides of the equator, whose shadows at noon are projected opposite ways. Thus the people of the north are Antiscii to those of the south; the one projecting their shadows at noon towards the north pole, and the other toward the south pole.
