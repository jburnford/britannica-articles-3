ARTOLICA, in Ancient Geography, a town of the Salassi in Gallia Cispadana, at the foot of the Alps: now called la Tuile by the inhabitants, a hamlet of Savoy, in the duchy of Aosta, at the foot of Mount St Bernard the Less.
