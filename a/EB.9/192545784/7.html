ANTACIDS, in Pharmacy, an appellation given to all medicines proper to correct acid or sour humours.

Under the class of Antacids come, 1. Absorbents; as chalk, coral, sea shells, hematites, and steel filings. 2. Obtundents; as oils and fats. 3. Immuntants; as lixivious salts and soaps.
