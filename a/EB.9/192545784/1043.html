ASCETIC

article · 245 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASCETIC is also a title of several books of spiritual exercises.—As, the Ascetics, or devout exercises of St Basil, archbishop of Cæsarea in Cappadocia.

We also say the ascetic life, meaning the exercise of prayer, meditation, and mortification.
