ANTESIGNANI

article · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTESIGNANI, in the Roman armies, soldiers placed before the standards, in order to defend them, according to Lampsius; but Cæsar and Livy mention the antesignani as the first line, or first body, of heavy-armed troops. The velites, who used to skirmish before the army, were likewise called antesignani.
