ARTOBRIGA

ARTOBRIGA, a town of Vindelicia (Ptolemy): Now Altzbourg, in Bavaria, on the Danube, below Ingolstadt (Aventinus): but Cluverius supposes it to be Lebenau, on the Salzach, below Lauffen, in the archbishopric of Salzburg.
