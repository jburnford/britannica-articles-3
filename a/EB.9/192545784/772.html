ARIMASPI

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARIMASPI, (Pliny), a people of Sarmatia Europea, to the south of the Montes Riphei, said by Meila to have but one eye; a fable broached by Aristeas Proconnicus, according to Herodotus.
