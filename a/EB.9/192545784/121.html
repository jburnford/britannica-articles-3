ANTIGRAPHUS

article · 330 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTIGRAPHUS is also applied in ecclesiastical writers to an abbreviator of the papal letters. In which sense the word is used by Pope Gregory the Great in his register. Of late days the office of antigraphus consists in making minutes of bulls from the petitions agreed to by his holiness, and renewing the bulls after engrossing.
