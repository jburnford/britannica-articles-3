ASIONGABER, ESIONGEBER, or EZIONGEBER, a town of Arabia Petrea, on the bay of Elath, a part of the Arabian gulf: the dock or station for the ships of Solomon and Jehoshaphat; an ancient town, mentioned also by Moses. It was afterwards called Berenice (Josephus).
