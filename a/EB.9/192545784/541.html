ARAM-Maacha

ARAM-Maacha, in Ancient Geography, a district of Syria, at the foot of Mount Hermon, (2 Samuel, 1 Chronicles); on the borders of the half tribe of Manasseh, on the other side the Jordan, called the coast of Maachathi, (Moses, Joshua).
