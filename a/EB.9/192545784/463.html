APRON

article · 217 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APRON, in Gunnery, a piece of lead which caps or covers the vent or touch-hole of a great gun.

APSIS or ABSSIS, signifies the bowed or arched roof of a house, room, or oven, &c. as also the ring or compass of a wheel.
