ASH-Hole

sub_entry · 222 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ASH-Hole, among Chemists, is the lowest part of a furnace; and is intended to receive the ashes falling from the fire, and to give a passage to the air which is to be introduced into the furnace, to keep up the combustion.
