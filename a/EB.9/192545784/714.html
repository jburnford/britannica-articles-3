ARGENTON

article · 200 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARGENTON, a town and county of France, in the department of Indre, divided into two by the river Creuse. Here was formerly a castle, but it was demolished by Lewis XIV. E. Long. 1. 38. N. Lat. 40. 30.
