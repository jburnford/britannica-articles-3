Cross-references: ENFANT PERDUE

ASSAULT, in the Military Art, a furious effort made to carry a fortified post, camp, or fortrefs, wherein the assailants do not screen themselves by any works: while the assault continues, the batteries cease, for fear of killing their own men. The enfant perdu march first to the assault. See ENFANT PERDUE.
