ARGOS Hippium, the ancient name of Arpi; but Lampe is a still more ancient; afterwards called Argyrippa, and Argippa; built by, and the residence of, Diomedes, on the Cerbalus, (Virgil); afterwards a large and populous city, (Livy): A town of Apulia; now in ruins, and the place called Arpi.
