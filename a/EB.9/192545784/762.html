ARIUS MONTANUS

article · 208 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARIUS MONTANUS, a learned Spanish divine, employed by Philip II. of Spain to publish another edition of the Bible, after that of Cardinal Ximenes; which he finished with applause, and died at Seville in 1598.
