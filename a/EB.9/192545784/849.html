ARMORUM CONCUSSIO

Cross-references: CLASSICUM

ARMORUM CONCUSSIO, the clashing of arms practised by the Roman armies previous to an engagement, and intended to strike a panic into their enemies; It always followed the clasicum and the barritus. See CLASSICUM and BARRITUS.
