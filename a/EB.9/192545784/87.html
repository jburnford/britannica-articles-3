ANTHROPOMORPHOUS

article · 241 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTHROPOMORPHOUS, something that bears the figure or resemblance of a man. Naturalists give instances of anthropomorphic plants, anthropomorphic minerals, &c. These generally come under the class of what they call lusus naturae, or monsters.
