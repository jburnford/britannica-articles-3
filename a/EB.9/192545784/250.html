APARITHMESIS

article · 184 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APARITHMESIS, in Rhetoric, denotes the answer to the protasis or proposition itself. Thus, if the protasis be, Appellandi tempus non erat.—The aparithmesis is, At tecum anno plus vivi.
