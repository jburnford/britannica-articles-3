ARGESTES

article · 294 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARGESTES, is used by Vitruvius for the wind which blows from that quarter of the horizon, which is 75° from the south and westward. Riccioius uses the term to denote the wind which blows at 22° 30' from the west towards the north, coinciding with that which is otherwise called west-north-west.
