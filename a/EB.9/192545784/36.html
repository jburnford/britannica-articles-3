ANTENCLEMA

article · 210 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ANTENCLEMA, in Oratory, is where the whole defence of the person accused turns on criminating the accuser. Such is the defence of Orestes, or the oration for Milo: Occisus est, sed latro. Exsecutus, sed raptor.
