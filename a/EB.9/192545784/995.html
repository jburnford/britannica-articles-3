ARUCI VETUS

article · 330 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARUCI VETUS, in Ancient Geography, a small city of the Turdetani, in Bætica, (Ptolemy); now Aroche, a hamlet of Andalusia, on the confines of Portugal and Estremadura, on the river Gama, seven leagues to the east of Aruci Novum or Moura. From it a mountain, in its neighbourhood, takes the name Arucianus. Now la Sierra de Aroche.
