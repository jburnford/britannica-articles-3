APANAGE

article · 219 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

APANAGE, or APPENAGE, in the French customs, lands assigned by a sovereign for the subsistence of his younger sons, which revert to the crown upon the failure of male issue in that branch to which the lands are granted.
