ARTIFICIAL Lines

sub_entry · 239 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARTIFICIAL Lines, on a sector or scale, are certain lines so contrived, as to represent the logarithmic fines and tangents; which, by the help of the line of numbers will solve all questions in trigonometry, navigation, &c. pretty exactly.
