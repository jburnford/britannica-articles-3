ARTABAZUS

article · 377 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARTABAZUS, the son of Pharnaces, commanded the Parthians and Chersonesians in the famous expedition of Xerxes. After the battle of Salamis, he escorted the king his master to the Hesperont with 60,000 chosen men; and after the battle of Plataea, in which Mardonius engaged contrary to his advice, he made a noble retreat, and returned to Asia with 40,000 men under his command.
