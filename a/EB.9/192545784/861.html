ARNAUD DE MEYERVELIN

article · 270 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ARNAUD DE MEYERVELIN, or MEZVILLE, a poet of Provence, who lived at the beginning of the 13th century. He wrote a book entitled Las recepciones de sa contesse, and a collection of poems and sonnets. He died in the year 1220. Petrarch mentions him in his Triumph of Love.
