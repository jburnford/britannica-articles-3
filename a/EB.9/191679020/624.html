LAUDATIO

article · 314 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LAUDATIO, in a legal sense, was anciently the testimony delivered in court of the accused person's good behaviour and integrity of life. It resembled the custom, which prevails in our trials, of calling persons to speak to the character of the prisoner. The least number of the laudatores among the Romans was ten.
