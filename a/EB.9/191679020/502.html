LANGOBARDI

article · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LANGOBARDI, a people of Germany situated between the Elbe and the Oder, in the Marche of Brandenburg, whom their paucity ennobled; in regard that, being encompassed by many and powerful nations, they preserved themselves, not so much by submission, as by dint of arms and encountering dangers, (Tacitus).
