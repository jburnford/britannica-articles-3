KAINSI

Cross-references: CAPRA

KAINSI, the Hottentot name of a species of ante-
lope, denominated by the Dutch, on account of its agi-
lity, hip-springer. It is of a yellowish-gray colour, and
of the size of a kid of a year old. See CAPRA, MAM-
MALIA Index.
