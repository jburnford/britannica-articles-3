LANGELAND

LANGELAND, an island of Denmark in the Baltic sea, in the strait called the great belt, and between Zealand, Seland, and Eyösis. It produces plenty of corn, and the principal town is Rutecoping. E. Long. 11. 10. N. Lat. 55. 0.
