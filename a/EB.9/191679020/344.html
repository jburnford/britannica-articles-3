LABDASSEBA

LABDASSEBA, a tribe of savage Arabs inhabiting the desert of Sahara in Africa. They are considered as the most powerful of all those tribes except the Ouadelims, and very much resemble them in every particular. See SAMARA and OUADELIMS.
