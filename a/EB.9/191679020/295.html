KNOT

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KNOT, a part of a tree, from which shoot out branches, roots, or even fruit. The use of the knots is, to strengthen the stem; they serve also as fearces, to filtrate, purify, and refine the juices raised up for the nourishment of the plant.
