LEBRIXA, an ancient, strong, and pleasant town of Spain, in Andalusia; seated on a territory abounding in corn, wine, and a great number of olive trees, of whose fruit they make the best oil in Spain. W. Long. 5. 32. N. Lat. 36. 52.
