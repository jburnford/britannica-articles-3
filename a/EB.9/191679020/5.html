IVREA

IVREA, an ancient and strong town of Italy, in Piedmont, and capital of Canaveze, with a strong fort,

Jurieu a bishop's see, the title of a marquisate, and an ancient castle. It is subject to the king of Sardina, and seated on the river Dora between two hills, in E. Long. 7. 48. N. Lat. 45. 12.
