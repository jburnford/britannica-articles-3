KIRKBY-STEVEN

article · 359 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KIRKBY-STEVEN, or Stephen's Church, a town of Westmorland, 257 miles from London, stands on the river Eden near Sedbergh and Afsarth. The church is a large building with a lofty tower; in it are several old monuments. Here is a good free school that has two exhibitions. The town is noted for the manufactory of yarn stockings; and it has a market and a fair.
