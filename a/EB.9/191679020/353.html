LABOUR

article · 255 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LABOUR, in general, denotes a close application to work or business.—Among seamen a ship is said to labour when she rolls and tumbles very much, either a-hull, under sail, or at anchor.—It is also spoken of a woman in travail or childbirth; see MIDWIFERY.
