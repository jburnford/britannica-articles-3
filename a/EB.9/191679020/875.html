LETHE

article · 265 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LETHE, (from λαθω, "I hide or conceal"), in the ancient mythology, one of the rivers of hell, signifying oblivion or forgetfulness; its waters having, according to poetic fiction, the peculiar quality of making those who drank them forget every thing that was past.
