LICOLA

article · 285 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LICOLA, or LAGO DI LICOLA, a lake in the kingdom of Naples, formerly famous for plenty of excellent fish; but in the year 1538 an explosion of a volcano changed one part of it into a mountain of ashes, and the other into a marsh. It was anciently known by the name of the Lucrine lake.
