LIBETHRIUS MONS, in Ancient Geography, a mountain of Boeotia, distant from Coronea 40 stadia; where

Libitina where stood the statues of the Muses, and of the nymphs furnished Libethrides: a mountain probably conjoined with, or at least very near to, Helicon.
