KANISCA

article · 191 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KANISCA, a very strong town of Lower Hungary, capital of the county of Szilawar. It was taken by the Imperialists in 1692. It is seated on the river Drave, in E. Long. 17. 37. N. Lat. 46. 23.
