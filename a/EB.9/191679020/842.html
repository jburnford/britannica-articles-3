LEPTOPOLYGINGLIMI

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LEPTOPOLYGINGLIMI, in Natural History, a genus of fossil shells, distinguished by a number of minute teeth at the hinge. Specimens of these are found at Harwich cliff, and in the marl pits of Sussex.
