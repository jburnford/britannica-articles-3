JUROR, JURATOR

article · 291 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

JUROR, JURATOR, in a legal sense, is one of those twenty-four or twelve men who are sworn to deliver truth upon such evidence as shall be given them touching any matter in question. The punishment of petty jurors attainted of giving a verdict contrary to evidence, willingly, is very severe.
