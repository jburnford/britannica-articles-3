KIT

article · 314 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KIT, in Music, the name of a small violin of such form and dimension as to be capable of being carried in a case or sheath in the pocket. Its length, measuring from the extremities, is about 16 inches, and that of the bow about 17. Small as this instrument is, its powers are co-extensive with those of the violin.
