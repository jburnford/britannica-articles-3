LIBNA

LIBNA, in Ancient Geography, a sacerdotal city in the tribe of Judah, a place of strength, as appears from Sennacherib's laying siege to it, 2 Kings xix. Isaiah xxxvii. In Jerome's time, a village, called Lonna, in the territory of Eleutheropolis.
