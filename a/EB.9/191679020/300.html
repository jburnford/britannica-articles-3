KNOTTESFORD

article · 237 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

KNOTTESFORD, a town of Cheshire, near the Mersey, 184 miles from London, is divided into the upper and lower towns by a rivulet called Bicken. In the former is the church; and in the latter is a chapel of ease, the market and town-house.
