LEBADEA, or LEBADIA

sub_entry · 242 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LEBADEA, or LEBADIA, an ancient town of Boeotia, on the borders of Phocis, situated between Helicon and Chaeronea, near Coronea. In it stood the oracle of Jupiter Trophonius, which whoever went to consult, descended into a subterraneous gulf.
