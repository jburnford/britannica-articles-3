LANISTA

article · 316 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LANISTA, in antiquity, is sometimes used to signify an executioner; but more frequently for a master gladiator, who taught the use of arms, and had always people under him ready to exhibit shows of that kind. For this purpose, they either purchased gladiators, or educated children in that art that had been exposed.
