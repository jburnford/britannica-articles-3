LELEX

article · 308 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LELEX, an Egyptian who came with a colony to Megara, where he reigned about 200 years before the Trojan war. His subjects were called from him Leleger.—Also the name of a Greek who was the first king of Laconia in Peloponnesus. His subjects were also called Leleger, and the country where he reigned Lelegia.
