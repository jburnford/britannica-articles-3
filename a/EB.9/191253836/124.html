NOEOMAGUS LEXUVIORUM

article · 260 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOEOMAGUS LEXUVIORUM, (Ptol.); thought to be the Civitas Lexuviorum of the lower age. Now Lisieux, a city in Normandy.—Another of the Tricastini; a town of Gallia Narbonensis; thought to be S. Pol. de Trois Châteaux, six miles to the west of Nyons in Dauphiné.
