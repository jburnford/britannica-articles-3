NICOPOLI

NICOPOLI, a town of Turkey in Europe, and in Bulgaria, famous for being the place where the first battle was fought between the Turks and Christians in 1396; and where the latter were defeated with the loss of 20,000 men. E. Long. 25. 33. N. Lat. 43. 46.
