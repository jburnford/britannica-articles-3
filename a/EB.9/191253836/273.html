NUNCIO

article · 179 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NUNCIO, or NUNTIO, an ambassador from the pope to some Catholic prince or state, or a person who attends on the pope's behalf at a congress, or an assembly of several ambassadors.
