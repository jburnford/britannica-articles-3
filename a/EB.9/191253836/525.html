OMBROMETER

article · 421 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OMBROMETER, an instrument to measure the quantity of rain that falls. We have the description and figure of one in Phil. Trans. No 473. p. 12. It consists of a tin funnel, whose surface is an inch square, with a flat board, and a glass tube set into the middle of it in a groove. The rise of the water in the tube, whole capacity at different times must be measured and marked, shows the quantity of rain that has fallen.
