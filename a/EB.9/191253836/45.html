NIEPER

article · 414 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NIEPER, or DNEPER, a large river of Europe, and one of the most considerable of the North, formerly called the Boristhenes. Its source is in the middle of Muscovy, running west by Smolensko, as far as Orfa; and then turns south, passing by Mohilow, Bohacz, Kiow, Czyrkaff, the fortrefs of Kudak, Delfau, and Oczakow, falling into the Black sea; as also in its course it divides Little Tartary from Budziac Tartary.
