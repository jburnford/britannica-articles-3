ONTARIO, a lake of North America, in the country of the Iroquois, 180 miles in length and 60 in breadth. There are many rivers that run into it: and from

Oontology from it the great river St Lawrence proceeds. It communicates with the lake Erie by a river 33 miles in length, on which is the remarkable cataract of Niagara.
