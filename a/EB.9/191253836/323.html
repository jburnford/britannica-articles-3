OATH-LAW

article · 206 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OATH-LAW, the name of a parish in Angus, about two miles from Forfar, chiefly remarkable for the remains of a Roman camp called Battle-dykes (vulgarly Black-dykes), which is about a mile west of the church.
