OBLATE

article · 258 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OBLATE, flattened or shortened; as an oblate spheroid, having its axis shorter than its middle diameter; being formed by the rotation of an ellipse about the shorter axis. The earth, whose polar diameter is shorter than the equatorial, is an oblate spheroid.
