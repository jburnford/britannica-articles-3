OONELLA

article · 378 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OONELLA, and OONEMAH, two islands of the same archipelago with Oonalashka; the former of which lies to the north-east of that island, being separated from it by a navigable strait; the other is more to the westward, being in E. Long. 192. 30. and N. Lat. 54. 30. The circumference of Oonella is

about seven leagues, and the produce of both much the same with that of Oonalashka.
