ONOMANCIA

article · 354 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ONOMANCIA, or rather ONOMANTIA, a branch of divination, which foretells the good or bad fortune of a man, from the letters in his name. See the article DIVINATION and NAME.

From much the same principle the young Romans toasted their mistresses as often as there were letters in their names: Hence Martial says,

Nativa sex cyathis, septem Iustina bibatur.
