OESSEL

article · 304 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OESSEL, an island of the Baltic sea, at the entrance of the gulf of Livonia. It is about 70 miles in length, and 50 in breadth, and contains 10 parishes. It is defended by the fortresses of Airenburg and Sonneburg. It lies between 22° and 24° of east longitude, and between 58° and 59° of north latitude.
