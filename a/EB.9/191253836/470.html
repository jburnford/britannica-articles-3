OGYGES

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

OGYGES, king of the Thebans, or, according to others, of Ogygia and Aëte, afterwards called Bæotia and Attica. He is recorded to have been the first founder of Thebes and Eleusis. The famous deluge happened in his time, in which some say he perished with all his subjects, 1796 B. C.
