NORMANS

NORMANS, a fierce warlike people of Norway, Denmark, and other parts of Scandinavia. They at different times overrun and ravaged most countries in Europe: to the respective histories of those countries we therefore refer for a fuller account of them, as it is impossible to enlarge upon particulars in this place without repeating what has been already said, or may be said, in different parts of the work.
