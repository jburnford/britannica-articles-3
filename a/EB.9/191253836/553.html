ONOSANDER

article · 416 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ONOSANDER, a Greek author and Platonic philosopher, who wrote Commentaries on Plato's Politics, which are lost: but his name is particularly famous for a treatise entitled Λογος Στρατηγος, "Of the duty and virtues of the general of an army;" which has been translated into Latin, Italian, Spanish, and French. The time when he lived is not precisely known: but is imagined to be in the reign of the emperor Claudius.
