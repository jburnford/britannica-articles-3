ODA Bachi

sub_entry · 594 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ODA Bachi, or Oddabachi, an officer in the Turkish soldiery, equivalent to a sergeant or corporal among us. The common soldiers and janizaries, called oldachis, after having served a certain number of years, are always preferred and made bacheliers; and of bacheliers in time become odobachis, i. e. corporals of companies, or chiefs of certain divisions, whose number is not fixed; being sometimes ten, and sometimes twenty.

Their pay is six doubles per month; and they are distinguished by a large felt, a foot broad and above a foot long, hanging on the back, with two long ostrich feathers.
