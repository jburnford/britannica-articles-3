NIWEGAL

article · 293 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NIWEGAL, a village lying on the coast in Pembrokeshire, South Wales, remarkable only for the discovery of an immense quantity of the stumps of trees appearing below low water mark, after and during a storm in the year 1590, notwithstanding the country all round is now entirely barren of wood.
