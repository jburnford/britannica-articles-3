NONAGESIMAL

article · 406 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NONAGESIMAL, or NONAGESIMAL Degree, called also the Mid Heaven, is the highest point, or 90th degree of the ecliptic, reckoned from its intersection with the horizon at any time; and its altitude is equal to the angle which the ecliptic makes with the horizon at their intersection, or equal to the distance of the zenith from the pole of the ecliptic. It is much used in the calculation of solar eclipses.
