NICHE

article · 290 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NICHE, in Architecture, a hollow sunk into a wall, for the commodious and agreeable placing of a statue. The word comes from the Italian nechia, "sea-shell;" in regard the statue is here enclosed in a shell, or perhaps on account of the shell wherewith the tops of some of them are adorned.
