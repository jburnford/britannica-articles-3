NOETIANS

article · 514 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NOETIANS, in church history, Christian heretics in the third century, followers of Noetus, a philosopher of Ephesus, who pretended that he was another Moses sent by God, and that his brother was a new Aaron. His heresy consisted in affirming that there was but one person in the Godhead; and that the Word and the Holy Spirit were but external denominations given to God in consequence of different operations: that, as Creator, he is called Father; as Incarnate, Son; and as defending on the apostles, Holy Ghost.
