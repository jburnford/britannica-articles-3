NIESTER

article · 303 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

NIESTER, a large river of Poland, which has its source in the lake Nießer, in the palatinate of Lemberg, where it passes by Halicz. Then it separates Podolia and Oczakow Tartary from Moldavia and Budziac Tartary; and falls into the Black sea at

Belgorod, between the mouths of the Nieper and the Danube.
