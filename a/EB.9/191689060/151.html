PAUSE

Cross-references: POETRY

PAUSE, a stop or cessation in speaking, singing, playing, or the like. One use of pointing in grammar is to make proper pauses, in certain places.—There is a pause in the middle of each verse; in an hemistich, it is called a rest or repose. See POETRY, and READING.
