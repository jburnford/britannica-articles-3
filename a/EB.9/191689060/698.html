PHILYRA

article · 470 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHILYRA, in fabulous history, was one of the Oceanides, whom Saturn met in Thrace. The god, to escape from the vigilance of Rhea, changed himself into a horse, to enjoy the company of Phylra, by whom he had a son half a man and half a horse, called Chiron.

Philyra was so ashamed of giving birth to such a monster, that she entreated the gods to change her nature. She was accordingly metamorphosed into a tree, called by her name among the Greeks.
