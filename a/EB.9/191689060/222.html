PEINE FORT ET DURE

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

Cross-references: ARRAIGNMENT

PEINE FORT ET DURE, (Lat. pæna fortis et dura), signifies a special punishment inflicted on those who, being arraigned of felony, refuse to put themselves on the ordinary trial, but stubbornly stand mute; it is vulgarly called pressing to death. See ARRAIGNMENT.
