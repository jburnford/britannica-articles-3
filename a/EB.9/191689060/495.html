PESSARY

article · 248 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PESSARY, in Medicine, a solid substance composed of wood, lint, or linen, mixed with powder, oil, wax, &c. made round and long like a finger, in order to be introduced into the exterior neck of the matrix, for the cure of several uterine disorders.
