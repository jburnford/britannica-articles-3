PERPIGNAN

article · 216 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PERPIGNAN, a considerable town of Roussillon, in France, with a strong citadel, an university, and a bishop's see. It is seated on the river Tet; over which there is a handsome bridge. E. Long. 0. 43. N. Lat. 45. 18.
