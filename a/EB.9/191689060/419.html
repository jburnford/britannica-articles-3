PERISTALTIC

article · 314 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PERISTALTIC, a vermicular spontaneous motion of the intestines, performed by the contraction of the circular and longitudinal fibres of which the fleshy coats of the intestines are composed; by means whereof the chyle is driven into the orifices of the lacteal veins, and the faeces are protruded towards the anus.
