PETRA, (Cæsar, Lucian), a town of Greece, on the coast of Illyricum, near Dyrrhachium, and not far from the mouth of the river Panyasus.—Another PETRA, (Livy); a town of Mædica, a district of Thrace, lying towards Macedonia; but in what part of Macedonia, he does not say.
