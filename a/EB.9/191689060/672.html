PHILIPPISTS

PHILIPPISTS, a sect or party among the Lutherans; the followers of Philip Melanchthon. He had strenuously opposed the Ussquites, who arose in his time; and the dispute growing still hotter after his death, the university of Wirttemberg, who espoused Melanchthon's opinion, were called by the Flacians, who attacked it, Philippists.
