PHILIBEG

article · 472 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PHILIBEG, is a little plaid, called also kilt, and is a sort of short petticoat reaching nearly to the knees, worn by the Scotch Highlanders. It is a modern substitute for the lower part of the plaid, being found to be less cumbersome, especially in time of action, when the Highlanders used to tuck their breeches into their girdle. Almost all of them have a great pouch of badger and other skins, with tassels dangling before, in which they keep their tobacco and money.
