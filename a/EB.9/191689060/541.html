PETROJOANNITES

article · 511 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PETROJOANNITES, were followers of Peter John, or Peter Joannus, i. e. Peter the son of John, who flourished in the 12th century. His doctrine was not known till after his death, when his body was taken out of his grave and burnt. His opinions were, that he alone had the knowledge of the true sense wherein the apostles preached the gospel; that the reasonable soul is not the form of man; that there is no grace infused by baptism; and that Jesus Christ was pierced with a lance on the cross before he expired.
