PERCH

PERCH, in land-measuring, a rod or pole of 16½ feet in length, of which 40 in length and 4 in breadth make an acre of ground. But, by the customs of several counties, there is a difference in this measure. In Staffordshire it is 24 feet; and in the forest of Shierwood 25 feet: the foot being there 18 inches long; and in Herefordshire a perch of ditching is 21 feet, the perch of walling 16½ feet, and a pole of denuded ground is 12 feet, &c.
