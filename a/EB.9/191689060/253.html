PELLICLE

article · 199 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PELLICLE, among physicians, denotes a thin film or fragment of a membrane. Among chemists it signifies a thin surface of crystals uniformly spread over a saline liquor evaporated to a certain degree.
