PAVOR

article · 198 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PAVOR, a Roman deity, whose worship was introduced by Tullus Hostilius, who, in a panic, vowed a shrine to him, and one to Pallor, Palensis; and therefore they are found on the coins of that family.
