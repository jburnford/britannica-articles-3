PEON

article · 220 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PEON, in the language of Hindostan, means a foot soldier, armed with sword and target. In common use it is a footman, so armed, employed to run before a panquin. Piaah is the proper word; from which peon is a corruption.
