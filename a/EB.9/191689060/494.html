PESENAS

PESENAS, an ancient town of France, in Languedoc, and in the diocese of Agde; delightfully seated on the river Pein, 12 miles north-east of Belfeirs, and eight north of Agde. E. Long. 3. 34. N. Lat. 43. 28.
