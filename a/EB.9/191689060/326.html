PENTACHORD

article · 262 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PENTACHORD (compounded of πέντε free, and χορδή string), an ancient musical instrument with five strings. The invention of the pentachord is referred to the Scythians; the strings were of bullocks leather, and they were struck with a plectrum made of goats horn.
