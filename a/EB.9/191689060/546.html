PETROSA OSSA

Cross-references: ANATOMY

PETROSA OSSA, in Anatomy, a name given to the fourth and fifth bones of the cranium, called also ossa temporum and ossa squamosa; the substance whereof, as their first and last names express, is squamous and very hard. See ANATOMY Index.
