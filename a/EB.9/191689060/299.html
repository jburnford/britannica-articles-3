PENITENTIAL, an ecclesiastical book, retained among the Romanists; in which is prescribed what relates to the imposition of penance and the reconciliation of penitents. See PENANCE.

There are various penitentials, as the Roman penitential, that of the venerable Bede, that of Pope Gregory III. &c.
