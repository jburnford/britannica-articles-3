PENSION

article · 312 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

PENSION, a sum of money paid annually for services or considerations already past. The yearly pay-

ment of each member to the houses of the inns of courts Pensionary are likewise named pensions; and the yearly assembly of the society of Gray's Inn, to consult on the affairs of the house, is also called a pension.
