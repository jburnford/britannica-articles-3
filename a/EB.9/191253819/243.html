ALCINOUS

article · 376 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALCINOUS, king of the Phœacians, in the island now called Corfu, was son of Nausithous, and grandson of Neptune and Peribea. It is by his gardens this king has chiefly immortalized his memory. He received Ulysses with much civility, when a storm had cast him on his coast. The people here loved pleasure and good cheer, yet were skilful seamen; and Alcinous was a good prince.
