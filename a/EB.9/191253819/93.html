AISNE

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AISNE, a river of France, which rises in Champagne, and runs west by Soissons in the Isle of France, falling into the river Oise, a little above Compiègne.

It gives name to one of the five departments which comprehend the ancient Isle of France, and contains five communal districts.
