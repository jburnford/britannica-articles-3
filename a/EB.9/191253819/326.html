ALEXANDRIAN

article · 362 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALEXANDRIAN is more particularly understood of a college of priests, consecrated to the service of Alexander Severus after his deification. Lampridius relates, that, notwithstanding Severus was killed by Maximin, the senate prosecuted his apotheosis; and, for regularity of worship, founded an order of priests, or fodales, under the denomination of Alexandrini.
