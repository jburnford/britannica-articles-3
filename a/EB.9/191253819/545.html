ALPISTE

article · 281 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALPISTE, or ALPIA, a sort of feed used to feed birds with, especially when they are to be nourished for breeding. The alpiſte feed is of an oval figure, of a pale yellow, inclining to an ifabel colour, bright and glossy. It is an article of the corn-chandlers and feed-men's trade.
