ALICATA

article · 183 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALICATA, a mountain of Sicily, near the valleys Mazara and Noto, upon which was situated (as is generally thought) the famous Dædalion, where the tyrant Phalaris kept his brazen bull.
