AMAIN

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMAIN, in the Sea Language, a term importing to lower something at once. Thus, to strike amain, is to lower or let fall the topsails; to wave amain, is to make a signal, by waving a drawn sword, or the like, as a demand that the enemy strike their topsails.
