ALGAGIOLA

article · 207 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALGAGIOLA, a small sea-port town in the island of Corsica, fortified with walls and bastions. It was almost destroyed by the malecontents in 1731, but has since been repaired. E. Long. 9. 45. N. Lat. 42. 20.
