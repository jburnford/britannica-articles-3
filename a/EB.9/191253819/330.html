ALEXICACUS

article · 325 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALEXICACUS, in Antiquity, was an attribute of Neptune, whom the tunny-fishers used to invoke under this appellation, that their nets might be preserved from the ἔξω, or sword-fish, which used to tear them; and that he might prevent the assistance which it was pretended the dolphins used to give the tunnies on this occasion.
