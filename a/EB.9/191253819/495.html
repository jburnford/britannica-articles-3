LORD ALMONER

sub_entry · 384 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

LORD ALMONER, or LORD HIGH ALMONER of England, is an ecclesiastical officer, generally a bishop, who has the forfeiture of all deodands, and the goods of felons de se, which he is to distribute among the poor. He has also, by virtue of an ancient custom, the power of giving the first dish from the king's table to whatever poor person he pleases, or, instead of it, an alms in money.
