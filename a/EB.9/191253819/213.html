ALBURN

article · 205 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALBURN, the English name of a compound colour, being a mixture of white and red, or reddish brown. Skinner derives the word, in this sense, from the Latin albus, and the Italian bruno, from bruno, "brown."
