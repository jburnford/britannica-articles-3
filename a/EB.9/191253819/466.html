ALMADEN

article · 193 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALMADEN, a town of Spain, in the province of La Mancha, in the kingdom of Castile, situated upon the top of a mountain, where are the most ancient as well as the richest silver mines in Europe.
