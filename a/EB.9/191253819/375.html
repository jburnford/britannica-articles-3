ALIQVOT PART

article · 156 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALIQVOT PART, is that part of a number or quantity which will exactly measure it without any remainder. Thus 2 is an aliquot part of 4, 3 of 9, 4 of 16, &c.
