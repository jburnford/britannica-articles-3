AMBERT

AMBERT, a small town of France, in the department of Puy de Dome, formerly Lower Auvergne. It is the chief place of a small territory called Livadaois. Paper and playing cards, camlets, and woollen stuffs are manufactured here. E. Long. 5. 15. N. Lat. 45. 58.
