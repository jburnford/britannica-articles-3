AIDS

article · 257 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AIDS, in the Manege, are the same with what some writers call cherispinge, and used to avoid the necessity of corrections.—The inner heel, inner leg, inner rein, &c. are called inner aids; as the outer heel, outer leg, outer rein, &c. are called outer aids.
