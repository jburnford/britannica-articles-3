ALLOWAY CREEK

article · 143 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALLOWAY CREEK, in Salem county, New-Jersey, empties into the Delaware. It is navigable 16 miles, interrupted, however, by several draw-bridges.
