ALETUM

article · 192 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALETUM, or ALETA, in Ancient Geography, a town of Celtic Gaul, now extinct. From its ruins arose St Malo in Brittany, at the distance of a mile. Its ruins are called Gueb Aleib in the British.
