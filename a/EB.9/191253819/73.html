AILE

article · 240 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AILE, in Law, a writ which lies where a person's grandfather, or great-grandfather, being seized of lands, &c. in fee-simple, the day that he died, and a stranger abates and enters the same day, and dispossesses the heir of his inheritance.
