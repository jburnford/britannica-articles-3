ALCAZAR LEGUER, a town of Africa, in the kingdom of Fez, and in the province of Ilakat. It

was taken by Alfonso, king of Portugal, in 1468; but soon after that, it was abandoned to the Moors. It is seated on the coast of the Straits of Gibraltar. W. Long. 3. 50. N. Lat. 38. 0.
