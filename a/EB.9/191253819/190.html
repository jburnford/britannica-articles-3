ALBINTEMELIUM. ALAINTIMILUM, (Tacitus); or at full length, ALBIUM INTEMELIUM, (Pliny, Strabo); now Vintimiglia, situated in the south-west of the territory of Genoa, near the borders of the county of Nice, with a port on the Mediterranean, at the mouth of the rivulet Rotta, about halfway between Monaco and S. Remo. E. Long. 7. 40. N. Lat. 43. 17.
