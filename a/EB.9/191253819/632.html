ALWAIDII

article · 232 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALWAIDII, a sect of Mahometans who believe all great crimes to be unpardonable.—The Alwaiddi stand in opposition to the Morgii. They attribute less efficacy to the true belief in the salvation of men than the rest of the Mussulmans.
