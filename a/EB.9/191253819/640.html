AMABYR

article · 238 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMABYR, a barbarous custom which formerly prevailed in several parts of England and Wales, being a sum of money paid to the lord when a maid was married within his lordship. The word is old British, and signifies "the price of virginity."
