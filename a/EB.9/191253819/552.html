ALSA

article · 261 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALSA, in Ancient Geography, a river of Carniola (Pliny), now the Ausa, running by Aquileia, with a short course from north to south, into the Adriatic; where Constantine, the son of Constantine the Great, fighting against Constantius his brother, lost his life.
