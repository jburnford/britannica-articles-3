ALBEMARLE

ALBEMARLE, or AUMARLE, a town of France, in Upper Normandy, and in the territory of Caux, from whence the noble family of Keppel takes the title of Earl. The ferges of this town are in high esteem. It is seated on the declivity of a hill, on the confines of Picardy, 35 miles north-east of Rouen, and 70 north-west of Paris. E. Long. 2. 21. N. Lat. 49. 50.
