AMELIA Isle

sub_entry · 391 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMELIA Isle, on the coast of East Florida, lies about seven leagues north of St. Augustine, and very near Taibot island on the south, at the mouth of St. John's river. It is 13 miles long and 2 broad, is very fertile, and has an excellent harbour. Its north end lies opposite Cumberland island, between which and Amelia is the entry into St. Mary's river, in N. Lat. 30. 52. W. Long. 67. 23.
