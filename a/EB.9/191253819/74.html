AILESURY, AYLESBURY, or ALESBURY

AILESURY, AYLESBURY, or ALESBURY, a borough town in Buckinghamshire, consisting of about 400 houses. The streets lie round the market-place, in the middle of which is a convenient hall, where the sessions are held, and sometimes the assizes for the county. It sends two members to parliament. It is sixty miles south-east of Buckingham, and forty-four north-west of London. W. Long. 0. 40. N. Lat. 51. 40.
