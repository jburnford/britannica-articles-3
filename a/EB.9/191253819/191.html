ALBIOECE

article · 402 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALBIOECE, or ALEBEECE, (Pliny, Strabo); otherwise called Reii Apollinaris, from their superstitious worship of Apollo; also Civitas Reienfium; now Riez, in Provence, about 18 leagues to the north-east of Toulon, on the north side of the rivulet Verdon; was originally a Roman colony, (Inscription). It is sometimes written Regium. The people were called Albici, (Caesar.) E. Long. 1. 0. N. Lat. 43. 20.
