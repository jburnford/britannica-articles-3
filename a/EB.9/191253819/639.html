AMA

article · 283 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMA, in Ecclesiastical Writers, denotes a vessel wherein wine, water, or the like, were held, for the service of the eucharist. In this sense the word is also written amula; sometimes also hama, and hamula.

AMA is sometimes also used for a wine measure, as a cask, pipe, or the like.
