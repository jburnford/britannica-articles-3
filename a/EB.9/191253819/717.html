AMBIGUOUS

article · 286 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AMBIGUOUS, a term applied to a word or expression which may be taken in different senses. An anonymous writer has published a dictionary of ambiguous words: Lexicon Philosophicum de Ambiguitate Vocabulorum, Francof. 1597, 4to.—The responses of the ancient oracles were always ambiguous.
