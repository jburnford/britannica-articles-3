ALIQVANT PART

article · 225 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALIQVANT PART, in Arithmetic, is that number which cannot measure any other exactly without some

remainder. Thus 7 is an aliquant part of 16; for twice 7 wants two of 16, and three times 7 exceeds 16 by 5. Aliquot & Alkermes.
