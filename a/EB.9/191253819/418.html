ALLENDORF

article · 243 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALLENDORF, a small town in the circle of the Upper Rhine, and in the landgraviate of Hesse Cassel, remarkable for its salt works and three stone-bridges. It is seated on the river Wefer, 15 miles east of Cassel. E. Long. 10. 5. N. Lat. 51. 26.
