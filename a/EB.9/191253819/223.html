ALCALA-Real

ALCALA-Real, a small city of Spain, in Andalusia, with a fine abbey. It is built on the top of a high mountain, in a mountainous country; and the road to it is incommodious, rough, and unequal; but to make amends for this, here are several kinds of exquisite fruit and wine. W. Long. 4. 15. N. Lat. 37. 18.
