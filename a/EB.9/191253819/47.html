AHULI

article · 285 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

AHULI, in the sea-language, the situation of a ship when all her sails are furled on account of the violence of the storm, and when having lashed her helm on the lee-side, she lies nearly with her side to the wind and sea, her head being somewhat inclined to the direction of the wind.
