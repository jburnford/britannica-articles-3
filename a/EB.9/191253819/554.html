ALSEN

ALSEN, an island of Denmark, situated in the Lesser Belt, or entrance into the Baltic sea, between Slefwick and Funen, 100 miles west of Copenhagen. It extends in length six leagues, and about two in breadth. The soil is fertile, producing abundance of fruit and variety of grain, with large crops of aniseeds, a carminative much used in seasoning the food and mixing with the bread all over the Danish dominions. E. Long. 10. 12. N. Lat. 55. 12.
