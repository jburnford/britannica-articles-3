ALAMODE

article · 282 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALAMODE, a phrase originally French, importing a thing to be in the fashion or mode. The phrase has been adopted not only into several of the living languages, as the English and High-Dutch, but some have even taken it into the Latin. Hence we meet with Alamodicus and Alamodalitas.
