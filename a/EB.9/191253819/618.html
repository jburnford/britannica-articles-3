ALVA DE TORMES

ALVA DE TORMES, a considerable town in Spain, in the kingdom of Leon, and territory of Salamanca, with a very handsome castle. It is seated on the north bank of the river Tormes. W. Long. 6. 1. N. Lat. 41. 0.
