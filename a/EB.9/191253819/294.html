ALENTEJO

article · 173 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

ALENTEJO, a province of Portugal, between the rivers Tajo and Guadiana: the soil is very fertile, and the inhabitants laborious and industrious. The principal town is Evora.
