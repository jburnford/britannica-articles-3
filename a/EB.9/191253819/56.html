AJAZZO, a sea-port town of Napolis, in the province of Caramania, anciently Cilicia, seated on the coast of the Mediterranean, 30 miles north of Antioch and 50 west of Aleppo, where the city of Issus anciently stood, and near which Alexander fought his second battle with Darius. E. Long. 33. 10. N. Lat. 37. 0.
