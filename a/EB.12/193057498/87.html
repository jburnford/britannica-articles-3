AEROLITE, a term recently, but perhaps improperly, applied to those singular substances commonly called METEORIC STONES. The reader will find an interesting article upon this subject under the word METEOROLITE in the body of the work; and we shall give such additions as may be necessary, under the same head, in this Supplement.
