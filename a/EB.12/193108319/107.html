BUTESHIRE

article · 166 chars · lineage ↗ · page image at NLS ↗

BUTESHIRE, a county on the west coast of Scotland, in the Firth of Clyde, comprehends the Islands of Bute, Arran, the Cumbraes, Inchmarnoch, and a few smaller islets.
