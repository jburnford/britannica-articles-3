INCHMARNOCH

article · 245 chars · page image at NLS ↗

INCHMARNOCH, a beautiful islet, lies to the south-west of Bute, and takes its name from a chapel on it, dedicated to St Marnoch, of which the ruins are still visible. Its surface is less than a square mile, and about a third of it is cultivated.
